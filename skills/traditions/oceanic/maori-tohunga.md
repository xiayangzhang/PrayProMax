# 毛利图杭加（Tohunga）

## 结构
[Whakatūwhera / whakatau i te hau] → [Whakapapa / whakaara i te take] → [Karakia matua / inoi ki te atua-tīpuna e tika ana] → [Whakanoa / whakatau i te mauri] → [Whakakapi / tāiki]

Whakataka te hau ki te uru,

Kia mākinakina ki uta,
kia mātaratara ki tai.
E hī ake ana te atakura,
he tio, he huka, he hau hū.

tūtawa mai i raro,

tūtawa mai i waho.

te mauri ora,
ki runga ki a {{anchors.ingoa}},
ki runga ki {{anchors.whanau_or_kaupapa}},
ki runga ki {{anchors.wahi}}.

Kia hora te marino,
kia whakapapa pounamu te moana,

hui e,
tāiki e!
