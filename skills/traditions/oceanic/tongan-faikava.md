  - Do not use faikava speech to curse, publicly shame, or manipulate named persons.

Ko e Lotu ʻa e ʻEiki:

Ko ʻemau Tamai ʻoku ʻi hēvani,
ke tapuhā ho huafa.
Ke hoko mai ho puleʻanga.
Ke fai ho finangalo ʻi māmani,
ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku fai ʻi hēvani.
Foaki mai kiate kimautolu he ʻaho ni
ʻemau meʻakai fakaʻaho.
Pea fakamolemoleʻi ʻemau ngaahi angahala,
ʻo hangē ko ʻemau fakamolemoleʻi
ʻa kinautolu ʻoku fai angahala mai kiate kimautolu.
Pea ʻoua naʻa tuku kimautolu ke ʻahiʻahiʻi,
ka ke fakahaofi kimautolu mei he kovi.
He ʻoku ʻoʻou ʻa e puleʻanga,
mo e mālohi,
mo e lāngilangi,
ʻo taʻengata.
ʻĒmeni.

Kapau ko e lotu maluʻi pe fakamanatu, ʻoku lau ʻa Saame 23:

Ko Sihova ko hoku Tauhi ia;
ʻe ʻikai te u masiva.
ʻOku ne pule ke u tokoto ʻi he ngaahi mohuku lau maʻuiʻui;
ʻoku ne tataki au ki he veʻe vai mālōlō.
ʻOku ne fakafoki hoku laumālie;
ʻoku ne tataki au ʻi he ngaahi hala ʻo e māʻoniʻoni
koeʻuhi ko hono huafa.
ʻIo, neongo ʻeku ʻalu ʻi he teleʻa ʻo e ʻata ʻo e mate,
ʻe ʻikai te u manavahē ki ha kovi;
he ʻoku ke ʻiate au;
ko hoʻo tokotoko mo hoʻo meʻa tā,
ʻoku na fakafiemālieʻi au.
ʻOku ke teuteu ha keinangaʻanga ʻi hoku ʻao,
ʻi he ʻao ʻo hoku ngaahi fili;
ʻoku ke pani hoku ʻulu ʻaki ʻa e lolo;
ʻoku fonu hoku ipu.
Ko e moʻoni, ko e lelei mo e ʻaloʻofa
ʻe muimui ʻiate au ʻi he ngaahi ʻaho kotoa pē ʻo ʻeku moʻui;
pea te u nofo ʻi he fale ʻo Sihova ʻo taʻengata.

ʻĒmeni.
