In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Holy and triune God, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit,
before you we remember {{anchors.person_name}} and bring before you {{anchors.petition_subject}}.

Κύριε, ἐλέησον.

Κύριε, ἐλέησον.

Have mercy upon us according to your covenant mercy.
We confess {{anchors.sin_or_burden}} and do not hide our need before you.
Cleanse, restore, govern, and keep {{anchors.person_name}} according to your Word.

יְהוָה רֹעִי לֹא אֶחְסָר׃
בִּנְאוֹת דֶּשֶׁא יַרְבִּיצֵנִי עַל־מֵי מְנֻחוֹת יְנַהֲלֵנִי׃
נַפְשִׁי יְשׁוֹבֵב יַנְחֵנִי בְמַעְגְּלֵי־צֶדֶק לְמַעַן שְׁמוֹ׃

O God, in your providence receive this prayer concerning {{anchors.wish_type_detail}}.
If it concerns sickness, sustain the body and soul of {{anchors.person_name}}.

If it concerns danger, preserve {{anchors.person_name}} from evil and from despair.
If it concerns bereavement, comfort {{anchors.bereaved_names}} in the hope of the resurrection.
If it concerns reconciliation, grant repentance, forgiveness, truth, and peace between {{anchors.relationship_parties}}.
If it concerns wisdom, illumine the mind and conscience of {{anchors.person_name}} by your Word.
If it concerns deliverance from sin or fear, break the dominion of evil over {{anchors.person_name}} by the grace of Christ.
If it concerns an event, order {{anchors.event_name}} according to your will and righteousness.

Πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς·
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου·
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου·
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου,
ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς·
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον

καὶ ἡ δύναμις

εἰς τοὺς αἰῶνας.
