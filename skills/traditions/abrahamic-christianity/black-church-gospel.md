## 通用骨架（占位符版本）
```
Father God, in the name of Jesus, we come before You.

Leader:
Lord, we thank You for waking us up, clothing us in our right mind, and keeping us by Your mercy.

People:
Amen. Thank You, Lord.

Leader:
We come not in our own strength, but leaning on Your everlasting arms.
Search us, Lord; forgive us, cleanse us, and make our hearts right before You.

People:
Lord, have mercy.

Leader:
For {{anchors.person}}, for {{anchors.need}}, for {{anchors.place}}, and for all who are standing in need of prayer, we lift our hearts to You.

Scripture:
The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want.
He maketh me to lie down in green pastures:
he leadeth me beside the still waters.
He restoreth my soul:
he leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name's sake.
Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death,
I will fear no evil: for thou art with me;
thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.
Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies:
thou anointest my head with oil; my cup runneth over.
Surely goodness and mercy shall follow me all the days of my life:
and I will dwell in the house of the LORD for ever.

People:
Amen.

Spiritual:
There is a balm in Gilead,
To make the wounded whole;
There is a balm in Gilead,
To heal the sin-sick soul.

Leader:
Lord, where there is sickness, speak mercy.
Where there is fear, speak peace.
Where there is lack, give daily bread.
Where there is danger, cover with Your hand.
Where there is grief, wipe away tears.
Where there is confusion, give wisdom.
Where there is bondage, bring deliverance.
Where there is division, teach charity and forgiveness.

For {{anchors.person}}, name by name and need by need, we ask:
let Your will be done,
let Your mercy be known,
let Your Spirit intercede,
and let Your people stand in love.

Scripture:
He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High
shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.
I will say of the LORD, He is my refuge and my fortress:
my God; in him will I trust.

People:
Yes, Lord.

Leader:
We put on the whole armour of God.
We stand having our loins girt about with truth,
and having on the breastplate of righteousness;
our feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace;
above all, taking the shield of faith,
the helmet of salvation,
and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God.

Spiritual:
Wade in the water,
Wade in the water, children,
Wade in the water,
God's gonna trouble the water.

Leader:
For the living, keep them.
For the weary, strengthen them.
For the bereaved, comfort them.
For those remembered before You, {{anchors.remembrance}}, hold them in Your mercy and hope.

Scripture:
And God shall wipe away all tears from their eyes;
and there shall be no more death, neither sorrow, nor crying,
neither shall there be any more pain:
for the former things are passed away.

People:
Amen.

Leader:
Now, Lord, teach us to pray:

All:
Our Father which art in heaven,
Hallowed be thy name.
Thy kingdom come.
Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.
Give us this day our daily bread.
And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.
And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil:
For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever.
Amen.

Leader:
We seal this prayer in faith, not by might, nor by power, but by Your Spirit.
In Jesus' name.

People:
Amen. Amen. Amen.
```
