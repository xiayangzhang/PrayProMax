```
{{anchors.self_name}} na stand by clean water.
Mami Wata, Mammy Water, Nne mmiri, Yeye Omi,
I call only your name for this prayer.

Mami Wata o, Mami Wata o,
bia, nuru olu m.
Nne mmiri, nne oma,
mmiri di ọcha, mmiri ndu.

Mammy Water, I hail you.
Fine lady for water, mother for river,
she wey sabi deep water,
she wey hold mirror for truth,
she wey cool hot heart.

Mami Wata o,
receive the name of {{anchors.petitioner_name}}.
For {{anchors.wish_subject}},
make water clean, make road open,
make bad mouth cool,
make heart hear truth.

Yeye Omi, iya omi,
bo {{anchors.petitioner_name}} pelu omi funfun.
Omi tutu, omi alaafia,
ko si ija, ko si iro,
alaafia ni mo pe.

Mami Wata o, Mammy Water o,
no vex, no trouble.
If {{anchors.petitioner_name}} don speak wrong,
make wrong word fall for water.
If {{anchors.petitioner_name}} don promise,
make only true promise stand.

Mmiri oma, kuru ihe ojoo laa.
Mmiri ndu, mee ka obi di jụụ.
Nne mmiri, nara olu m,
nara mmiri a,
nara okwu udo a.

Mami Wata o,
{{anchors.petitioner_name}} no call another spirit for this same mouth.
Only Mami Wata name dey here.
Peace for water,
peace for road,
peace for house of {{anchors.petitioner_name}}.

Mammy Water, I thank you.
Nne mmiri, daalu.
Yeye Omi, mo dupe.
Alaafia. Udo. Peace.
```
