# Spiritualists' National Union

## 结构
[静心与建立和谐] → [称呼 Infinite Intelligence / Great Spirit] → [承认 Seven Principles] → [陈明祈祷意向] → [请求爱、真理、疗愈与引导] → [个人责任与不强求结果] → [为生者与亡者祝福] → [结束语]

## 通用骨架（占位符版本）
```
Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, source of love, truth, and life,

In harmony and sincerity, I open this prayer for {{anchors.person_name}} and for the intention of {{anchors.intention}}.

The Fatherhood of God.
The Brotherhood of Man.
The Communion of Spirits and the Ministry of Angels.
The continuous existence of the human soul.
Personal Responsibility.
Compensation and Retribution hereafter for all the good and evil deeds done on earth.
Eternal progress open to every human soul.

May love surround {{anchors.person_name}}.
May truth guide {{anchors.person_name}}.
May healing, comfort, and strength be present according to the highest good.
May all thought, word, and action be held in kindness, honesty, and personal responsibility.

For {{anchors.named_need}}, may there be clarity without fear,
comfort without coercion,
guidance without domination,
and peace without claim of certainty beyond truth.

Where sorrow is present, may consolation be near.
Where confusion is present, may wisdom unfold.
Where conflict is present, may brotherhood and compassion arise.
Where remembrance is present, may the continuous existence of the human soul be honoured in love.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit,
let this prayer serve only love, truth, healing, and eternal progress.

Amen.
```
