# 新思维 · Unity / Religious Science

## 结构
[Recognition / 承认神圣实相] → [Unification / 与神圣合一] → [Realization / 肯定成就之真理] → [Denial and Affirmation / 否认错误信念并肯定真实] → [Thanksgiving / 感恩] → [Release / 释放交托]

## 通用骨架（占位符版本）
```
God, Spirit, Divine Mind, Infinite Life, Infinite Love, Infinite Intelligence,
there is one Presence, one Power, one Life, one Love, one Wisdom, one Substance.

I am one with this Presence.
{{anchors.person_name}} is included in this one Life.
There is no separation between God and the living truth of {{anchors.person_name}}.
The Christ within, the divine idea, the indwelling Spirit, is now recognized.

I deny power to fear, confusion, lack, resentment, bondage, and every false belief concerning {{anchors.topic}}.
They have no authority in Divine Mind, no law in Spirit, no substance in Truth.

I affirm:
God is my help in every need;
God does my every hunger feed;
God walks beside me, guides my way
Through every moment of the day.

I now speak the word for {{anchors.person_name}} and {{anchors.topic}}:
Divine Life is whole.
Divine Love is present.
Divine Wisdom is clear.
Divine Substance is sufficient.
Divine Order is established.
Right action unfolds through {{anchors.event_or_path}}.
Right relationship is expressed with consent, dignity, and peace.
Whatever is not of Truth is released.
Whatever is of Love remains.

For protection (paraphrased — original "Prayer for Protection" by James Dillet Freeman, 1941, copyright Unity; affirm one's own surrounded-by-God-light/love/power/presence framing without reproducing the exact lines):
Divine light is around me.
Divine love is within me.
Divine power upholds me.
Divine presence is with me, in every place.

I give thanks that this word is already known in Divine Mind.
I give thanks for peace, clarity, right action, and the visible expression of good in {{anchors.situation}}.

I release this word into the Law of Mind, into Spirit, into Love.
I let it be.
And so it is.
Amen.
```
