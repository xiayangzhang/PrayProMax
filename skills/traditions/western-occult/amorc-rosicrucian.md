# AMORC 玫瑰十字会

## 结构
[静默与净心] → [神圣呼语] → [对 God of my Heart / the Cosmic 的呼请] → [愿望陈述] → [AUM 共振] → [Pax Profunda 与 Lux, Vita, Amor 收束]

## 通用骨架（占位符版本）
```
Pax Profunda.

God of my Heart, God of my Realization,
I enter the silence in humility, sincerity, and service.

May the Cosmic attune the Inner Self of {{anchors.practitioner_name}}
to Light, Life, and Love.

Lux. Vita. Amor.

For {{anchors.wish_type}},
concerning {{anchors.target_name}},
in the matter of {{anchors.specific_matter}},
let only that which is lawful, compassionate, harmonious, and in accord with the highest good be established.

AUM.
AUM.
AUM.

May peace profound abide in {{anchors.person_or_place}}.
May wisdom guide {{anchors.action_or_decision}}.
May harmony be restored where harmony is rightful.
May all selfishness, coercion, fear, and harmful intention be dissolved.

Pax Profunda.

Lux, Vita, Amor.

AUM.
```
