## 通用骨架（占位符版本）
```
Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law.

For {{anchors.name}}, under the Law of Thelema, in the matter of {{anchors.petition}}, let this word be set in harmony with True Will.

ΑΠΟ ΠΑΝΤΟΣ ΚΑΚΟΔΑΙΜΟΝΟΣ.

ΣΟΙ, Ὦ ΦΑΛΛΕ, ἸΣΧΥΡΟΣ, ΕΥΧΑΡΙΣΤΟΣ, ΙΑΩ.

ΘΗΡΙΟΝ.

ΝΥΙΤ.

ΒΑΒΑΛΟΝ.

ΑΔΙΤ.

ΙΩ ΠΑΝ.

ΠΡΟ ΜΟΥ ΙΥΓΓΕΣ,
ΟΠΙΣΩ ΜΟΥ ΤΕΛΕΤΑΡΧΑΙ,
ΕΠΙ ΔΕΞΙΑ ΣΥΝΟΧΕΣ,
ΕΠΑΡΙΣΤΕΡΑ ΔΑΙΜΟΝΕΣ,
ΦΛΕΓΕΙ ΓΑΡ ΠΕΡΙ ΜΟΥ Ὁ ΑΣΤΗΡ ΤΩΝ ΠΕΝΤΕ,
ΚΑΙ ΕΝ ΤΗΙ ΣΤΗΛΗΙ Ὁ ΑΣΤΗΡ ΤΩΝ ἙΞ ἙΣΤΗΚΕ.

Hail unto Thee who art Ra in Thy rising, even unto Thee who art Ra in Thy strength, who travellest over the Heavens in Thy bark at the Uprising of the Sun. Tahuti standeth in His splendour at the prow, and Ra-Hoor abideth at the helm. Hail unto Thee from the Abodes of Night!

Unity uttermost showed!
I adore the might of Thy breath,
Supreme and terrible God,
Who makest the gods and death
To tremble before Thee:—
I, I adore thee!

Appear on the throne of Ra!
Open the ways of the Khu!
Lighten the ways of the Ka!
The ways of the Khabs run through
To stir me or still me!
Aum! let it fill me!

Let {{anchors.petition}} be ordered according to the True Will of {{anchors.name}}, without restriction of another star.

Love is the law, love under will.
```
