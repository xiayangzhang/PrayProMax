## 通用骨架（占位符版本）
```
Ground of being, Earth below and within,
I center in breath, body, and presence.

By my will, by my breath, by my truth,
this circle is cast around {{anchors.place}},
a boundary of clarity, consent, and love.

East, powers of Air:
breath, song, thought, and dawn,
be present in this circle.

South, powers of Fire:
will, courage, heat, and transformation,
be present in this circle.

West, powers of Water:
feeling, dream, grief, and healing,
be present in this circle.

North, powers of Earth:
body, root, stone, and bread,
be present in this circle.

Goddess, immanent one,
She who is within and around all beings,
be present in this circle.

Air I am,
Fire I am,
Water, Earth, and Spirit I am.

For {{anchors.name}},
for {{anchors.intent}},
for {{anchors.people}},
for the web of life that holds {{anchors.place}},
I raise this prayer.

She changes everything She touches,
and everything She touches changes.

Let coercion fall away.
Let fear be named and released.
Let power-over be undone.
Let power-with arise.
Let the work of {{anchors.intent}} be aligned with consent,
justice, healing, and the freedom of all beings concerned.

Earth my body,
Water my blood,
Air my breath,
and Fire my spirit.

If this prayer is for the dead:
We all come from the Goddess,
and to Her we shall return,
like a drop of rain,
flowing to the ocean.
May {{anchors.deceased_name}} be held in love,
and may the living remember with truth.

If this prayer is for breaking a harmful pattern:
By the turning of the spiral,
by the change that changes all,
the bond named {{anchors.pattern}} is released.
No will is chained.
No soul is diminished.
The energy returns to ground,
cleansed of domination and fear.

If this prayer is for protection:
This circle is whole.
This boundary is clear.
What serves love and justice may enter.
What feeds harm, coercion, or deceit is turned away.

If this prayer is for wisdom:
Let love, law, knowledge, liberty, and wisdom
stand as five bright points within the heart of {{anchors.name}}.
Let clear seeing arise.

If this prayer is for relationship:
Let truth be spoken.
Let consent be honored.
Let {{anchors.relationship_names}} meet, part, or transform
in freedom and right relation.

If this prayer is for an event:
Let the gathered will around {{anchors.event_name}}
rise as a cone of power,
not to dominate,
but to strengthen courage, clarity, protection, and shared good.

She changes everything She touches,
and everything She touches changes.

The power is raised.
The power is released.
The power is grounded in Earth,
carried by Water,
quickened by Air,
tempered by Fire,
and returned to Spirit.

Goddess, immanent one,
for presence and witness, blessed be.

North, powers of Earth,
for body and ground, blessed be.

West, powers of Water,
for feeling and healing, blessed be.

South, powers of Fire,
for will and transformation, blessed be.

East, powers of Air,
for breath and song, blessed be.

The circle is open, but unbroken.
May the peace of the Goddess be ever in your heart.
Merry meet, and merry part, and merry meet again
