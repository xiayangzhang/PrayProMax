I, {{anchors.practitioner_name}}, stand within the circle between the worlds,
in perfect love and perfect trust,
for {{anchors.wish_type}} concerning {{anchors.petition_subject}}.

By earth and sea and sky,
let this circle be cast and sealed.
What enters here shall enter in truth;
what leaves here shall leave in peace.

Guardians of the Watchtowers of the East, powers of Air,
I do summon, stir, and call you up,
to witness this rite for {{anchors.petition_subject}}.

Guardians of the Watchtowers of the South, powers of Fire,
I do summon, stir, and call you up,
to witness this rite for {{anchors.petition_subject}}.

Guardians of the Watchtowers of the West, powers of Water,
I do summon, stir, and call you up,
to witness this rite for {{anchors.petition_subject}}.

Guardians of the Watchtowers of the North, powers of Earth,
I do summon, stir, and call you up,
to witness this rite for {{anchors.petition_subject}}.

Great Mother,
Listen to the words of the Great Mother,
who of old was called Artemis, Astarte, Dione, Melusine,
Aphrodite, Cerridwen, Diana, Arionrhod, Brigid, and by many other names.
All acts of love and pleasure are my rituals.
Be present to this prayer for {{anchors.petition_subject}}.

Horned One,
Lord of the wildwood, keeper of life, death, and return,
be present to this prayer for {{anchors.petition_subject}}.

An it harm none, do what ye will.

Therefore I speak only within willing, rightful, and harmless bounds:
{{anchors.petition_text}}.
Let no will be bound.
Let no heart be compelled.
Let no life be diminished.
Let what is not mine return to its own place.
Let what is mine stand clear in truth.

Eko Eko Azarak,
Eko Eko Zomelak,
Eko Eko Azarak,
Eko Eko Zomelak.

For {{anchors.petition_subject}},
may the crooked be made clear,
may the harmful be made harmless,
may the hidden be known in right measure,
may the needed path open without coercion.

If any word of this rite has exceeded rightful boundary,
I withdraw it now.
If any force has been called beyond consent,
I release it now.
If any bond has been made against true will,
I unmake it now.
Peace to all beings touched by this prayer.

Great Mother, I thank you and release you.
Go if you must, stay if you will.
Hail and farewell.

Horned One, I thank you and release you.
Go if you must, stay if you will.
Hail and farewell.

Guardians of the Watchtowers of the North, powers of Earth,
I thank you and release you.
Hail and farewell.

Guardians of the Watchtowers of the West, powers of Water,
I thank you and release you.
Hail and farewell.

Guardians of the Watchtowers of the South, powers of Fire,
I thank you and release you.
Hail and farewell.

Guardians of the Watchtowers of the East, powers of Air,
I thank you and release you.
Hail and farewell.

The circle is open, but unbroken.
Merry meet, merry part, and merry meet again.
So mote it be.
