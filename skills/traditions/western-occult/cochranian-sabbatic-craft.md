## 结构
[立界 / Hallowing] → [设罗盘 / Compass] → [单一名号呼请] → [发愿 / Petition] → [正行咒句 / Charm] → [封缄 / Sealing] → [致谢与遣散 / Dismissal]

## 通用骨架（占位符版本）
```
In the name of the Old One,
Master of the Crooked Path,
Keeper of the hidden fire,
I hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,
let this ring be whole,
let no false word enter,
let no oath be broken,
let no secret be profaned.

Old One, hear the word of {{anchors.practitioner_name}}.
The work is for {{anchors.wish_type}}:
{{anchors.petition_plain}}.

No stolen oath is spoken here.
No hidden word is claimed here.
Only the open charm is set,
only the rightful prayer is made.

For health:
Bone to bone, blood to blood,
breath to breath, and flesh to flesh;
that which is torn be mended,
that which is fevered be cooled,
that which is weakened be strengthened,
for {{anchors.target_name}}.

For wealth:
Hand to hand, hearth to hearth,
seed to field, and coin to purse;
let honest increase gather,
let waste be stopped,
let need be answered,
for {{anchors.target_name}}.

For protection:
Thorn before, thorn behind,
thorn at the left, thorn at the right;
by blackthorn, whitethorn, and the crooked bough,
let the ward stand fast
around {{anchors.target_name}}.

For relationship:
Truth to tongue, peace to hand,
clear sight to heart, and bounds to bond;
let goodwill stand where it is rightful,
let false binding fall away,
between {{anchors.target_name}} and {{anchors.other_name}}.

For wisdom:
Fire in the head, light in the bone,
the crooked road beneath the feet;
let cunning be awakened,
let folly be uncovered,
let the true sign be known
to {{anchors.target_name}}.

For breaking:
Knot unknotted, cord uncorded,
mark unmarked, and hook unhooked;
what is not rightful is released,
what is not sworn is severed,
what is not owned is returned,
from {{anchors.target_name}}.

For event:
Road before, road behind,
gate before, gate behind;
let the way be opened,
let the appointed work be met,
let {{anchors.event_name}} pass in right order
for {{anchors.target_name}}.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,
the word is spoken,
the work is sealed,
the compass is whole.

Old One, receive thanks.
Let the road be shut where it must be shut.
Let the road be open where it must be open.
Peace to the ring,
peace to the dead,
peace to the living.

So mote it be.
```
