## 威卡 Gardnerian

In this place, in this hour, we stand between the worlds,  
with clean hands, clear speech, and a will held in Perfect Love and Perfect Trust.

I conjure thee, O Circle of Power,  
that thou beest a boundary between the world of men and the realms of the Mighty Ones,  
a guardian and a meeting-place for this passing-blessing for the beloved dead and the ancestors.

Lady of the Moon,  
Great Mother, Bright One, Queen of the secret place,  
hear the words spoken within this circle.

Whenever ye have need of anything,  
once in the month, and better it be when the moon is full,  
then shall ye assemble in some desert place,  
or in a forest all together join  
to adore the potent spirit of your queen.

Horned One,  
Lord of the wildwood, keeper of life and death,  
stand apart in thy own power and witness this working.

{{petition}}

Before the Lord we speak the boundary:  
Let no will be forced, no spirit be bound, no living heart be harmed.  
Let this prayer follow only the true path of release, mercy, and right remembrance.

Let this prayer be for peaceful passage, healing remembrance, and joyful rest,  
harming none, binding none against the true will,  
and turning aside all falsehood, fear, and ill-wrought force.

Eko, Eko, Azarak,  
Eko, Eko, Zomelak.

By earth, may the dead be gently received.  
By air, may their names be remembered in clarity.  
By fire, may all fear be transformed without harm.  
By water, may grief flow toward peace.

Lady of the Moon, receive this prayer.  
Horned One, witness this prayer.  
Mighty Ones of the circle, keep it within right measure.

As the cup and the bread are blessed in fellowship,  
so may the work be whole:  
for the ancestors and beloved dead,  
for peace after death,  
according to love, freedom, and the turning of the sacred wheel.

Let what is spoken here remain clean, open, and rightly measured.  
Let all sworn mysteries rest veiled and unharmed.  
Let no coercive force, no fear, no pride, and no ill intent remain in this circle.

By clear breath, be we purified.  
By quiet heart, be we guarded.  
By Perfect Love and Perfect Trust, be this blessing released.

The circle is open, yet unbroken.  
Merry meet, and merry part, and merry meet again.
