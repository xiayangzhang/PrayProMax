## 现代术士派（Cochranian、Sabbatic Craft）

In the name of the Old One,  
Master of the Crooked Path,  
Keeper of the hidden fire,  
I hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
let this ring be whole,  
let no false word enter,  
let no oath be broken,  
let no secret be profaned.

{{petition}}

No stolen oath is spoken here.  
No hidden word is claimed here.  
Only the open charm is set,  
only the rightful prayer is made.

Ash to ash, bone to earth,  
breath to wind, and spark to star;  
let the road behind be gentle,  
let the road before be clear,  
let grief be softened without binding the dead,  
let memory remain without calling them back.

Thorn guard the threshold,  
flame warm the way,  
stone hold the silence,  
stang mark the path;  
may the departed be at rest,  
may bitterness fall away,  
may suffering be ended,  
may peace be their portion.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
the word is spoken,  
the work is sealed,  
the compass is whole.

Old One, receive thanks.  
What has been asked is released into rightful keeping.  
No spirit is constrained,  
no dead are summoned,  
no secret is profaned.

Let the road be shut where it must be shut.  
Let the road be open where it must be open.  
Let every unrightful bond fall from this ring.  
Let the living return to their own bounds.  
Let the dead go in peace.

Peace to the ring,  
peace to the dead,  
peace to the living.

So mote it be.
