## 共济会

Most holy and glorious Lord God,  
the Great Architect of the Universe,  
the Giver of all good gifts and graces:

Thou hast promised that where two or three are gathered together in Thy Name,  
Thou wilt be in the midst of them and bless them.

{{petition}}

Grant that every thought, word, and act  
may be ordered by Wisdom,  
supported by Strength,  
and adorned with Beauty.

הִנֵּה מַה־טּוֹב וּמַה־נָּעִים  
שֶׁבֶת אַחִים גַּם־יָחַד׃

Let Brotherly Love, Relief, and Truth  
govern our hearts toward all brethren, kindred, and humankind.  
Let no anger, vanity, unlawful gain, false witness,  
or betrayal of trust enter this remembrance.

Almighty Father,  
into Thy hands we commend the souls of the departed.  
Receive them with mercy;  
let their labor cease in peace;  
let light perpetual shine upon them;  
and keep the living in sobriety, remembrance, charity, and concord.

Teach us to number our days,  
to square our actions by justice,  
to raise our hearts by truth,  
and to build within ourselves a temple worthy of Thy presence.

Keep us faithful to every lawful obligation,  
silent where silence is due,  
truthful where speech is required,  
and charitable toward all mankind.

May the Supreme Grand Master  
preside over this intention,  
until all work is closed in peace.

Guard our lips from vain display,  
our hands from wrongful work,  
our minds from pride,  
and our fellowship from discord.  
Let this remembrance remain within the bounds of reverence, morality, and peace.

So mote it be.
