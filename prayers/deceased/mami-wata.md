## Mami Wata 信仰

We stand by clean water with cool heart.  
Mami Wata, Mammy Water, Nne mmiri, Yeye Omi,  
we call only your name for this prayer.

Mami Wata o,  
Mother for river, lady for deep water,  
mirror wey show true face,  
cool water wey calm hot sorrow,  
we hail you with respect.

{{petition}}

No harm we ask for any living person,  
no trouble we send to any house,  
no curse, no fight, no bad mouth.  
Only peace for the dead,  
only comfort for the family,  
only clean road for memory.

Mami Wata o,  
let water wash sorrow,  
let water carry heavy cry far away,  
let heart remember with truth,  
let the ancestors find quiet,  
let them leave suffering and enter joy.

Mammy Water o, no vex, no trouble.  
If any word here no be straight,  
make wrong word fall inside water and scatter.  
If any fear call what it no suppose to call,  
make only clean respect remain.

We no call another spirit with this same mouth.  
We no bring blood, no forced oath, no secret work.  
We bring clean water, peace word, and humble heart.

Mami Wata, receive this clean water in peace.  
Cool the road, cool the house, cool the dream.  
Peace for water, peace for road, peace for the ancestors.  
Alaafia. Udo. Peace.
