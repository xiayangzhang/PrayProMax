## Shembe

Nkulunkulu kaShembe, Baba wethu,  
siyakhuleka phambi kwakho,  
sikhumbula abokhokho bethu nabafileyo bethu,  
sibabeka emseni wakho, Nkosi.

Siyakudumisa, Jehova;  
siyakubonga, Nkosi;  
igama lakho malibongwe.  
UJehova ungumalusi wethu;  
ngomusa wakho akukho oswele ukuthula kwakho.

Simi phambi kwakho ngokuzithoba.  
Uma kukhona isono, ukungcola, ukungathembeki, noma ukona okwaziwayo nokungaziwayo,  
siyacela intethelelo, ukuthula, nokubuyiselwa endleleni yakho.  
Sihlanze izinhliziyo zethu,  
usifundise ukukhumbula abafileyo ngokuthula,  
kungabi ngokwesaba, kodwa ngokwethemba kuwe.

Baba wethu osezulwini,  
malingcweliswe igama lakho;  
mawufike umbuso wakho;  
mayenziwe intando yakho emhlabeni njengasezulwini.  
Usiphe namuhla isinkwa sethu semihla ngemihla;  
usithethelele amacala ethu,  
njengokuba nathi sibathethelela abanamacala kithi;  
ungasingenisi ekulingweni,  
kodwa usikhulule kokubi.  
Ngokuba umbuso ungowakho, namandla, nenkazimulo, kuze kube phakade.  
Amen.

Nkosi, gcina amakhaya asele emhlabeni.  
Duduza abakhalayo, qinisa abantwana, abadala, nomndeni wonke.  
Makungabi khona inzondo, ukwesaba, noma ukuhlukana phakathi kwethu.  
Sifundise ukuhamba ngobuqotho, ngobumsulwa, nangokuthula.

Uma umfundisi ogunyaziwe ekhona:  
Ngibeka lesi sibusiso emkhulekweni webandla,  
ngithi: Nkosi, yamukela ukukhumbula kwethu,  
ubophe amanxeba ezinhliziyo,  
unike abafileyo ukuphumula,  
unike abaphilayo amandla okuqhubeka endleleni yakho.

Siyakubonga, Nkosi.  
Siyakudumisa, Jehova.  
Ukuthula makube kwabafileyo bethu,  
ukuthula makube emakhaya ethu,  
ukuthula makube kulo lonke ibandla.  
Makwenziwe intando yakho, hhayi eyethu.  
Amen.
