## 新思维 · Unity / Religious Science

**Recognition / 承认神圣实相**

God, Spirit, Divine Mind, Infinite Life, Infinite Love, Infinite Intelligence,  
there is one Presence, one Power, one Life, one Love, one Wisdom, one Peace.

Life is eternal.  
Love is boundless.  
Peace is the truth of Being.  
No one is outside the wholeness of Spirit.

**Unification / 与神圣合一**

I am one with this Presence.  
The departed ancestors are included in this one Life.  
There is no separation between God and the living truth of those who have passed from this visible world.  
The divine idea, the indwelling Spirit, the eternal light of Life, is now recognized in them and in all.

**Denial and Affirmation / 否认错误信念并肯定真实**

I deny power to fear, grief, regret, confusion, attachment, and every false belief concerning death and separation.  
They have no authority in Divine Mind, no law in Spirit, no substance in Truth.

I affirm:  
Life is not ended.  
Love is not broken.  
Peace is not delayed.  
The departed ancestors are held in Infinite Love, guided by Infinite Intelligence, and established in Infinite Peace.

Whatever is not of Truth is released.  
Whatever is of Love remains.

**Thanksgiving / 感恩**

I give thanks that this word is already known in Divine Mind.  
I give thanks for peace for the departed, comfort for the living, and the quiet assurance of eternal Life.  
I give thanks that Love fulfills all things in right order.

**Release / 释放交托**

I release this word into the Law of Mind, into Spirit, into Love.  
I let go of fear and rest in divine assurance.  
It is done in peace, in love, and in the highest good of all.

And so it is.  
Amen.
