Shubḥā l-Abā w-la-Brōnā w-la-Rūḥā d-Qudshā,  
min ‘ālam w-‘damā l-‘ālam. Āmēn.

Abun d-ba-shmayyā,  
nethqaddash shmakh;  
tite malkuthakh;  
nehwe sebyanakh,  
aykanna d-ba-shmayyā ap b-ar‘a.  
Haw lan laḥma d-sunqanan yawmana;  
washbuq lan ḥawbayn wa-khṭahayn,  
aykana d-af ḥnan shbaqn l-ḥayyabayn;  
wela ta‘lan l-nesyuna,  
ella paṣṣan min bisha.  
Meṭul dilakh hi malkutha w-ḥayla w-teshbuḥta  
l-‘ālam ‘ālmin. Āmēn.

Marya Alāhā,  
ḥus ‘al ‘abdayk d-shkhibu b-haymanutha,  
w-qabbel ba‘uta d-raḥme w-shayna  
b-ṣebyānā dīlakh,  
lā b-ṣebyānā dīlan.

Lakhu Mara d-kol modēnan,

w-‘bed raḥme ‘al aylen d-bakeyn w-met’ablin,  
w-anīḥ b-shayna l-kulhon ‘anide d-haymanutha,  
ayk ḥnānakh sagī’ā.

min ‘ālam w-‘damā l-‘ālam. Āmēn.

Marya,  
kol ba‘utan tēhwe b-malkuthakh,  
b-raḥmayk w-ba-ṣebyānakh qaddīshā;  
lā b-ḥarasha, lā b-’urḥa d-bisha,  
ella b-teshbuḥta, b-makkiḵutha,  
w-b-shayna d-Yeshu Mshiḥa Moran.
