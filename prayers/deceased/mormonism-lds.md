## 摩门教（LDS）

Our Father in Heaven,

We thank Thee for the gift of life, for the hope of the Resurrection, and for the redeeming love of Thy Son, Jesus Christ.

We acknowledge Jesus Christ as our Savior and Redeemer, and we seek the comfort and guidance of the Holy Ghost.

{{petition}}

In the name of Jesus Christ, and by the authority of the Melchizedek Priesthood, we dedicate this resting place as a peaceful place of remembrance, asking that it be kept from disturbance and that Thy Spirit may bring consolation to the living according to faith in Jesus Christ.

May Thy will be done in all things. We do not seek to command Thy purposes, but humbly entrust the departed to Thy justice, mercy, and eternal plan through Jesus Christ.

We offer this prayer in reverence, gratitude, and trust, relying only upon Thy revealed gospel, Thy holy will, and the grace of Thy Beloved Son.

We say these things in the name of Jesus Christ, amen.
