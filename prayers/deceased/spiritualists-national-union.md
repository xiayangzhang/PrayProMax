## Spiritualists' National Union

In stillness, harmony, and sincere remembrance, we open our hearts to love, truth, and peace.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, source of love, truth, and life,

{{petition}}

The Fatherhood of God.  
The Brotherhood of Man.  
The Communion of Spirits and the Ministry of Angels.  
The continuous existence of the human soul.  
Personal Responsibility.  
Compensation and Retribution hereafter for all the good and evil deeds done on earth.  
Eternal progress open to every human soul.

May love surround those who have gone before.  
May truth guide them onward.  
May healing, comfort, and strength be present according to the highest good.  
May sorrow be eased, fear be released, and every soul be upheld in compassion, freedom, and eternal progress.

For the departed, may there be peace without coercion,  
comfort without claim of certainty beyond truth,  
guidance without domination,  
and remembrance held in kindness, honesty, and personal responsibility.

Where suffering has been, may healing influence draw near.  
Where burden has been, may release and light unfold.  
Where longing remains among the living, may consolation be present.  
Where love continues, may the continuous existence of the human soul be honoured with reverence and peace.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit,  
may this prayer serve only love, truth, healing, comfort, and spiritual growth.  
May no word spoken here bind, command, or disturb any soul.  
May all be held in goodwill, free will, and the highest good of life seen and unseen.

Bless the living with courage and serenity.  
Bless the departed with peace, progress, and loving remembrance.  
May all souls move ever nearer to light, harmony, and joy.

Amen.
