## الكنيسة المارونية

B-shmo d-Abo w-d-Bro w-d-Ruho d-Qudsho,  
Had Aloho shariro. Amin.

Qadishat Aloho,  
Qadishat Hayelthono,  
Qadishat lo Moyutho,  
ethraham ‘alayn.

Shubho l-Abo w-la-Bro w-la-Ruho d-Qudsho,  
men ‘olam w-‘adamo l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Abun d-bashmayo,  
netqadash shmokh.  
Tite malkuthokh.  
Nehwe sebyonokh,  
aykano d-bashmayo oph bar‘o.  
Haw lan lahmo d-sunqonan yawmono.  
Washboq lan hawbayn wahtohayn,  
aykano doph hnan shbaqn l-hayyobayn.  
Wlo ta‘lan l-nesyuno,  
elo fasso lan men bisho.  
Metul d-dilokh hi malkutho w-haylo w-teshbuhto  
l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Shlom lekh Maryam,  
mlayat taybutho,  
Moran ‘amekh.  
Mbarakhto at b-neshe,  
w-mbarakh hu piro d-karsekh,  
Maran Yeshua.  
O qadishto Maryam,  
Yoldath Aloho,  
saloy hlofayn hnaye,  
hosho w-bsho‘at mawthan. Amin.

Moran Yeshua Mshiha,  
Bro d-Aloho hayo,  
ra‘yo tobo d-nafshe,  
phtah tar‘e d-rahmayk  
l-‘anide mhaymne,  
w-ahlanun b-shayno d-lo ‘aber,  
b-nuho d-sadiqe,  
w-b-nuhro d-paradisokh.

B-shfa‘otho d-Yoldath Aloho Maryam,  
w-d-Mor Maron,  
w-d-Mor Efram,  
w-d-Mor Ya‘qub,  
w-d-Mor Sharbel,  
w-d-kulhon qadisho,  
Moryo etraham ‘alayn.

Moryo,  
haw lehon nuho ‘am Avrohom w-Is’hoq w-Ya‘qub,  
‘am shlihe w-sahde,

w-‘am kulhon aylin d-shpar qdomayk

Nhoor lan b-nuhrokh,  
nattar lan b-slibokh,

w-ahlan b-shaynokh,  
d-nedkar l-‘anide b-hubbo,  
w-n‘abed tobtho b-sabro,  
w-nshabah lokh b-lebo dkhe.

Lo b-zedqathan metkarvinan, Moryo,  
elo b-rahmayk rabe.  
Lo b-haylan shaylinan,  
elo b-sebyonokh qadisho.  
L-iddayk mashlminan l-haye w-l-‘anide,  
w-menakh shaylinan nuho, shayno, w-hnono.

Lakh shubho,  
l-Abokh iqoro,  
w-l-Ruho d-Qudsho segedto,  
hosho w-bkul zban  
w-l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.
