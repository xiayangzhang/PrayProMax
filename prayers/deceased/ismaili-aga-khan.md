## 伊斯玛仪派 / 阿迦汗系

**Niyyah / النية:**  
Allāhumma innī ajʿalu hādhā al-duʿāʾ li-wajhika al-karīm, wa aṭlubu raḥmataka wa hidāyataka, wa ahdī thawābahu ilā arwāḥi l-marḥūmīn wa l-marḥūmāt, rajāʾa s-sakīnah wa r-raḥmah wa l-faraj.

**Basmalah:**  
Bismillāhi r-Raḥmāni r-Raḥīm.  
بِسْمِ ٱللّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

**Hamd wa al-Fātiḥah:**  
Al-ḥamdu lillāhi rabbi l-ʿālamīn.  
Ar-raḥmāni r-raḥīm.  
Māliki yawmi d-dīn.  
Iyyāka naʿbudu wa iyyāka nastaʿīn.  
Ihdinā ṣ-ṣirāṭa l-mustaqīm.  
Ṣirāṭa lladhīna anʿamta ʿalayhim, ghayri l-maghḍūbi ʿalayhim wa lā ḍ-ḍāllīn.  
Āmīn.

**Shahādah wa walāyah:**  
Ashhadu an lā ilāha illā Allāh.  
Ashhadu anna Muḥammadan Rasūlu llāh.  
Ashhadu anna Mawlānā ʿAlīyyan Amīru l-Muʾminīn ʿAlīyyu llāh.

**Ṣalawāt:**  
Allāhumma ṣalli ʿalā Muḥammadin wa āli Muḥammad.  
Allāhumma bārik ʿalā Muḥammadin wa āli Muḥammad.

**Duʿāʾ bi-dhikri d-Duʿāʾ al-Muqaddas:**  
{{petition}}

Allāhumma lā najʿalu hādhā duʿāʾan li-ḍarar aḥad,  
wa lā li-ẓulm aḥad,  
bal dhikran wa raḥmatan wa istighfāran wa taslīman li-amrika.

**Āyat an-Nūr:**  
Allāhu nūru s-samāwāti wa l-arḍ.  
Mathalu nūrihī ka-mishkātin fīhā miṣbāḥ.  
Al-miṣbāḥu fī zujājah.  
Az-zujājatu ka-annahā kawkabun durrīyyun  
yūqadu min shajaratin mubārakatin zaytūnatin  
lā sharqīyyatin wa lā gharbīyyah,  
yakādu zaytuhā yuḍīʾu wa law lam tamsashu nār.  
Nūrun ʿalā nūr.  
Yahdī llāhu li-nūrihī man yashāʾ.  
Wa yaḍribu llāhu l-amthāla li-n-nās.  
Wa llāhu bi-kulli shayʾin ʿalīm.

**Tasbīḥ:**  
Yā Allāh.  
Yā Muḥammad.  
Yā ʿAlī.  
Yā Ḥāḍir Imām, madad bi-idhnillāh.

**Ginan remembrance:**  
Eji satgur miliyā munē āj, ānand ānand thayō.  
Eji sāhebjī tū̃ morē man bhāvē.

**Duʿāʾ li-l-arwāḥ wa-l-jamāʿah:**  
Allāhumma ghfir li-l-muʾminīna wa l-muʾmināt,  
wa rḥam arwāḥa l-marḥūmīn wa l-marḥūmāt,  
wa-jʿal nūra l-hidāyah fī qulūbi l-aḥyāʾ,  
wa s-sakīnata fī buyūti l-maḥzūnīn,  
wa r-raḥmata fī jamāʿatinā kullihā.

**Khitām wa taqwiyah:**  
Allāhumma ijʿal hādhā dhikran khāliṣan li-wajhika,  
wa ṣalātan ʿalā Nabiyyika wa ālihi,  
wa istighfāran li-man sabaqanā,  
wa ʿamalan ṣāliḥan lā riyāʾa fīhi wa lā ḍarar.  
Allāhumma taqabbal duʿāʾanā bi-raḥmatika.  
Āmīn.  
Ya ʿAlī madad.
