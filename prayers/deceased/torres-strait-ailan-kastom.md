## 托雷斯海峡 Ailan Kastom

Mipla stanap long ples blo solwata, win ane ailan.  
Mipla tingbaut pamle, home, reef, tide ane rod blo ol bipo pipol.

Mipla tingbaut bipo pipol.  
Mipla tingbaut ol bigwan uda bin kipem kastom, gud tok, ane respek.  
Mipla askem pis for ol pipol uda paswei bipo mipla.

Mipla no tekem song blo nada pamle.  
Mipla no kolem nem blo man, ailan, reef, zogo o stori wea mipla no gad lo.  
Mipla no mekem secret song, no mekem clan dance, no mekem zogo tok.

Bipo pipol, mipla tingbaut yu.  
Bigwan, mipla lisen long gud tok.

Mipla kipem kastom long stret rod, no mekem harm, no tekem samting blo nada man.

Sapos mipla tok rong o go pas lo mipla no gad, mipla stap kwait.  
Let bigwan, elder ane songholder stretim mipla long gudwei.  
Mipla put daun praud heart; mipla kisim respek, pis ane wanwan.
