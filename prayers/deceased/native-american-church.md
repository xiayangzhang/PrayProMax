## 美洲原住民教会（NAC）

With humble hearts, we sit in respect before Creator, Great Spirit, Father God.  
Before the fire of prayer and the witness of family, we remember our departed ancestors.  
{{petition}}  
May the living family be comforted, forgiven, and kept on a clean road.  
Where the authorized Roadman and community carry their own songs, may those prayers rise in a good way.  
Creator, keep us from taking what is not ours, from crossing another family’s way, and from misusing holy things.  
We give thanks for life, for those who came before, for those yet to come, and we close in peace. Amen.
