## स्मार्त

**śauca / ācamanam**

oṃ apavitraḥ pavitro vā sarvāvasthāṃ gato 'pi vā |  
yaḥ smaret puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ sa bāhyābhyantaraḥ śuciḥ ||

oṃ keśavāya svāhā |  
oṃ nārāyaṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ mādhavāya svāhā |

**prāṇāyāma**

oṃ bhūḥ | oṃ bhuvaḥ | oṃ suvaḥ | oṃ mahaḥ | oṃ janaḥ | oṃ tapaḥ | oṃ satyam |  
oṃ tat savitur vareṇyaṃ bhargo devasya dhīmahi dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt |  
oṃ āpo jyotī raso 'mṛtaṃ brahma bhūr bhuvaḥ suvar oṃ ||

**saṅkalpa**

mama upātta-samasta-duritakṣayadvārā śrī-parameśvara-prītyarthaṃ  
samasta-pitṛjanānāṃ sadgati-śānti-duḥkha-nivṛtti-śuddha-pada-prāptyarthaṃ  
śrīmannārāyaṇa-pradhāna-pitṛ-smaraṇa-pūjāṃ kariṣye ||

**Gaṇapati-smaraṇa**

oṃ gaṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ |  
vakratuṇḍa mahākāya sūryakoṭisamaprabha |  
nirvighnaṃ kuru me deva sarvakāryeṣu sarvadā ||

**kalaśa / śuddhi**

oṃ kalaśasya mukhe viṣṇuḥ kaṇṭhe rudraḥ samāśritaḥ |  
mūle tatra sthito brahmā madhye mātṛgaṇāḥ smṛtāḥ |  
kukṣau tu sāgarāḥ sarve sapta-dvīpā vasundharā |  
ṛgvedo 'tha yajurvedaḥ sāmavedo hy atharvaṇaḥ |  
aṅgaiś ca sahitāḥ sarve kalaśāmbu samāśritāḥ ||

**dhyāna / āvāhana**

śāntākāraṃ bhujagaśayanaṃ padmanābhaṃ sureśaṃ  
viśvādhāraṃ gaganasadṛśaṃ meghavarṇaṃ śubhāṅgam |  
lakṣmīkāntaṃ kamalanayanaṃ yogibhirdhyānagamyaṃ  
vande viṣṇuṃ bhavabhayaharaṃ sarvalokaikanātham ||

oṃ śrīmannārāyaṇa āvāhayāmi |

oṃ śrīmannārāyaṇāya namaḥ gandhaṃ samarpayāmi |

**pitṛ-smaraṇa / japa**

oṃ namo nārāyaṇāya |  
oṃ namo nārāyaṇāya |  
oṃ namo nārāyaṇāya ||

oṃ pitṛbhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ |

**namaskāra**

oṃ śrīmannārāyaṇāya namaḥ |  
namaste bhagavan viṣṇo nārāyaṇa jagatpate |  
anena pūjā-japa-smaraṇa-pāṭhena pitṛ-śānti-sadgati-siddhir astu ||

**kṣamāpaṇa**

mantra-hīnaṃ kriyā-hīnaṃ bhakti-hīnaṃ janārdana |  
yat pūjitaṃ mayā deva paripūrṇaṃ tad astu me ||

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante 'harniśaṃ mayā |  
dāso 'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvara ||

**visarjana / hṛdaya-nyāsa**

yāntu devagaṇāḥ sarve pūjām ādāya māmakīm |  
iṣṭakāma-prasiddhyarthaṃ punarāgamanāya ca ||

hṛdaye śrīmannārāyaṇaṃ dhyāyāmi |  
hṛdaye pitṝn śāntān smarāmi |  
hṛdaye sarvaṃ brahmārpaṇam iti niścinomi ||

**phala-samarpaṇa / śānti / parisaṃkhyāna**

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ sarve santu nirāmayāḥ |  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ||

pitṝṇāṃ śāntir astu | kulasya śāntir astu | lokasya śāntir astu |  
na kasyacid aniṣṭaṃ kāṅkṣe, na kasyacid duḥkham icchāmi |  
sarveṣāṃ bhūtānāṃ kalyāṇaṃ bhavatu ||

oṃ keśavāya svāhā |  
oṃ nārāyaṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ mādhavāya svāhā |

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ||  
sarvaṃ śrī-parameśvarārpaṇam astu ||
