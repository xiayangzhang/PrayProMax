## Obeah

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost.

O Lord God, look with mercy upon the foreparents who have gone before us.  
Let their passing be held in light, truth, and right order.  
Let sorrow be softened, let fear be quieted, and let no false word, envy, crossing, or wicked intention trouble the living or the dead.

The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want.  
He maketh me to lie down in green pastures:  
he leadeth me beside the still waters.  
He restoreth my soul:  
he leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name's sake.  
Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death,  
I will fear no evil: for thou art with me;  
thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.

Let what is not of God depart.  
Let what was tied in grief be loosed in mercy.  
Let what was spoken in fear fall silent.  
Let the dead be commended to peace, and let the living stand steady under the light that shineth in darkness.
