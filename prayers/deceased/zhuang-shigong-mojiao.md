## 壮族师公 / 麽教

[净坛报坛]

香灯已列，清水已陈，坛面已净，门户已开。  
今为先人往生安宁、离苦得乐、早登清净好处，至诚告坛。

[请师祖]

Cingj boux sai mo daengz.  
Cingj boux swhgung daengz.  
Cingj gyoengq swhbo daengz.  
Daihnaj miz dangh, daihlaeng miz roen.  
Gyoengq swhbo yawj, gyoengq swhbo couh.

[请布洛陀]

Cingj Bouxloxdoh daengz.  
Bouxloxdoh gwnz mbwn, Bouxloxdoh laj deih.  
Bouxloxdoh dingh leix, Bouxloxdoh dingh lwg.  
Cingj Bouxloxdoh yawj gij sim cingq,  
cingj Bouxloxdoh dingh gij roen ndei.

[请姆洛甲]

Cingj Mehloeggyap daengz.  
Mehloeggyap hoz vunz, Mehloeggyap hoz lwg.  
Mehloeggyap yawj ranz, Mehloeggyap yawj sim.  
Cingj Mehloeggyap yawj gij hwan gaenq,  
cingj Mehloeggyap hawj sim ndei, roen ndei.

[告祖]

Cingj goeng meh daengz.  
Ranz miz mbaw, vunz miz sim.  
Cingj goeng meh yawj ranz,  
cingj goeng meh yawj sim.  
Gij vunz gaenq, gij goeng meh gaenq,  
mbouj hawj youq roen dam,  
mbouj hawj youq roen doek.

[告社土]

Cingj deih boux, cingj lungz ranz.  
Gwnz mbwn miz dingh, laj deih miz roen.  
Cingj deih boux dingh deih,  
cingj lungz ranz dingh dou.  
Roen mbouj dam, dou mbouj laux,  
sim mbouj loek, fwen mbouj loek.

[发愿回向]

Gou daeuj baenz cieng,  
gou daeuj baenz raiz.  
Mbouj gangj dox, mbouj gangj laux.  
Gij ndei hawj gij vunz gaenq,  
gij ndei hawj goeng meh,  
gij ndei mbouj harm vunz, mbouj doek ranz.

[正行唱诵：麽唱]

Fwen mo hwnj, fwen mo loengh.  
Hwnj bae gwnz mbwn, loengh daeuj laj deih.  
Fwen hwnj roen ndei, fwen loengh sim ndei.  
Cingj swhgung yawj roen,  
cingj swhbo yawj sim.  
Cingj gyoengq swhbo dawz fwen,  
cingj gyoengq swhbo dawz roen.

[正行：请布洛陀定礼]

Cingj Bouxloxdoh dingh leix.  
Gij sienq mbouj loek,  
gij ciengz mbouj rox.  
Cingj Bouxloxdoh dingh roen,  
hawj roen mbouj dam, roen mbouj doek.

[正行：请姆洛甲慈护]

Cingj Mehloeggyap hoz lwg.  
Gij hwan gaenq miz gij ciengz,  
gij hwan gaenq miz gij sienq.  
Cingj Mehloeggyap yawj sim,  
hawj sim ndaej an, hwan ndaej ndei.

[正行：告祖开路]

Cingj goeng meh dingh ranz.  
Cingj gyoengq goeng meh dawz roen.  
Roen mbouj dam, roen mbouj doek,  
roen gvaq bya, roen gvaq dah.  
Daeuj baenz vunz gaenq,  
bae baenz vunz goeng.  
Bae ra goeng meh,  
bae ra ranz gwnz.

[开路送亡科]

Mingz vunz ndeu mbouj gangj,  
roen vunz ndeu cingq gangj.  
Cingj swhgung hoi roen,  
cingj swhbo sawq roen.  
Roen mbouj dam, roen mbouj doek,  
roen gvaq bya, roen gvaq dah.  
Hwan gaenq mbouj souh fu,  
hwan gaenq ndaej fuk.  
Bae ra goeng meh,  
bae ra ranz gwnz,  
bae dieg cingq ndei,  
youq dieg an ndei.

[纳福回向]

Gij fuk mbouj dox, gij ndei mbouj laux.  
Gij ndei dauq goeng meh,  
gij ndei dauq ranz,  
gij ndei dauq gij vunz gaenq.  
Hawj hwan gaenq roen ndei,  
hawj goeng meh sim an.

[辞布洛陀]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq Bouxloxdoh dauq gwnz.

[辞姆洛甲]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.

[辞祖先]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq goeng meh dauq ranz.  
Hawj goeng meh an,  
hawj hwan gaenq an.

[辞社土龙神]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq deih lungz dauq deih.  
Deih dingh, lungz dingh,  
dou dingh, roen dingh.

[送师祖]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq swhgung dauq dangh,  
soengq swhbo dauq roen.

[封坛回向]

Dou laeg, roen laeg.  
Fwen gaeq, fap gaeq.  
Gij ndei dauq gij vunz gaenq,  
gij ndei dauq goeng meh,  
gij ndei dauq ranz.  
Mbouj loek fwen,  
mbouj loek leix.  
Dingh.
