## 威卡 Alexandrian

[Purification and Intent]

I stand within the Circle, in perfect love and perfect trust.  
Let all within this rite be bounded by the Rede:  
An it harm none, do what ye will.

[Circle Casting]

By Air, by Fire, by Water, by Earth,  
this Circle is cast and sealed.  
Between the worlds, and in the presence of the Mighty Ones,  
let this rite be held for the peaceful passing of the beloved dead and the ancestors.

[Quarter Calls]

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

[Goddess Invocation]

O Great Mother,  
Lady of the Moon,  
giver of comfort, mystery, and rest,  
receive this prayer for the beloved dead and the ancestors:  
may they be held in peace, released from sorrow,  
and led gently into joy.

[God Invocation]

O Lord of the Greenwood,  
Horned One,  
guardian of life, death, and return,  
receive this prayer for the beloved dead and the ancestors:  
may their path be clear, their burdens loosened,  
and their spirits find safe passage beyond pain.

[Circle Chant and Petition]

Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak,  
Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak.

[Confirmation]

So mote it be.

[Thanks and Release of the Quarters]

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

[Circle Opening and Grounding]

The Circle is open, but unbroken.  
Merry meet, merry part, and merry meet again.

All excess power returns to Earth.  
All sorrow is grounded; all will is released.  
This rite is ended in peace, in right boundary, and in harm to none.

Blessed be.
