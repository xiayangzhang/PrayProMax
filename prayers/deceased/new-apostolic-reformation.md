## 新使徒改革

Father God, I come before You in the name of Jesus Christ, entrusting the deceased ancestors to Your righteous mercy and praying for comfort, peace, forgiveness, and surrender for the living who grieve.

{{petition}}

Πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς·  
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου·  
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου·  
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου,  
ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς·  
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον δὸς ἡμῖν σήμερον·  
καὶ ἄφες ἡμῖν τὰ ὀφειλήματα ἡμῶν,  
ὡς καὶ ἡμεῖς ἀφήκαμεν τοῖς ὀφειλέταις ἡμῶν·  
καὶ μὴ εἰσενέγκῃς ἡμᾶς εἰς πειρασμόν,  
ἀλλὰ ῥῦσαι ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τοῦ πονηροῦ.

Father, I confess every sin, fear, bitterness, accusation, unbelief, occult curiosity, unhealthy attachment, and false burden connected with death, mourning, ancestry, and memory. I repent of every attempt to control what belongs to You alone. I place the living household under the blood and lordship of Jesus Christ.

I forgive all who must be forgiven, and I release the deceased, the living, and myself from judgment, accusation, and vengeance. I do not curse, summon, control, or seek communication with the dead. I bless what You bless, and I reject only the works of darkness, lies, bondage, sin, fear, despair, and oppression.

Ἐγώ εἰμι ἡ ἀνάστασις καὶ ἡ ζωή·  
ὁ πιστεύων εἰς ἐμὲ κἂν ἀποθάνῃ ζήσεται·  
καὶ πᾶς ὁ ζῶν καὶ πιστεύων εἰς ἐμὲ  
οὐ μὴ ἀποθάνῃ εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα.

Εὐλογητὸς ὁ θεὸς καὶ πατὴρ  
τοῦ κυρίου ἡμῶν Ἰησοῦ Χριστοῦ,  
ὁ πατὴρ τῶν οἰκτιρμῶν  
καὶ θεὸς πάσης παρακλήσεως.

הַיֹּשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן  
בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי  
אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃

Ὑποτάγητε οὖν τῷ θεῷ·  
ἀντίστητε δὲ τῷ διαβόλῳ,  
καὶ φεύξεται ἀφ᾽ ὑμῶν.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I bind every spirit of fear, tormenting grief, accusation, condemnation, confusion, despair, occult deception, ancestral shame, and every power of the enemy that seeks to exploit mourning. I command every unclean influence operating through fear, guilt, bitterness, or hopelessness to leave and not return, according to the authority of Jesus Christ.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I loose the comfort of the Holy Spirit, the peace of Christ, holy remembrance, repentance where needed, forgiveness, healing of grief, hope in the resurrection, and obedience to the will of God over the living household.

I cut off every ungodly soul tie, false burden, occult claim, fear agreement, spoken curse, inherited pattern of sorrow, and demonic access point connected with death and mourning. I declare that the handwriting against the living is canceled in Christ, and that every hostile power is disarmed under the cross of Jesus Christ.

Καὶ αὐτοὶ ἐνίκησαν αὐτὸν  
διὰ τὸ αἷμα τοῦ ἀρνίου  
καὶ διὰ τὸν λόγον τῆς μαρτυρίας αὐτῶν.

Father, receive what belongs to Your judgment and mercy. Establish Your kingdom order, not human striving; Your comfort, not despair; Your truth, not superstition; Your peace, not fear. Fill every cleansed place with the Holy Spirit, the peace of Christ, the fear of the Lord, wisdom, purity, and steadfast love.

Father, guard this prayer from all misuse. Let no word become a curse against any human person, family, people, or generation. I refuse fear, compulsion, vengeance, manipulation, and spiritual pride. If grief becomes overwhelming, lead the living into repentance, forgiveness, wise pastoral care, and proper help. Let only Your mercy, Your truth, Your holiness, and Your peace remain.

I thank You, Father, and seal this prayer in the name of Jesus Christ.

Ἀμήν.
