## श्रौत / कर्मकाण्ड

oṃ

śuddhiḥ । ācamanaṃ । manasaḥ prasādaḥ ।

pitṛṇāṃ śānti-gati-duḥkha-vimokṣa-sukha-prāpti-puṇya-loka-prāpti-saṃbandhena  
idaṃ śānti-svasti-saṅkalpa-vacanaṃ kriyate ।  
na hiṃsāyai, na parāpakārāya, kevalaṃ śāntyai, kṣemāya, ṛtasya pathi iti saṅkalpaḥ ।

agnim īḷe purohitaṃ yajñasya devaṃ ṛtvijam ।  
hotāraṃ ratnadhātamam ॥

agne naya supathā rāye asmān  
viśvāni deva vayunāni vidvān ।  
yuyodhy asmaj juhurāṇam eno  
bhūyiṣṭhāṃ te nama-uktiṃ vidhema ॥

idam vāyave na mama ।

oṃ svaḥ sūryāya svāhā ।  
idam sūryāya na mama ।

tryambakaṃ yajāmahe sugandhiṃ puṣṭivardhanam ।  
urvārukam iva bandhanān mṛtyor mukṣīya mā'mṛtāt ॥

tat savitur vareṇyaṃ bhargo devasya dhīmahi ।  
dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt ॥

agnaye sviṣṭakṛte svāhā ।  
idam agnaye sviṣṭakṛte na mama ।

svasti na indro vṛddhaśravāḥ ।  
svasti naḥ pūṣā viśvavedāḥ ।  
svasti nas tārkṣyo ariṣṭanemiḥ ।  
svasti no bṛhaspatir dadhātu ॥

dyauḥ śāntir antarikṣaṃ śāntiḥ  
pṛthivī śāntir āpaḥ śāntir oṣadhayaḥ śāntiḥ ।  
vanaspatayaḥ śāntir viśve devāḥ śāntir brahma śāntiḥ  
sarvaṃ śāntiḥ śāntir eva śāntiḥ  
sā mā śāntir edhi ॥

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ॥

yat kṛtaṃ yad akṛtaṃ yad duṣkṛtaṃ yad vikṛtaṃ  
tat sarvaṃ agnaye sviṣṭakṛte samarpayāmi ।  
pitṛṇāṃ śāntaye, sarvabhūtānāṃ kṣemāya, ṛtasya pratiṣṭhāyai samarpayāmi ।  
idam na mama ।
