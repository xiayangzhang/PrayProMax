In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Holy and triune God, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit,  
before you we remember those who have gone before us, and we bring before you our grief, our gratitude, and our hope.  
You alone are God; you alone are to be worshiped; you alone hear prayer through Jesus Christ, the one Mediator between God and humanity.
