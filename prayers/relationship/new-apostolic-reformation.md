## 新使徒改革

{{petition}}

Πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς·  
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου·  
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου·  
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου,  
ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς·  
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον δὸς ἡμῖν σήμερον·  
καὶ ἄφες ἡμῖν τὰ ὀφειλήματα ἡμῶν,  
ὡς καὶ ἡμεῖς ἀφήκαμεν τοῖς ὀφειλέταις ἡμῶν·  
καὶ μὴ εἰσενέγκῃς ἡμᾶς εἰς πειρασμόν,  
ἀλλὰ ῥῦσαι ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τοῦ πονηροῦ.

Father, I confess and renounce every sin, pride, fear, bitterness, jealousy, accusation, selfish demand, ungodly vow, false expectation, and agreement with rejection or division connected with this relationship. I place my heart, my words, my desires, and this relationship under the blood and lordship of Jesus Christ.

I forgive, release, and bless the beloved person before You. I do not curse, control, pressure, manipulate, or claim vengeance over anyone. I surrender every desire to possess, force, or dominate, and I ask that love be purified by truth, patience, honor, and holiness.

הַיֹּשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן  
בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי  
אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃

Ἰδοὺ δέδωκα ὑμῖν τὴν ἐξουσίαν  
τοῦ πατεῖν ἐπάνω ὄφεων καὶ σκορπίων,  
καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν δύναμιν τοῦ ἐχθροῦ,  
καὶ οὐδὲν ὑμᾶς οὐ μὴ ἀδικήσει.

ὃ ἐὰν δήσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται δεδεμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς,  
καὶ ὃ ἐὰν λύσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται λελυμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς.

Ὑποτάγητε οὖν τῷ θεῷ·  
ἀντίστητε δὲ τῷ διαβόλῳ,  
καὶ φεύξεται ἀφ᾽ ὑμῶν.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I bind every work of darkness, every lie, fear, confusion, resentment, accusation, unhealthy attachment, spirit of division, and every demonic influence seeking to distort this relationship. I reject only the works of Satan, sin, bondage, deception, and oppression; I do not make any human person my enemy.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I loose truth, repentance, humility, peace, patience, wisdom, clear communication, emotional healing, mutual honor, godly affection, and obedience to the will of God over this relationship.

I cut off every ungodly soul tie, false burden, fear agreement, spoken curse, inner vow, fantasy of control, and demonic access point connected with this relationship. I declare that every handwriting against love, peace, and righteousness is canceled in Christ, and every hostile power is disarmed under the cross of Jesus Christ.

Καὶ αὐτοὶ ἐνίκησαν αὐτὸν  
διὰ τὸ αἷμα τοῦ ἀρνίου  
καὶ διὰ τὸν λόγον τῆς μαρτυρίας αὐτῶν.

Father, fill every cleansed place with the Holy Spirit, the peace of Christ, the fear of the Lord, wisdom, purity, steadfast love, and self-control. Establish Your kingdom order in this relationship. Let affection endure only in truth, freedom, consent, kindness, and righteousness. Let hearts understand one another without coercion, and let harmony grow without deception or bondage.

Father, keep this prayer under the mercy, lordship, and holy will of Jesus Christ. Silence every word in me that would accuse, frighten, manipulate, or wound. Turn my heart continually toward repentance, forgiveness, wise counsel, peace-making, and love that does no harm.

I thank You, Father, and seal this prayer in the name of Jesus Christ.

Ἀμήν.
