## الكنيسة المارونية

B-shmo d-Abo w-d-Bro w-d-Ruho d-Qudsho, Had Aloho shariro. Amin.

Qadishat Aloho,  
Qadishat Hayelthono,  
Qadishat lo Moyutho,  
ethraham ‘alayn.

Shubho l-Abo w-la-Bro w-la-Ruho d-Qudsho,  
men ‘olam w-‘adamo l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Abun d-bashmayo,  
netqadash shmokh.  
Tite malkuthokh.  
Nehwe sebyonokh,  
aykano d-bashmayo oph bar‘o.  
Haw lan lahmo d-sunqonan yawmono.  
Washboq lan hawbayn wahtohayn,  
aykano doph hnan shbaqn l-hayyobayn.  
Wlo ta‘lan l-nesyuno,  
elo fasso lan men bisho.  
Metul d-dilokh hi malkutho w-haylo w-teshbuhto  
l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Shlom lekh Maryam,  
mlayat taybutho,  
Moran ‘amekh.  
Mbarakhto at b-neshe,  
w-mbarakh hu piro d-karsekh,  
Maran Yeshua.  
O qadishto Maryam,  
Yoldath Aloho,  
saloy hlofayn hnaye,  
hosho w-bsho‘at mawthan. Amin.

B-shfa‘otho d-Yoldath Aloho Maryam,  
w-d-Mor Maron,  
w-d-Mor Efram,  
w-d-Mor Ya‘qub,  
w-d-kulhon qadisho,  
Moryo etraham ‘alayn.

Dkar, Moryo, l-‘abdayk w-l-amhotokh,  
kulhon d-ba‘en shayno, husoyo, w-hubo shariro;  
nattar enon men meltho qashyto, men lebbo palig,  
w-men kol re‘yana d-maphreq bayn habibe.

Dkar, Moryo, l-‘anide mhaymne,  
w-ashre enon b-nuhrakh w-b-shaynokh.

Moryo, nhoor lan b-nuhrokh,  
nattar lan b-slibokh,  
shadar ‘alayn rahmayk,  
w-ahlan b-shaynokh.  
B-sebyonokh qadisho nhewe kol meddem,  
w-b-rahmayk netdakker, netdakka, w-netnattar;  
d-la nteba‘ shultono ‘al nafsho d-akhro,  
elo nethaded b-hubo d-Mshiha w-b-shulmono d-Ruho.

Lakh shubho,  
l-Abokh iqoro,  
w-l-Ruho d-Qudsho segedto,  
hosho w-bkul zban  
w-l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.
