## 美洲原住民教会（NAC）

With humble hearts, we honor the Native American Church Roadmen, the Cedarman and Drummer, the families and tribes who keep the prayer road.

{{petition}}

Let love be steady without force, let anger cool without shame, and let each person walk in truth, patience, forgiveness, and respect.

Where we have spoken wrongly, help us make it right; where we have been wounded, help us heal without wishing harm on anyone.

Remember the families, the elders, the ancestors, the children yet to come, and all who sit in prayer for harmony.

Keep our feet on the humble road; let us take nothing that is not given, sing nothing that is not ours, and leave every medicine in rightful hands. Thank You, Creator. Amen.
