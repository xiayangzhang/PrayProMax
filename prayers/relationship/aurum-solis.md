Aphroditē Ourania, bearer of gracious harmony,  
turn only what is willing toward gentleness;  
kindle only what is rightful into warmth;  
make speech clear, patience strong, and affection steadfast.  
Let discord be softened, not by compulsion, but by truth;  
let hearts meet in freedom, respect, and luminous goodwill.

IAO. IAO. IAO.

The word is spoken, the measure is sealed, and the force is returned to its proper sphere.

If any impulse of coercion, fear, or excess has arisen, let it cease.  
Let personal grasping be withdrawn into silence.  
Let the Light purify the heart, the word, and the intention.  
Let all powers invoked return in peace to their proper order.
