## Obeah

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost.

{{petition}}

John 1:1-5:  
In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.  
The same was in the beginning with God.  
All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing made that was made.  
In him was life; and the life was the light of men.  
And the light shineth in darkness; and the darkness comprehended it not.

Psalm 133:1:  
Behold, how good and how pleasant it is  
for brethren to dwell together in unity!

Song of Solomon 8:6-7:  
Set me as a seal upon thine heart, as a seal upon thine arm:  
for love is strong as death; jealousy is cruel as the grave:  
the coals thereof are coals of fire, which hath a most vehement flame.  
Many waters cannot quench love, neither can the floods drown it:  
if a man would give all the substance of his house for love,  
it would utterly be contemned.

Let love be a seal of faithfulness, not a chain of compulsion.  
Let peace enter the house, the mouth, the mind, and the heart.  
Let each hear the other rightly.  
Let bitterness be loosed in truth.  
Let suspicion fall silent where honesty may stand.  
Let no harmful word, no envy, no fear, and no crooked intention take root between them.

Psalm 91:1-4:  
He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.  
I will say of the LORD, He is my refuge and my fortress: my God; in him will I trust.  
Surely he shall deliver thee from the snare of the fowler, and from the noisome pestilence.  
He shall cover thee with his feathers, and under his wings shalt thou trust:  
his truth shall be thy shield and buckler.

Let what is not of God depart.  
Let what was tied in falsehood be loosed in truth.  
Let what was spoken in envy fall silent.  
Let this love stand only where it can stand in freedom, mercy, and right order.  
Let the light that shineth in darkness keep the way clear.

Numbers 6:24-26:  
The LORD bless thee, and keep thee:  
The LORD make his face shine upon thee, and be gracious unto thee:  
The LORD lift up his countenance upon thee, and give thee peace.

In the light of the Word, let fear be stilled.  
Under the shadow of the Almighty, let harm be turned away.  
By the blessing of the LORD, let peace remain, truth remain, and love remain in righteousness.

Amen.
