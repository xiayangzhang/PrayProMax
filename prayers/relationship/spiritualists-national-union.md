## Spiritualists' National Union

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, source of love, truth, and life,

{{petition}}

The Fatherhood of God.  
The Brotherhood of Man.  
The Communion of Spirits and the Ministry of Angels.  
The continuous existence of the human soul.  
Personal Responsibility.  
Compensation and Retribution hereafter for all the good and evil deeds done on earth.  
Eternal progress open to every human soul.

May love surround this relationship.  
May truth guide each heart.  
May healing, comfort, and strength be present according to the highest good.  
May all thought, word, and action be held in kindness, honesty, patience, and personal responsibility.

For enduring affection and harmony, may there be clarity without fear,  
comfort without coercion,  
guidance without domination,  
and peace without claim of certainty beyond truth.

Where misunderstanding is present, may gentle communication unfold.  
Where distance is present, may compassion draw hearts nearer in freedom.  
Where conflict is present, may brotherhood, forgiveness, and respect arise.  
Where love is present, may it be strengthened by sincerity, trust, and mutual care.

May all in the Spirit World who work in love and light bless the living with peace, without pressure or command.  
May all souls, seen and unseen, be honoured in the continuous life of the spirit, and may only goodwill, upliftment, and healing influence be welcomed here.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit,  
let this prayer remain a humble service of love, truth, healing, comfort, spiritual growth, and eternal progress.  
May no will be forced, no fear be planted, and no promise be claimed beyond the highest good.

Amen.
