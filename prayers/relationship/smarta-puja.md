## स्मार्त

oṃ apavitraḥ pavitro vā sarvāvasthāṃ gato 'pi vā |  
yaḥ smaret puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ sa bāhyābhyantaraḥ śuciḥ ||

oṃ keśavāya svāhā |  
oṃ nārāyaṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ mādhavāya svāhā ||

oṃ bhūḥ | oṃ bhuvaḥ | oṃ suvaḥ | oṃ mahaḥ | oṃ janaḥ | oṃ tapaḥ | oṃ satyam |  
oṃ tat savitur vareṇyaṃ bhargo devasya dhīmahi dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt |  
oṃ āpo jyotī raso 'mṛtaṃ brahma bhūr bhuvaḥ suvar oṃ ||

mama upātta-samasta-duritakṣayadvārā śrī-parameśvara-prītyarthaṃ  
priya-janena saha dīrgha-sneha-sauhārda-sāmāñjasya-citta-saṃvāda-siddhyarthaṃ  
śrī-umāmaheśvara-pūjāṃ kariṣye ||

oṃ gaṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ |  
vakratuṇḍa mahākāya sūryakoṭisamaprabha |  
nirvighnaṃ kuru me deva sarvakāryeṣu sarvadā ||

oṃ kalaśasya mukhe viṣṇuḥ kaṇṭhe rudraḥ samāśritaḥ |  
mūle tatra sthito brahmā madhye mātṛgaṇāḥ smṛtāḥ |  
kukṣau tu sāgarāḥ sarve sapta-dvīpā vasundharā |  
ṛgvedo 'tha yajurvedaḥ sāmavedo hy atharvaṇaḥ |  
aṅgaiś ca sahitāḥ sarve kalaśāmbu samāśritāḥ ||

oṃ gaurīśaṅkarau dhyāyāmi |  
śivāṃ śivamayaṃ śaṅkaraṃ ca umāsahitaṃ hṛdaye bhāvayāmi ||

oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ āvāhayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ āsanaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ pādyaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ arghyaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ ācamanīyaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ snānaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ vastraṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ yajñopavītaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ gandhaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ akṣatān samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ puṣpāṇi samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ dhūpam āghrāpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ dīpaṃ darśayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ naivedyaṃ nivedayāmi |  
oṃ prāṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ apānāya svāhā |  
oṃ vyānāya svāhā |  
oṃ udānāya svāhā |  
oṃ samānāya svāhā |  
oṃ brahmaṇe svāhā |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ tāmbūlaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ nīrājanaṃ samarpayāmi ||

oṃ namaḥ śivāya |  
oṃ śrī-mātre namaḥ |  
oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ namaḥ ||

oṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyāṃ namaḥ |  
namaste astu bhagavantau śrī-umāmaheśvarau |  
anena pūjā-japa-stotra-pāṭhena  
dīrgha-sneha-sauhārda-citta-saṃvāda-siddhir astu ||

mantra-hīnaṃ kriyā-hīnaṃ bhakti-hīnaṃ śrī-umāmaheśvarābhyām |  
yat pūjitaṃ mayā devau paripūrṇaṃ tad astu me ||

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante 'harniśaṃ mayā |  
dāso 'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvara ||

yāntu devagaṇāḥ sarve pūjām ādāya māmakīm |  
iṣṭakāma-prasiddhyarthaṃ punarāgamanāya ca ||

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ sarve santu nirāmayāḥ |  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ||

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ||

sarvaṃ śrī-parameśvarārpaṇam astu ||
