## Hoodoo / Rootwork

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.

I stand before the Most High God with clean speech and a steady heart.  
{{petition}}

דּוֹדִי לִי וַאֲנִי לוֹ הָרֹעֶה בַּשּׁוֹשַׁנִּים׃

שִׂימֵנִי כַחוֹתָם עַל־לִבֶּךָ כַּחוֹתָם עַל־זְרוֹעֶךָ כִּי־עַזָּה כַמָּוֶת אַהֲבָה קָשָׁה כִשְׁאוֹל קִנְאָה רְשָׁפֶיהָ רִשְׁפֵּי אֵשׁ שַׁלְהֶבֶתְיָה׃  
מַיִם רַבִּים לֹא יוּכְלוּ לְכַבּוֹת אֶת־הָאַהֲבָה וּנְהָרוֹת לֹא יִשְׁטְפוּהָ אִם־יִתֵּן אִישׁ אֶת־כָּל־הוֹן בֵּיתוֹ בָּאַהֲבָה בּוֹז יָבוּזוּ לוֹ׃

My beloved is mine, and I am my beloved’s.  
Set love as a seal upon the heart, and let many waters not quench what is honest, willing, and blessed.

Let speech be sweet and not deceitful.  
Let anger cool before it wounds.  
Let pride step back, and let mercy step forward.  
Let listening be opened, understanding be strengthened, and affection remain free, faithful, and kind.

I speak no harm into the innocent.  
I bind no unwilling heart.  
I seek no taking of another’s peace, no breaking of another’s rightful love, and no sorrow laid upon any household.  
I take back every crooked word, every proud demand, and every petition not fit to stand before God.

As my word is spoken before the Lord,  
let confusion, resentment, coldness, and needless strife be cut away.  
Let the road of concord be opened in righteousness.  
Let the house be guarded in peace.  
Let the hands be clean.  
Let the feet be set on a straight path.

יָפְיָפִיתָ מִבְּנֵי אָדָם הוּצַק חֵן בְּשִׂפְתוֹתֶיךָ עַל־כֵּן בֵּרַכְךָ אֱלֹהִים לְעוֹלָם׃

Let grace be poured upon the lips.  
Let words carry healing and not a knife.  
Let love be lawful, truthful, mutual, and unbound by force.

יֹשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃  
כִּי הוּא יַצִּילְךָ מִפַּח יָקוּשׁ מִדֶּבֶר הַוּוֹת׃  
בְּאֶבְרָתוֹ יָסֶךְ לָךְ וְתַחַת־כְּנָפָיו תֶּחְסֶה צִנָּה וְסֹחֵרָה אֲמִתּוֹ׃

If this prayer touches the living, let it touch them in justice and mercy.  
If this prayer concerns love, let love be willing, truthful, and unbound by force.  
If this prayer concerns danger, let the snare be seen, the harm be turned aside, and the soul be kept.

Set this petition under the judgment of the Most High.  
What is righteous, establish it.  
What is crooked, remove it.  
What is not mine to command, return it to God.

חָנֵּנִי אֱלֹהִים כְּחַסְדֶּךָ כְּרֹב רַחֲמֶיךָ מְחֵה פְשָׁעָי׃  
הֶרֶב כַּבְּסֵנִי מֵעֲוֹנִי וּמֵחַטָּאתִי טַהֲרֵנִי׃  
לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים וְרוּחַ נָכוֹן חַדֵּשׁ בְּקִרְבִּי׃

Wash away every wrongful desire, every forcing hand, every jealous snare, and every unclean petition.  
Cleanse this work and turn it only toward protection, reconciliation, open roads, honest speech, and peace under God’s mercy.

שִׁיר לַמַּעֲלוֹת אֶשָּׂא עֵינַי אֶל־הֶהָרִים מֵאַיִן יָבֹא עֶזְרִי׃  
עֶזְרִי מֵעִם יְהוָה עֹשֵׂה שָׁמַיִם וָאָרֶץ׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָרְךָ מִכָּל־רָע יִשְׁמֹר אֶת־נַפְשֶׁךָ׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָר־צֵאתְךָ וּבוֹאֶךָ מֵעַתָּה וְעַד־עוֹלָם׃

Amen. Amen. Amen.
