## 共济会

Most holy and glorious Lord God,  
the Great Architect of the Universe,  
the Giver of all good gifts and graces:

Thou hast promised that where two or three are gathered together in Thy Name,  
Thou wilt be in the midst of them and bless them.

{{petition}}

Grant that every thought, word, and act  
may be ordered by Wisdom,  
supported by Strength,  
and adorned with Beauty.

הִנֵּה מַה־טּוֹב וּמַה־נָּעִים  
שֶׁבֶת אַחִים גַּם־יָחַד׃

Let Brotherly Love, Relief, and Truth  
govern every motion of the heart.  
Let patience temper speech,  
let sincerity make plain the inward intention,  
and let peace dwell between two hearts without compulsion, deceit, or pride.

May affection be built as a well-laid stone,  
true to the square, steady by the plumb,  
and joined in harmony under Thy light.  
Let no anger, vanity, false witness, unlawful desire,  
or betrayal of trust enter this work.

We seek no mastery over another will,  
no harm to any household,  
and no gain by the sorrow or loss of others;  
but only concord, honesty, faithful regard,  
and the quiet blessing of hearts that understand one another.

Keep us faithful to every lawful obligation,  
silent where silence is due,  
truthful where speech is required,  
and charitable toward all mankind.

May the Supreme Grand Master  
preside over this intention,  
guide it by mercy and uprightness,  
and close all discord in peace.

So mote it be.
