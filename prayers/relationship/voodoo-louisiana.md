## 路易斯安那伏都 Voodoo

Bon Dieu, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit,  
I wash my mouth clean of bitterness, haste, and command.  
I speak this prayer for lasting affection, harmony, and true understanding with the beloved one,  
not to bind another will, not to dominate the heart, but to make a clean road for peace, kindness, and mutual regard.

Let this hand be named Peaceful Heart Hand,  
and let its prayer be bound only to honest love, patient speech, faithful friendship, and right consent.

Pater noster, qui es in caelis,  
sanctificetur nomen tuum.  
Adveniat regnum tuum.  
Fiat voluntas tua,  
sicut in caelo et in terra.  
Panem nostrum quotidianum da nobis hodie,  
et dimitte nobis debita nostra,  
sicut et nos dimittimus debitoribus nostris.  
Et ne nos inducas in tentationem,  
sed libera nos a malo.  
Amen.

Holy ancestors of my blood and my house, witness only what is clean in this prayer.  
Guardian spirits on the right road, stand near only in peace and right measure.  
Turn away envy, confusion, and restless speech, without harm to any soul.  
Let no promise be made beyond what is mine to keep.  
Let no hidden knot be tied against another person’s freedom.

This Peaceful Heart Hand is sealed for harmony, patience, tenderness, and mutual understanding.  
It is not sealed for force, obsession, fear, or command.  
May what is clean be strengthened; may what is crooked fall away.

Gloria Patri, et Filio, et Spiritui Sancto.  
Sicut erat in principio, et nunc, et semper,  
et in saecula saeculorum.  
Amen.

לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים

Bon Dieu, if any word has gone beyond rightful measure, let it be washed clean.  
If any vow was spoken in heat, fear, or longing, let it be released without debt and without harm.  
Ancestors, receive my thanks, cool my head, steady my heart, and keep this prayer within clean boundaries.  
By mercy, by humility, by the right road, this prayer is closed.  
Amen. Ashe.
