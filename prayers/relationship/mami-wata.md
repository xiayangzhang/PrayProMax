## Mami Wata 信仰

We stand by clean water with quiet mouth and cool heart.  
Mami Wata, Mammy Water, Nne mmiri, Yeye Omi,  
we call only your name for this prayer.

Mami Wata o, Mami Wata o,  
bia, nuru olu anyi.  
Nne mmiri, nne oma,  
mmiri di ọcha, mmiri ndu.

Mammy Water, we hail you.  
Fine lady for water, mother for river,  
she wey sabi deep water,  
she wey hold mirror for truth,  
she wey cool hot heart.

Yeye Omi, iya omi,  
bo wa pelu omi funfun.  
Omi tutu, omi alaafia,  
ko si ija, ko si iro,  
alaafia ni a pe.

Mami Wata o, Mammy Water o,  
no vex, no trouble.  
If wrong word don pass our mouth,  
make wrong word fall for water.  
If anger don stain this bond,  
make clean water wash the stain.  
If promise dey, make only true promise stand;  
if promise no clean, make it cool and loosen in peace.

Mmiri oma, kuru ihe ojoo laa.  
Mmiri ndu, mee ka obi di jụụ.  
Nne mmiri, nara olu anyi,  
nara mmiri a,  
nara okwu udo a.

Mami Wata o,  
we no call another spirit for this same mouth.  
Only Mami Wata name dey here.  
Peace for water,  
peace for road,  
peace for house,  
peace for the hearts wey seek to understand each other.

Any proud hand wey want do secret oath, wrong sacrifice, forced love, or false spirit work,  
make that hand rest.  
Any unclean request, we withdraw am into clean water.  
Any matter wey pass simple praise and peace,  
make it go to proper elders and rightful hands.  
Clean water before us, cool water behind us,  
no fear, no manipulation, no harm.

Mammy Water, we thank you.  
Nne mmiri, daalu.  
Yeye Omi, mo dupe.  
Alaafia. Udo. Peace.
