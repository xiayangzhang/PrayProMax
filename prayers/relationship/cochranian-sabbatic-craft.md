## 现代术士派（Cochranian、Sabbatic Craft）

In the name of the Old One,  
Master of the Crooked Path,  
Keeper of the hidden fire,  
I hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
let this ring be whole,  
let no false word enter,  
let no oath be broken,  
let no secret be profaned.

{{petition}}

No stolen oath is spoken here.  
No hidden word is claimed here.  
Only the open charm is set,  
only the rightful prayer is made.

Truth to tongue, peace to hand,  
clear sight to heart, and bounds to bond;  
let goodwill stand where it is rightful,  
let false binding fall away,  
let no will be chained,  
let no heart be compelled,  
let affection remain by freedom,  
and harmony dwell where love is mutual.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
the word is spoken,  
the work is sealed,  
the compass is whole.

If any word has crossed its proper bound,  
let it be withdrawn into silence.  
If any desire has reached beyond right measure,  
let it be cooled and returned to its own hearth.  
Old One, receive thanks.  
Let the road be shut where it must be shut.  
Let the road be open where it must be open.  
Peace to the ring,  
peace to the dead,  
peace to the living,  
and peace to the hearts that seek concord.

So mote it be.
