## 威卡 Gardnerian

In this place, in this hour, I stand between the worlds,  
with clean hands, clear speech, and a will held in Perfect Love and Perfect Trust.

I conjure thee, O Circle of Power,  
that thou beest a boundary between the world of men and the realms of the Mighty Ones,  
a guardian and a meeting-place for the working of this event:  
a job interview blessed with calm presence, clear mind, graceful speech, fair recognition, and the finding of a right and fitting opportunity.

Lady of the Moon,  
Great Mother, Bright One, Queen of the secret place,  
hear the words spoken within this circle.

Whenever ye have need of anything,  
once in the month, and better it be when the moon is full,  
then shall ye assemble in some desert place,  
or in a forest all together join  
to adore the potent spirit of your queen.

Horned One,  
Lord of the wildwood, keeper of life and death,  
stand apart in thy own power and witness this working.

{{petition}}

Before the Lord I speak the boundary:  
Let this prayer bind no will, cloud no judgment, and compel no person.  
Let success come only by truth, readiness, good timing, and right accord.  
Let it dispossess no other, diminish no other’s livelihood, and take no blessing unjustly from another’s hands.

Let this prayer be for clear presence, fair recognition, and right livelihood,  
harming none, binding none against the true will,  
and turning aside all falsehood, fear, and ill-wrought force.

Eko, Eko, Azarak,  
Eko, Eko, Zomelak.

By earth, let this opportunity take lawful root.  
By air, let thought be clear and speech be skillful.  
By fire, let courage be kindled without arrogance.  
By water, let feeling flow in peace and composure.

Lady of the Moon, receive this prayer.  
Horned One, witness this prayer.  
Mighty Ones of the circle, keep it within right measure.

As the cup and the bread are blessed in fellowship,  
so may the work be whole:  
for this interview, for right success,  
according to freedom, consent, truth, and the Rede.

May all excess be grounded into the good earth.  
May all fear be washed away in clean water.  
May all speech be purified by living air.  
May all will be tempered by sacred fire.  
What is not mine to hold, I release; what is rightly mine to learn, I receive.

Lady of the Moon, I thank thee.  
Horned One, I thank thee.  
Mighty Ones of the circle, I thank thee.  
Let no hidden oath be trespassed, no secret thing be taken, no will be constrained.  
Let this be only a plain and open devotion, held in Perfect Love and Perfect Trust.

The circle is open, yet unbroken.  
Merry meet, and merry part, and merry meet again.
