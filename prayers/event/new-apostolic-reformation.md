## 新使徒改革

{{petition}}

Πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς·  
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου·  
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου·  
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου,  
ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς·  
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον δὸς ἡμῖν σήμερον·  
καὶ ἄφες ἡμῖν τὰ ὀφειλήματα ἡμῶν,  
ὡς καὶ ἡμεῖς ἀφήκαμεν τοῖς ὀφειλέταις ἡμῶν·  
καὶ μὴ εἰσενέγκῃς ἡμᾶς εἰς πειρασμόν,  
ἀλλὰ ῥῦσαι ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τοῦ πονηροῦ.

Father, I confess and renounce every sin, agreement, fear, bitterness, accusation, pride, comparison, idolatry of success, ungodly vow, inherited pattern, false word, and anxious imagination connected with this interview and employment process. I place this matter under the blood and lordship of Jesus Christ.

I forgive every person connected with past disappointment, rejection, misunderstanding, or unfair treatment, and I release them from my judgment. I do not curse them, control them, or claim vengeance over them. I bless what You bless, and I reject only the works of darkness, lies, bondage, sin, confusion, fear, and oppression.

הַיֹּשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן  
בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי  
אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃

Ἰδοὺ δέδωκα ὑμῖν τὴν ἐξουσίαν  
τοῦ πατεῖν ἐπάνω ὄφεων καὶ σκορπίων,  
καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν δύναμιν τοῦ ἐχθροῦ,  
καὶ οὐδὲν ὑμᾶς οὐ μὴ ἀδικήσει.

ὃ ἐὰν δήσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται δεδεμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς,  
καὶ ὃ ἐὰν λύσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται λελυμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς.

Ὑποτάγητε οὖν τῷ θεῷ·  
ἀντίστητε δὲ τῷ διαβόλῳ,  
καὶ φεύξεται ἀφ᾽ ὑμῶν.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I bind every demonic assignment, accusation, covenant of darkness, curse, word of death, spirit of fear, spirit of confusion, spirit of rejection, spirit of intimidation, spirit of self-sabotage, and every power of the enemy operating against wisdom, peace, integrity, and right communication in this interview process. I command every unclean influence attached to anxiety, shame, delay, discouragement, or deception to leave and not return, according to the authority of Jesus Christ.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I loose truth, repentance, faith, holiness, peace, protection, discernment, wisdom, favor, courage, clear memory, orderly thought, gracious speech, honest presentation, and obedience to the will of God over this interview and employment decision.

I cut off every ungodly soul tie, false burden, occult claim, generational bondage, spoken curse, inner vow, fear agreement, comparison agreement, rejection agreement, poverty agreement, and demonic access point connected with work, identity, provision, and opportunity. I declare that the handwriting against this matter is canceled in Christ and that every hostile power is disarmed under the cross of Jesus Christ.

I do not seek to dispossess another person, manipulate any decision-maker, harm any competitor, or take what You have appointed for someone else. Father, open only the door that is righteous, fitting, timely, and aligned with Your kingdom purpose; close every door that would lead into bondage, compromise, pride, exploitation, or confusion.

Καὶ αὐτοὶ ἐνίκησαν αὐτὸν  
διὰ τὸ αἷμα τοῦ ἀρνίου  
καὶ διὰ τὸν λόγον τῆς μαρτυρίας αὐτῶν.

Father, fill every cleansed place with the Holy Spirit, the peace of Christ, the fear of the Lord, wisdom, purity, humility, steadfast love, and sound judgment. Establish Your kingdom order over this interview: let preparation be fruitful, timing be ordered, questions be understood, answers be honest and clear, and the outcome be received with trust.

Cover this process with the blood of Jesus Christ. Let no fear, compulsion, bitterness, manipulation, rivalry, or accusation remain. I bless the interviewers, the workplace, all other candidates, and every person involved; may truth, justice, mercy, and peace prevail among them. If fear, obsession, conflict, or distress rises up, lead me back to repentance, forgiveness, wise counsel, pastoral care, and the peace of Christ.

I thank You, Father, and seal this prayer in the name of Jesus Christ.

Ἀμήν.
