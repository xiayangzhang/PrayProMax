## 共济会

Most holy and glorious Lord God,  
the Great Architect of the Universe,  
the Giver of all good gifts and graces:

Thou hast promised that where two or three are gathered together in Thy Name,  
Thou wilt be in the midst of them and bless them.

In Thy Name, and in reverence before Thy presence,  
we place this petition concerning the appointed interview,  
that it may proceed in order, concord, and uprightness.

Grant that every thought, word, and act  
may be ordered by Wisdom,  
supported by Strength,  
and adorned with Beauty.

{{petition}}

We seek no gain by falsehood, coercion, vanity,  
or by dispossessing another of rightful good;  
but only that the path most just, useful, and honorable  
may be opened according to Thy wise providence.

הִנֵּה מַה־טּוֹב וּמַה־נָּעִים  
שֶׁבֶת אַחִים גַּם־יָחַד׃

Let Brotherly Love, Relief, and Truth  
govern our hearts toward all persons met in this undertaking.  
Let no anger, unlawful gain, false witness,  
or betrayal of trust enter this work.

Direct the labor of this interview  
so that it may be conducted with fairness, clarity, and mutual respect;  
and if this opportunity be rightly fitted,  
let favor, understanding, and good accord attend it.

Keep us faithful to every lawful obligation,  
silent where silence is due,  
truthful where speech is required,  
and charitable toward all mankind.

May the Supreme Grand Master  
preside over this intention,  
until all work is closed in peace.

So mote it be.
