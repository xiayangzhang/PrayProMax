## 黑人教会传统

Father God, in the name of Jesus, we come before You.

Leader:  
Lord, we thank You for waking us up, clothing us in our right mind, and keeping us by Your mercy.

People:  
Amen. Thank You, Lord.

Leader:  
We come not in our own strength, but leaning on Your everlasting arms.  
Search us, Lord; forgive us, cleanse us, and make our hearts right before You.

People:  
Lord, have mercy.

Leader:  
{{petition}}

Scripture:  
I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from whence cometh my help.  
My help cometh from the LORD, which made heaven and earth.  
He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: he that keepeth thee will not slumber.  
Behold, he that keepeth Israel shall neither slumber nor sleep.  
The LORD is thy keeper: the LORD is thy shade upon thy right hand.  
The sun shall not smite thee by day, nor the moon by night.  
The LORD shall preserve thee from all evil: he shall preserve thy soul.  
The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in from this time forth, and even for evermore.

People:  
Amen.

Spiritual:  
There is a balm in Gilead,  
To make the wounded whole;  
There is a balm in Gilead,  
To heal the sin-sick soul.

Leader:  
Lord, when the interview hour comes, let anxiety be quieted and let wisdom rise.  
Set the mind in order, steady the breath, and season every word with grace.  
Let the answers be honest, clear, and confident.  
Let listening be attentive, speech be measured, and presence be peaceful.

People:  
Yes, Lord.

Leader:  
If this opportunity is fitting, let there be favor, recognition, and a good match.  
If another door is better, guide the feet without shame and without fear.  
We do not ask to take what belongs to another, to harm another candidate, or to gain by anyone’s loss.  
We ask for daily bread, righteous provision, and the place where gifts may serve with integrity.

People:  
Amen. Make the way, Lord.

Leader:  
Lord, where there is confusion, give wisdom.  
Where there is nervousness, give peace.  
Where there is self-doubt, give remembrance of Your care.  
Where there is a closed door, give patience.  
Where there is an open door, give humility and readiness.

Scripture:  
The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want.  
He maketh me to lie down in green pastures:  
he leadeth me beside the still waters.  
He restoreth my soul:  
he leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name's sake.

People:  
Amen.

Leader:  
We remember all who are seeking work, seeking stability, seeking dignity, and seeking a place to use what You have placed in their hands.  
Strengthen the weary, encourage the overlooked, and provide for every household standing in need of prayer.

People:  
Lord, provide. Lord, guide.

Leader:  
Now, Lord, teach us to pray:

All:  
Our Father which art in heaven,  
Hallowed be thy name.  
Thy kingdom come.  
Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.  
Give us this day our daily bread.  
And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.  
And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil:  
For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever.  
Amen.

Leader:  
Father, keep this prayer humble, rooted in Your Word, held in the love of the community, and covered by Your mercy.  
Let no fear, pride, or bargaining enter here; let Your Spirit lead, let Christ be honored, and let Your will be done.  
We seal this prayer in faith, not by might, nor by power, but by Your Spirit.  
In Jesus’ name.

People:  
Amen. Amen. Amen.
