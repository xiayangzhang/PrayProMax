## 神智学协会

{{petition}}

From the point of Light within the Mind of God  
Let light stream forth into the minds of men.  
Let Light descend on Earth.

May clear perception, steady thought, and wise discernment illumine this event, so that words may be truthful, measured, and timely.

From the point of Love within the Heart of God  
Let love stream forth into the hearts of men.  
May Christ return to Earth.

May goodwill govern the meeting of persons; may appreciation arise without compulsion; may no will be coerced, no place be taken unjustly, and no one be deprived of rightful good.

From the centre where the Will of God is known  
Let purpose guide the little wills of men—  
The purpose which the Masters know and serve.

May the purpose of this interview be aligned with honest work, right livelihood, service, and the unfolding of a proper path.

From the centre which we call the race of men  
Let the Plan of Love and Light work out  
And may it seal the door where evil dwells.

May anxiety, confusion, harmful influence, and self-seeking obstruction be closed away, without hatred, retaliation, or harm to any being.

Let Light and Love and Power restore the Plan on Earth.

May this personal need be held within Light, Love and Power; may success, if rightly granted, serve not only private gain but the wider good; may all outcomes be accepted without coercion, and may the good of the whole human family and of all beings be served.
