## 室利韦迪亚（Śrī Vidyā）

oṃ apavitraḥ pavitro vā sarvāvasthāṃ gato 'pi vā |  
yaḥ smaret puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ sa bāhyābhyantaraḥ śuciḥ ||

oṃ gaṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ ||

gurur brahmā gurur viṣṇuḥ gurur devo maheśvaraḥ |  
guruḥ sākṣāt paraṃ brahma tasmai śrī-gurave namaḥ ||

oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ śrī-gurubhyo namaḥ |  
oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ śrī-lalitā-tripurasundaryai namaḥ |

udyoga-sākṣātkāra-sarva-maṅgala-pravṛttyarthaṃ,  
tat-kāla-citta-prasāda-smṛti-prakāśa-vāk-sauṣṭhava-dhairya-siddhyarthaṃ,  
sat-puruṣaiḥ guṇa-parijñāna-prāptyarthaṃ,  
yogya-udyoga-lābha-siddhyarthaṃ,  
aparānāṃ bhāgya-hāniṃ vinā, aparānām āyuḥ-kṣayaṃ vinā,  
anṛta-vañcana-parapīḍā-varjita-śubha-saṅkalpa-siddhyarthaṃ,  
śrī-lalitā-tripurasundarī-prītyarthaṃ,  
ayaṃ saṅkalpaḥ ||

aruṇāṃ karuṇā-taraṅgitākṣīṃ  
dhṛta-pāśāṅkuśa-puṣpa-bāṇa-cāpām |  
aṇimādibhirāvṛtāṃ mayūkhair  
ahamityeva vibhāvaye bhavānīm ||

oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ śrī-lalitā-tripurasundaryai namaḥ ||  
oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ śrī-mātre namaḥ ||  
oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ śrī-mahārājñyai namaḥ ||  
oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ śrīmat-siṃhāsaneśvaryai namaḥ ||

śrīmātā śrīmahārājñī śrīmat-siṃhāsaneśvarī |  
cidagni-kuṇḍa-sambhūtā devakārya-samudyatā ||  
udyad-bhānu-sahasrābhā caturbāhu-samanvitā |  
rāga-svarūpa-pāśāḍhyā krodhākārāṅkuśojjvalā ||  
manorūpekṣu-kodaṇḍā pañca-tanmātra-sāyakā |  
nijāruṇa-prabhāpūra-majjad-brahmāṇḍa-maṇḍalā ||

oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ śrī-lalitā-tripurasundaryai namaḥ |  
nāma-japaṃ samarpayāmi ||

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante 'harniśaṃ mayā |  
dāso 'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvari ||

yadakṣara-pada-bhraṣṭaṃ mātrā-hīnaṃ ca yad bhavet |  
tat sarvaṃ kṣamyatāṃ devi prasīda parameśvari ||

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ sarve santu nirāmayāḥ |  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu mā kaścid duḥkha-bhāg bhavet ||

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ||

anena śrī-lalitā-tripurasundarī-nāma-stotra-mantra-japena  
udyoga-sākṣātkāra-saṅkalpasya śuddhi-siddhir astu |  
etat phalaṃ śrī-lalitā-tripurasundaryai samarpyate |  
guru-paramparā-prasādena sarvaṃ śubham astu |  
oṃ tat sat ||
