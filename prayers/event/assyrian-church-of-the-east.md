nethqaddash shmakh;  
tite malkuthakh;  
nehwe sebyanakh,  
aykanna d-ba-shmayyā ap b-ar‘a.

washbuq lan ḥawbayn wa-khṭahayn,  
aykana d-af ḥnan shbaqn l-ḥayyabayn;

ella paṣṣan min bisha.

l-‘ālam ‘ālmin. Āmēn.

Marya Alāhā,  
ḥus ‘layn w-dak lan men khṭahayn,  
w-shari b-libbān shayna,  
w-b-re‘yanān nuhrā,  
w-b-pumman millē d-ḥekhmta w-makyikhuta.
