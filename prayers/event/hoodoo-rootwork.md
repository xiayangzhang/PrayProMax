## Hoodoo / Rootwork

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.

שִׁיר לַמַּעֲלוֹת אֶשָּׂא עֵינַי אֶל־הֶהָרִים מֵאַיִן יָבֹא עֶזְרִי׃  
עֶזְרִי מֵעִם יְהוָה עֹשֵׂה שָׁמַיִם וָאָרֶץ׃  
אַל־יִתֵּן לַמּוֹט רַגְלֶךָ אַל־יָנוּם שֹׁמְרֶךָ׃  
הִנֵּה לֹא־יָנוּם וְלֹא יִישָׁן שׁוֹמֵר יִשְׂרָאֵל׃  
יְהוָה שֹׁמְרֶךָ יְהוָה צִלְּךָ עַל־יַד יְמִינֶךָ׃  
יוֹמָם הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ לֹא־יַכֶּכָּה וְיָרֵחַ בַּלָּיְלָה׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָרְךָ מִכָּל־רָע יִשְׁמֹר אֶת־נַפְשֶׁךָ׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָר־צֵאתְךָ וּבוֹאֶךָ מֵעַתָּה וְעַד־עוֹלָם׃

As my eyes are lifted up, let help come from the Lord.  
As my feet go out and come in, let the road to the interview be kept.  
Let the mouth be opened with truth, the mind be clear as clean water, the heart be steady, and the answer come in its right time.  
Let the worthy skill be seen, let fair favor meet honest effort, and let the door that is righteous open without forcing any will.

I speak no harm into the innocent.  
I bind no unwilling heart.  
I take back every crooked word, every proud demand, and every petition not fit to stand before God.  
Let confusion, fear, stumbling speech, unjust delay, and hidden obstruction be cut away.  
Let the path of this interview be opened in righteousness.  
Let clean hands, clear speech, and a straight road be established.

Set this petition under the judgment of the Most High.  
What is righteous, establish it.  
What is crooked, remove it.  
What is not mine to command, return it to God.  
If this employment is a good and lawful match, let it come with peace, honest increase, and right recognition.  
If another way is better, guide the feet to that better door and keep the soul from harm.

חָנֵּנִי אֱלֹהִים כְּחַסְדֶּךָ כְּרֹב רַחֲמֶיךָ מְחֵה פְשָׁעָי׃  
הֶרֶב כַּבְּסֵנִי מֵעֲוֹנִי וּמֵחַטָּאתִי טַהֲרֵנִי׃  
לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים וְרוּחַ נָכוֹן חַדֵּשׁ בְּקִרְבִּי׃

Wash away every unclean intention, every anxious grasping, every wrongful petition, and every word not fit for the Lord’s sight.  
Let this prayer be cleansed, corrected, and kept within mercy.  
Let protection stand around the going out and the coming in, and let all judgment rest with God.

Amen. Amen. Amen.
