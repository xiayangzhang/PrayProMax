## 满族萨满 / 关姓堂、博、查玛

Guwan hala-i tangse neyen.

Abka enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Abka-i jalan de,  
abka-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Na enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Na-i jalan de,  
na-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mafa,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i mafa,  
boo-i dolo tehe mafa,  
hala-i dolo tehe mafa,  
ere gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mama,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i mama,  
boo-i dolo tehe mama,  
hala-i dolo tehe mama,  
ere gisun be donji.

Tangse-i enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i weceku,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i tangse de,  
ere baita be alambi,  
ere jugūn be alambi,  
sain gisun be alambi.

Abka enduri de wesihun,

Donjiha be donjiha seme,  
aliha be aliha seme,  
jifi seme jihe,  
gene seme genehe.

Enduri gene,  
mafa mama gene,  
tangse-i hūsun tangse de tehe.  
Gisun wajire,  
neyen wajire.  
Aisin gisun akū,  
hulhun gisun akū,  
yargiyan gisun be alaha.

不冒师承，不越秘传；  
不作正仪，不动禁物；
