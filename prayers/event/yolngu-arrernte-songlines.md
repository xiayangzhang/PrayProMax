## 澳大利亚 Yolŋu / Arrernte 原住民仪式

With humble hearts, we honor Yolŋu, Arrernte, Anangu, and all First Nations custodians, Elders, Country, kinship, Law, rom, and Altyerre.

{{petition}}

May success come without taking another person’s place unjustly, without dispossessing others, and without harm to any living relation.

May Country, kinship, owners and managers, the living and the deceased, remain rightly respected in their places.

We close with quiet respect: no secret names, songs, places, images, or restricted ways are crossed; may we honor without overreach.
