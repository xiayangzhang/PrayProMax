净身口意：  
oṃ svabhāva śuddhāḥ sarva dharmāḥ svabhāva śuddho 'ham

namo buddhāya  
namo dharmāya  
namo saṃghāya

oṃ bodhicittaṃ utpādayāmi

namo mahāvairocanāya tathāgatāya
