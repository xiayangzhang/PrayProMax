## الكنيسة المارونية

B-shmo d-Abo w-d-Bro w-d-Ruho d-Qudsho, Had Aloho shariro. Amin.

Qadishat Aloho,  
Qadishat Hayelthono,  
Qadishat lo Moyutho,  
ethraham ‘alayn.

Shubho l-Abo w-la-Bro w-la-Ruho d-Qudsho,  
men ‘olam w-‘adamo l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Abun d-bashmayo,  
netqadash shmokh.  
Tite malkuthokh.  
Nehwe sebyonokh,  
aykano d-bashmayo oph bar‘o.  
Haw lan lahmo d-sunqonan yawmono.  
Washboq lan hawbayn wahtohayn,  
aykano doph hnan shbaqn l-hayyobayn.  
Wlo ta‘lan l-nesyuno,  
elo fasso lan men bisho.  
Metul d-dilokh hi malkutho w-haylo w-teshbuhto  
l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Shlom lekh Maryam,  
mlayat taybutho,  
Moran ‘amekh.  
Mbarakhto at b-neshe,  
w-mbarakh hu piro d-karsekh,  
Maran Yeshua.  
O qadishto Maryam,  
Yoldath Aloho,  
saloy hlofayn hnaye,  
hosho w-bsho‘at mawthan. Amin.

Moran Yeshua Mshiha,  
Bro d-Aloho hayo,  
qabel sluthan b-rahmayk.

{{petition}}

B-shfa‘otho d-Yoldath Aloho Maryam,  
w-d-Mor Maron,  
w-d-Mor Efram,  
w-d-Mor Ya‘qub,  
w-d-Mor Sharbel,  
w-d-kulhon qadisho,  
Moryo etraham ‘alayn.

Nhoor, Moryo, mad‘an b-nuhrokh,  
w-fthah saphwathan b-shaynokh.  
Haw lan meltho rakhikhto w-shariro,  
lebo nisho,  
ruho shayno,  
w-tar‘e d-taybutho,  
ayk sebyonokh qadisho.

Dkar, Moryo, l-‘abdayk w-l-amhotokh,  
kulhon hayye d-sabreen b-rahmayk,  
w-kulhon d-ba‘eyn pulhono keno w-lahmo d-sunqono.

Dkar, Moryo, l-‘anide mhaymne,  
w-a‘med enon b-nuhro d-afayk,  
b-rahmayk saggiye.

Moryo, nhoor lan b-nuhrokh,  
nattar lan b-slibokh,  
shadar ‘alayn rahmayk,  
w-ahlan b-shaynokh.  
Nehwe sebyonokh,  
w-la sebyonan;  
b-rahmayk netqabel,  
w-b-shaynokh neshtalem.

Lakh shubho,  
l-Abokh iqoro,  
w-l-Ruho d-Qudsho segedto,  
hosho w-bkul zban  
w-l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.
