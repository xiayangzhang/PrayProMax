## Spiritualists' National Union

In stillness and harmony, I open this prayer with sincerity, seeking peace of mind, clarity of spirit, and goodwill in the undertaking ahead.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, source of love, truth, and life,

We acknowledge the Seven Principles:

The Fatherhood of God.  
The Brotherhood of Man.  
The Communion of Spirits and the Ministry of Angels.  
The continuous existence of the human soul.  
Personal Responsibility.  
Compensation and Retribution hereafter for all the good and evil deeds done on earth.  
Eternal progress open to every human soul.

{{petition}}

May love surround all who take part.  
May truth guide every judgement.  
May healing, comfort, and strength be present.  
May all thought, word, and action be held in kindness, honesty, and service.

For all who are living, may there be encouragement and fair dealing.  
For those in the Spirit World, may loving remembrance be honoured in peace, without force or demand.  
Where uncertainty is present, may wisdom unfold.  
Where anxiety is present, may quiet strength arise.  
Where opportunity is present, may eternal progress be served.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit,  
let this prayer serve only love, truth, healing, consolation, goodwill, and spiritual growth.  
Let it make no claim of certainty, no bargain for results, and no power over another’s free will.  
May the outcome be received with courage, humility, and peace.

Amen.
