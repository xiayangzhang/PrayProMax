## 现代术士派（Cochranian、Sabbatic Craft）

In the name of the Old One,  
Master of the Crooked Path,  
Keeper of the hidden fire,  
I hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
let this ring be whole,  
let no false word enter,  
let no oath be broken,  
let no secret be profaned.

{{petition}}

No stolen oath is spoken here.  
No hidden word is claimed here.  
Only the open charm is set,  
only the rightful prayer is made.

Road before, road behind,  
gate before, gate behind;  
let the way be opened,  
let the appointed work be met,  
let the interview pass in right order  
under clear wit, steady breath, and truthful speech.

Fire in the head, light in the bone,  
the crooked road beneath the feet;  
let confusion fall as chaff,  
let fear be bridled,  
let fitting words rise cleanly,  
let the true sign be known.

Hand to hand, hearth to hearth,  
word to word, and eye to eye;  
let fair regard be given,  
let worthy skill be recognized,  
let the right door open,  
and the wrong door close without harm.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
the word is spoken,  
the work is sealed,  
the compass is whole.

Old One, receive thanks.  
If any word has strayed beyond its rightful road,  
let it be withdrawn into silence.  
If any desire has pressed too hard upon another’s will,  
let it be loosed and made harmless.  
Let all names uncalled remain untroubled.  
Let every boundary be restored.

Let the road be shut where it must be shut.  
Let the road be open where it must be open.  
Peace to the ring,  
peace to the dead,  
peace to the living.

So mote it be.
