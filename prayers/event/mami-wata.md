## Mami Wata 信仰

I stand by clean water, with cool heart and clean mouth.  
Mami Wata, Mammy Water, Nne mmiri, Yeye Omi,  
I call only your name for this prayer.

Mami Wata o, Mami Wata o,  
bia, nuru olu m.  
Nne mmiri, nne oma,  
mmiri di ọcha, mmiri ndu.

Mammy Water, I hail you.  
Fine lady for water, mother for river,  
she wey sabi deep water,  
she wey hold mirror for truth,  
she wey cool hot heart.

{{petition}}

I no ask to take another person blessing,  
I no ask to spoil another person road,  
I no ask to force love, favour, or choosing.  
Let only clean chance, fair regard, and good fit come near.

Yeye Omi, iya omi,  
bo mi pelu omi funfun.  
Omi tutu, omi alaafia,  
ko si ija, ko si iro,  
alaafia ni mo pe.

Mami Wata o, Mammy Water o,  
no vex, no trouble.  
If wrong word don rise,  
make wrong word fall for water.  
If fear don shake the heart,  
make cool water settle am.  
If pride or false promise wan stand,  
make only truth stand.

Mmiri oma, kuru ihe ojoo laa.  
Mmiri ndu, mee ka obi di jụụ.  
Nne mmiri, nara olu m,  
nara mmiri a,  
nara okwu udo a.

Mami Wata o,  
I no call another spirit for this same mouth.  
Only Mami Wata name dey here.  
Peace for water,  
peace for road,  
peace for the interview place,  
peace for the work wey may open.

Mammy Water, I thank you.  
Nne mmiri, daalu.  
Yeye Omi, mo dupe.  
With clean water, let any wrong calling be washed away;  
with cool water, let any improper asking be withdrawn;  
with peaceful water, let the mouth, heart, and road be reconciled.  
Alaafia. Udo. Peace.
