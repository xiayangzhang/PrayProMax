## 美洲原住民教会（NAC）

With humble hearts, we honor the Roadmen, the fire keepers, the drum, the families, and the old people who kept this road.

{{petition}}

Let its road be straight, its hands be honest, its helpers come in peace, and its benefit spread without taking another person’s blessing, life, or rightful share.

Where family-held songs and medicine ways belong, let us keep silence with respect; may the good prayer rise without crossing another’s road.

For the people now, the ones before us, and the ones coming after, we give thanks and ask for a clean mind, a steady heart, and a good dawn. Amen.
