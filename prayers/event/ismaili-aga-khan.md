## 伊斯玛仪派 / 阿迦汗系

**Niyyah / niyyah:**  
Allāhumma innī ajʿalu hādhā al-duʿāʾ li-wajhika al-karīm,  
wa aṭlubu raḥmataka wa hidāyataka fī amri muqābalati ṭalabi l-ʿamal,  
li-yakūna l-qalbu ṣāfiyan, wa l-lisānu ṭalqan, wa l-ʿaqlu munīran,  
wa li-l-jamāʿati kullihā.

**Basmalah:**  
Bismillāhi r-Raḥmāni r-Raḥīm.

**Al-Fātiḥah:**  
Al-ḥamdu lillāhi rabbi l-ʿālamīn.  
Ar-raḥmāni r-raḥīm.  
Māliki yawmi d-dīn.  
Iyyāka naʿbudu wa iyyāka nastaʿīn.  
Ihdinā ṣ-ṣirāṭa l-mustaqīm.  
Ṣirāṭa lladhīna anʿamta ʿalayhim, ghayri l-maghḍūbi ʿalayhim wa lā ḍ-ḍāllīn.  
Āmīn.

**Shahādah wa walāyah:**  
Ashhadu an lā ilāha illā Allāh.  
Ashhadu anna Muḥammadan Rasūlu llāh.  
Ashhadu anna Mawlānā ʿAlīyyan Amīru l-Muʾminīn ʿAlīyyu llāh.

**Ṣalawāt:**  
Allāhumma ṣalli ʿalā Muḥammadin wa āli Muḥammad.  
Allāhumma bārik ʿalā Muḥammadin wa āli Muḥammad.

**Duʿāʾ bi-smi l-Holy Du'a:**

**Tasbīḥ:**  
Yā Allāh.  
Yā Muḥammad.  
Yā ʿAlī.  
Yā Ḥāḍir Imām, madad.

**Ginan remembrance:**  
Eji satgur miliyā munē āj, ānand ānand thayō.  
Eji sāhebjī tū̃ morē man bhāvē.

**Duʿāʾ li-l-aḥyāʾ wa l-arwāḥ wa l-jamāʿah:**  
Allāhumma ghfir li-l-muʾminīna wa l-muʾmināt,  
wa rḥam arwāḥa l-marḥūmīn wa l-marḥūmāt,  
wa-jʿal nūra l-hidāyah fī qulūbinā,  
wa fī qulūbi ahli l-jamāʿah,  
wa fī jamāʿatinā kullihā.

Subḥānaka Allāhumma wa bi-ḥamdika,  
lā ilāha illā anta,  
nastaghfiruka wa natūbu ilayka.  
Allāhumma ṭahhir niyyātinā min al-kibr wa l-ijābār wa l-ḍarar,  
wa-jʿal hādhā d-duʿāʾa taqarruban ilayka wa shukran laka,  
lā tabdīlan li-qaḍāʾika illā bi-raḥmatika,  
wa lā ṭalaban illā min faḍlika.

Allāhumma taqabbal duʿāʾanā.  
Āmīn.  
Yā ʿAlī madad.
