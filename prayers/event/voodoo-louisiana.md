## 路易斯安那伏都 Voodoo

{{petition}}

Pater noster, qui es in caelis,  
sanctificetur nomen tuum.  
Adveniat regnum tuum.  
Fiat voluntas tua,  
sicut in caelo et in terra.  
Panem nostrum quotidianum da nobis hodie,  
et dimitte nobis debita nostra,  
sicut et nos dimittimus debitoribus nostris.  
Et ne nos inducas in tentationem,  
sed libera nos a malo.  
Amen.

שִׁיר לַמַּעֲלוֹת  
אֶשָּׂא עֵינַי אֶל־הֶהָרִים  
מֵאַיִן יָבֹא עֶזְרִי׃  
עֶזְרִי מֵעִם יְהוָה  
עֹשֵׂה שָׁמַיִם וָאָרֶץ׃  
אַל־יִתֵּן לַמּוֹט רַגְלֶךָ  
אַל־יָנוּם שֹׁמְרֶךָ׃  
הִנֵּה לֹא־יָנוּם וְלֹא יִישָׁן  
שׁוֹמֵר יִשְׂרָאֵל׃  
יְהוָה שֹׁמְרֶךָ  
יְהוָה צִלְּךָ עַל־יַד יְמִינֶךָ׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָרְךָ מִכָּל־רָע  
יִשְׁמֹר אֶת־נַפְשֶׁךָ׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָר־צֵאתְךָ וּבוֹאֶךָ  
מֵעַתָּה וְעַד־עוֹלָם׃

Clear Road Hand of Right Work, be sealed only for clean speech, calm breath, wise hearing, and a door that opens by mercy and honest fit.  
Let confusion fall from my mouth; let fear loosen from my hands; let my words stand straight, neither proud nor small.  
Let those who meet me see what is true, and let what is not mine pass by without bitterness.

Holy ancestors of my blood and my house, witness only what is clean in this prayer.  
Guard the road, steady the step, and turn away what is not rightfully mine to carry.  
If any word has been spoken beyond rightful measure, let it fall away and be forgiven.

Gloria Patri, et Filio, et Spiritui Sancto.  
Sicut erat in principio, et nunc, et semper,  
et in saecula saeculorum.  
Amen.

לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים  
וְרוּחַ נָכוֹן חַדֵּשׁ בְּקִרְבִּי׃

Bon Dieu, by mercy and not by bargain, cleanse this prayer of excess, release every unrightful promise, quiet every unfinished vow, and give peace to the ancestors and guardians who witness the clean road.  
This prayer is sealed for right employment, clear mind, and harmless success, under the mercy of Bon Dieu.  
Amen. Ashe.
