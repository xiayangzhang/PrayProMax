## Spiritualists' National Union

In stillness and harmony, I open this prayer with sincerity, peace, and goodwill.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, source of love, truth, and life,

We acknowledge the Seven Principles of Spiritualism:

The Fatherhood of God.  
The Brotherhood of Man.  
The Communion of Spirits and the Ministry of Angels.  
The continuous existence of the human soul.  
Personal Responsibility.  
Compensation and Retribution hereafter for all the good and evil deeds done on earth.  
Eternal progress open to every human soul.

{{petition}}

May healing influence, comfort, and strength be present according to the highest good.  
May all thought, word, and action be held in kindness, honesty, and personal responsibility.  
May guidance be received without coercion, protection sought without fear, and peace welcomed without any claim of certainty beyond truth.

For all who travel, all who wait, all who serve, and all who share the roads and ways of the world, may brotherhood, compassion, and mutual care arise.  
For those in the Spirit World, may love be honoured in peace, and may the continuous existence of the human soul be remembered with reverence.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit,  
let this prayer serve only love, truth, healing, harmony, and eternal progress.  
May no harm be wished upon any being, and may this intention rest in goodwill, humility, and the highest good of all.

Amen.
