## स्मार्त

oṃ apavitraḥ pavitro vā sarvāvasthāṃ gato 'pi vā |  
yaḥ smaret puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ sa bāhyābhyantaraḥ śuciḥ ||

oṃ keśavāya svāhā |  
oṃ nārāyaṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ mādhavāya svāhā ||

oṃ bhūḥ | oṃ bhuvaḥ | oṃ suvaḥ | oṃ mahaḥ | oṃ janaḥ | oṃ tapaḥ | oṃ satyam |  
oṃ tat savitur vareṇyaṃ bhargo devasya dhīmahi dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt |  
oṃ āpo jyotī raso 'mṛtaṃ brahma bhūr bhuvaḥ suvar oṃ ||

mama upātta-samasta-duritakṣayadvārā śrī-parameśvara-prītyarthaṃ  
yātrā-kṣema-saukarya-nirvighna-nirupadrava-siddhyarthaṃ,  
sarvāpada-vinivṛtti-pūrvakaṃ śubha-prāpti-siddhyarthaṃ,  
śrī-mahāgaṇapati-pūjāṃ kariṣye ||

oṃ gaṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ |  
vakratuṇḍa mahākāya sūryakoṭisamaprabha |  
nirvighnaṃ kuru me deva sarvakāryeṣu sarvadā ||

oṃ kalaśasya mukhe viṣṇuḥ kaṇṭhe rudraḥ samāśritaḥ |  
mūle tatra sthito brahmā madhye mātṛgaṇāḥ smṛtāḥ |  
kukṣau tu sāgarāḥ sarve sapta-dvīpā vasundharā |  
ṛgvedo 'tha yajurvedaḥ sāmavedo hy atharvaṇaḥ |  
aṅgaiś ca sahitāḥ sarve kalaśāmbu samāśritāḥ ||

oṃ gajānanaṃ bhūtagaṇādisevitaṃ  
kapittha-jambū-phalasāra-bhakṣitam |  
umāsutaṃ śokavināśakārakaṃ  
namāmi vighneśvara-pādapaṅkajam ||

oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, āvāhayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, āsanaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, pādyaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, arghyaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, ācamanīyaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, snānaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, vastraṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, yajñopavītaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, gandhaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, akṣatān samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, puṣpāṇi samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, dhūpam āghrāpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, dīpaṃ darśayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, naivedyaṃ nivedayāmi |  
oṃ prāṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ apānāya svāhā |  
oṃ vyānāya svāhā |  
oṃ udānāya svāhā |  
oṃ samānāya svāhā |  
oṃ brahmaṇe svāhā |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, tāmbūlaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ, nīrājanaṃ samarpayāmi ||

oṃ gaṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ ||  
oṃ namaste gaṇapataye |  
tvam eva pratyakṣaṃ tattvam asi |  
tvam eva kevalaṃ kartāsi |  
tvam eva kevalaṃ dhartāsi |  
tvam eva kevalaṃ hartāsi |  
tvam eva sarvaṃ khalv idaṃ brahmāsi |  
tvaṃ sākṣād ātmāsi nityam ||

oṃ śrī-mahāgaṇapataye namaḥ |  
namaste astu bhagavan śrī-mahāgaṇapate |  
anena pūjā-japa-stotra-pāṭhena yātrā-kṣema-siddhir astu ||

mantra-hīnaṃ kriyā-hīnaṃ bhakti-hīnaṃ gaṇādhipa |  
yat pūjitaṃ mayā deva paripūrṇaṃ tad astu me ||

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante 'harniśaṃ mayā |  
dāso 'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvara ||

yāntu devagaṇāḥ sarve pūjām ādāya māmakīm |  
iṣṭakāma-prasiddhyarthaṃ punarāgamanāya ca ||

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ sarve santu nirāmayāḥ |  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ||

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ||

sarvaṃ śrī-parameśvarārpaṇam astu ||
