## 现代术士派（Cochranian、Sabbatic Craft）

In the name of the Old One,  
Master of the Crooked Path,  
Keeper of the hidden fire,  
we hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
let this ring be whole;  
let no false word enter,  
let no oath be broken,  
let no secret be profaned.

{{petition}}

No stolen oath is spoken here.  
No hidden word is claimed here.  
Only the open charm is set,  
only the rightful prayer is made.

Thorn before, thorn behind,  
thorn at the left, thorn at the right;  
by blackthorn, whitethorn, and the crooked bough,  
let the ward stand fast around the road.

Road before, road behind,  
gate before, gate behind;  
let the way be opened where it is rightful,  
let the way be closed where harm would enter,  
let wheel, foot, wing, and vessel keep true course,  
let confusion be stilled,  
let accident pass by,  
let delay become guidance,  
let fear become clear sight,  
let the traveler be brought safely through.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
the word is spoken,  
the work is sealed,  
the compass is whole.

Old One, receive thanks.  
If any word has gone beyond its rightful bound,  
let it be withdrawn into silence.  
If any force has been stirred without need,  
let it be settled and sent in peace.  
Let the road be shut where it must be shut.  
Let the road be open where it must be open.  
Peace to the ring,  
peace to the dead,  
peace to the living.

So mote it be.
