## Hoodoo / Rootwork

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.

I stand before the Most High God with clean speech and a steady heart.  
Let this prayer be for safe and smooth travel, for a road without danger or hardship, for peril to be turned aside, and for a peaceful arrival.  
Let no falsehood, coercion, wrongful hand, or harm to the innocent be in it.

יֹשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃  
כִּי הוּא יַצִּילְךָ מִפַּח יָקוּשׁ מִדֶּבֶר הַוּוֹת׃  
בְּאֶבְרָתוֹ יָסֶךְ לָךְ וְתַחַת־כְּנָפָיו תֶּחְסֶה צִנָּה וְסֹחֵרָה אֲמִתּוֹ׃

He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.  
I will say of the LORD, He is my refuge and my fortress: my God; in him will I trust.  
Surely he shall deliver thee from the snare of the fowler, and from the noisome pestilence.  
He shall cover thee with his feathers, and under his wings shalt thou trust.

I speak no harm into the innocent.  
I bind no unwilling heart.  
I seek no person’s loss, no stolen blessing, no shortened life, and no gain by another’s ruin.  
I take back every crooked word, every proud demand, and every petition not fit to stand before God.  
Let every unjust obstacle be cut away without revenge, and let the road be opened in righteousness.

שִׁיר לַמַּעֲלוֹת אֶשָּׂא עֵינַי אֶל־הֶהָרִים מֵאַיִן יָבֹא עֶזְרִי׃  
עֶזְרִי מֵעִם יְהוָה עֹשֵׂה שָׁמַיִם וָאָרֶץ׃  
אַל־יִתֵּן לַמּוֹט רַגְלֶךָ אַל־יָנוּם שֹׁמְרֶךָ׃  
הִנֵּה לֹא־יָנוּם וְלֹא יִישָׁן שׁוֹמֵר יִשְׂרָאֵל׃  
יְהוָה שֹׁמְרֶךָ יְהוָה צִלְּךָ עַל־יַד יְמִינֶךָ׃  
יוֹמָם הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ לֹא־יַכֶּכָּה וְיָרֵחַ בַּלָּיְלָה׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָרְךָ מִכָּל־רָע יִשְׁמֹר אֶת־נַפְשֶׁךָ׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָר־צֵאתְךָ וּבוֹאֶךָ מֵעַתָּה וְעַד־עוֹלָם׃

I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from whence cometh my help.  
My help cometh from the LORD, which made heaven and earth.  
He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: he that keepeth thee will not slumber.  
The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in from this time forth, and even for evermore.

Set this petition under the judgment of the Most High.  
What is righteous, establish it.  
What is crooked, remove it.  
What is not mine to command, return it to God.  
Let mercy stand at the crossing, wisdom at the turning, protection at the door, and peace at the arrival.

חָנֵּנִי אֱלֹהִים כְּחַסְדֶּךָ כְּרֹב רַחֲמֶיךָ מְחֵה פְשָׁעָי׃  
הֶרֶב כַּבְּסֵנִי מֵעֲוֹנִי וּמֵחַטָּאתִי טַהֲרֵנִי׃  
לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים וְרוּחַ נָכוֹן חַדֵּשׁ בְּקִרְבִּי׃

Have mercy upon me, O God, according to thy lovingkindness.  
Wash me throughly from mine iniquity, and cleanse me from my sin.  
Create in me a clean heart, O God; and renew a right spirit within me.  
By mercy, let every unclean intention be washed away; by righteousness, let this prayer stand only as protection, clear road, and trust in God.

Amen. Amen. Amen.
