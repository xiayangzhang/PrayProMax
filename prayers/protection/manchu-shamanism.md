## 满族萨满 / 关姓堂、博、查玛

Guwan hala-i tangse neyen.

Abka enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Abka-i jalan de,  
abka-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Na enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Na-i jalan de,  
na-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mafa,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i mafa,  
boo-i dolo tehe mafa,  
hala-i dolo tehe mafa,  
gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mama,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i mama,  
boo-i dolo tehe mama,  
hala-i dolo tehe mama,  
gisun be donji.

Tangse-i enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i weceku,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i tangse de,  
genehe jugūn be alambi,  
elhe be alambi,  
sain be alambi.

Neyen be jafambi,  
gisun be tucibumbi,  
enduri de alambi,  
mafa mama de alambi,  
tangse de alambi.

Abka enduri,  
genehe jugūn be donji.  
Na enduri,  
genehe jugūn be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mafa,  
genehe jugūn be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mama,  
genehe jugūn be donji.  
Tangse-i enduri,  
genehe jugūn be donji.

Mafa mama,  
gisun be donji,  
sain be dosimbu,  
ehe be tucibu,  
faksi be waliyara,  
jobolon be waliyara.

Tangse-i enduri,  
weile be acabumbi,  
gisun be acabumbi,  
elhe genehe, elhe isinaha be acabumbi.

Abka enduri de wesihun,  
na enduri de wesihun,  
Guwan hala-i mafa de wesihun,  
Guwan hala-i mama de wesihun,  
tangse-i enduri de wesihun.

Donjiha be donjiha seme,  
aliha be aliha seme,  
jifi seme jihe,  
gene seme genehe.

Enduri gene,  
mafa mama gene,  
tangse-i hūsun tangse de tehe.  
Gisun wajire,  
neyen wajire.  
Aisin gisun akū,  
hulhun gisun akū,  
yargiyan gisun be alaha.
