## Shembe

Nkulunkulu kaShembe, Baba wethu,  
siyakhuleka phambi kwakho.

Siyakudumisa, Jehova;  
siyakubonga, Nkosi;  
igama lakho malibongwe.  
Wena unguMalusi wethu; asiyikweswela,  
usihola ezindleleni zokuthula, usivikele ebumnyameni nasengozini.

{{petition}}

Uma kukhona isono, ukungcola, ukungathembeki,  
noma ukona okwaziwayo nokungaziwayo,  
siyacela intethelelo, ukuthula,  
nokubuyiselwa endleleni yakho.

Baba wethu osezulwini,  
malingcweliswe igama lakho;  
mawufike umbuso wakho;  
mayenziwe intando yakho emhlabeni njengasezulwini.  
Usiphe namuhla isinkwa sethu semihla ngemihla;  
usithethelele amacala ethu,  
njengokuba nathi sibathethelela abanamacala kithi;  
ungasingenisi ekulingweni,  
kodwa usikhulule kokubi.  
Ngokuba umbuso ungowakho, namandla, nenkazimulo, kuze kube phakade.  
Amen.

Nkosi, bheka indlela yalolu hambo.  
Vala iminyango yengozi, uvule indlela yokuphepha.  
Gcina izinyawo, gcina amehlo, gcina izinhliziyo,  
gcina imoto, umgwaqo, nomoya walabo abahambayo.  
Uma kuvela ubunzima, bususe ngobumnene bakho;  
uma kuvela ukwesaba, buyise ukuthula kwakho.  
Asiceli ukulimala komuntu;  
sicela kuphela ukuvikelwa, ukuhlakanipha, nokufika ngokuthula.

Ngokubeka izandla komthandazo webandla,  
Nkosi, qinisa abahambayo, ubahlanze, ubavikele,  
ubanike ukuthula emzimbeni, enhliziyweni, nasemoyeni.  
Makube njalo ngokwentando yakho, hhayi ngokuzigqaja kwethu,  
kodwa ngokwethemba kuwe.

Siyakubonga, Nkosi.  
Siyakudumisa, Jehova.  
Ukuthula makuhambe ngaphambili,  
ukuthula makube phakathi nendlela,  
ukuthula makube lapho kufikwa khona.

Igama lakho malibongwe.  
Konke makwenziwe ngokuthobeka, ngokuhlanzeka kwenhliziyo,  
nangokuhlonipha umkhuleko webandla.  
Amen.
