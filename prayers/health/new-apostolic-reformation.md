## 新使徒改革

{{petition}}

Πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς·  
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου·  
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου·  
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου,  
ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς·  
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον δὸς ἡμῖν σήμερον·  
καὶ ἄφες ἡμῖν τὰ ὀφειλήματα ἡμῶν,  
ὡς καὶ ἡμεῖς ἀφήκαμεν τοῖς ὀφειλέταις ἡμῶν·  
καὶ μὴ εἰσενέγκῃς ἡμᾶς εἰς πειρασμόν,  
ἀλλὰ ῥῦσαι ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τοῦ πονηροῦ.

Father, I confess and renounce every sin, fear, bitterness, accusation, unbelief, despair, idolatry, occult involvement, ungodly vow, inherited pattern, false word, and agreement with sickness, torment, or hopelessness. I place my body, soul, and spirit under the blood and lordship of Jesus Christ.

I forgive those who have harmed, neglected, burdened, or spoken falsely against me, and I release them from my judgment. I do not curse them, control them, or claim vengeance over them. I bless what You bless, and I reject only the works of darkness, lies, bondage, sin, infirmity, and oppression.

הַיֹּשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן  
בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי  
אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃

Ἰδοὺ δέδωκα ὑμῖν τὴν ἐξουσίαν  
τοῦ πατεῖν ἐπάνω ὄφεων καὶ σκορπίων,  
καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν δύναμιν τοῦ ἐχθροῦ,  
καὶ οὐδὲν ὑμᾶς οὐ μὴ ἀδικήσει.

ὃ ἐὰν δήσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται δεδεμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς,  
καὶ ὃ ἐὰν λύσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται λελυμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς.

Ὑποτάγητε οὖν τῷ θεῷ·  
ἀντίστητε δὲ τῷ διαβόλῳ,  
καὶ φεύξεται ἀφ᾽ ὑμῶν.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I bind every demonic assignment, accusation, covenant of darkness, curse, word of death, spirit of fear, spirit of infirmity, spirit of heaviness, spirit of confusion, and every power of the enemy operating through sickness, pain, weakness, or despair. I command every unclean influence attached to disease, affliction, torment, and bondage to leave and not return, according to the authority of Jesus Christ.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I loose truth, repentance, faith, holiness, peace, protection, discernment, healing, endurance, wisdom, and obedience to the will of God. Let the life of Christ strengthen the body, calm the mind, cleanse the heart, and restore what has been weakened. Let pain be driven out, health be established, and days be ordered in peace before You.

I cut off every ungodly soul tie, false burden, occult claim, generational bondage, spoken curse, inner vow, fear agreement, and demonic access point connected with sickness, suffering, or premature destruction. I declare that the handwriting against me is canceled in Christ and that every hostile power is disarmed under the cross of Jesus Christ.

I ask for long life and peaceful days in Your mercy, not by taking life, blessing, health, or provision from another. I do not seek to shorten another’s days, dispossess another’s portion, or gain healing through harm. Let Your blessing come in righteousness, compassion, wisdom, and love.

Καὶ αὐτοὶ ἐνίκησαν αὐτὸν  
διὰ τὸ αἷμα τοῦ ἀρνίου  
καὶ διὰ τὸν λόγον τῆς μαρτυρίας αὐτῶν.

Father, fill every cleansed place with the Holy Spirit, the peace of Christ, the fear of the Lord, wisdom, purity, steadfast love, and bodily strength. Establish Your kingdom order in every thought, breath, organ, system, and step. Guide all care, counsel, rest, nourishment, and healing means in truth and wisdom.

Father, keep this prayer under the lordship of Jesus Christ and in the fruit of the Holy Spirit. Let no word become fear, control, accusation, or harm toward any person. Where there is fear, lead me into trust; where there is bitterness, into forgiveness; where there is striving, into surrender; where there is confusion or crisis, into wise shepherding, safe help, and peace. I reject only sin, lies, bondage, evil oppression, and the works of darkness, and I receive Your mercy, healing, and holy love.

I thank You, Father, and seal this prayer in the name of Jesus Christ.

Ἀμήν.
