## 毗湿奴派（Gaudiya / ISKCON 等）

oṁ ajñāna-timirāndhasya jñānāñjana-śalākayā  
cakṣur unmīlitaṁ yena tasmai śrī-gurave namaḥ

nama oṁ viṣṇu-pādāya kṛṣṇa-preṣṭhāya bhū-tale  
śrīmate bhaktivedānta-svāmin iti nāmine

vande śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya-nityānandau sahoditau  
gauḍodaye puṣpavantau citrau śandau tamo-nudau

śrī-kṛṣṇa-caitanya prabhu-nityānanda  
śrī-advaita gadādhara śrīvāsādi-gaura-bhakta-vṛnda

he śrī guru, he śrī gaura, he śrī śrī rādhā-kṛṣṇa,  
{{petition}}

Hare Kṛṣṇa Hare Kṛṣṇa  
Kṛṣṇa Kṛṣṇa Hare Hare  
Hare Rāma Hare Rāma  
Rāma Rāma Hare Hare

namas te narasiṁhāya prahlādāhlāda-dāyine  
hiraṇyakaśipor vakṣaḥ-śilā-ṭaṅka-nakhālaye

ito nṛsiṁhaḥ parato nṛsiṁho  
yato yato yāmi tato nṛsiṁhaḥ  
bahir nṛsiṁho hṛdaye nṛsiṁho  
nṛsiṁham ādiṁ śaraṇaṁ prapadye

愿身心诸苦得以止息，病障远离，寿命安稳，饮食清净，心念归正；愿此康健用于听闻、念诵、服务与行善，非为放逸、贪著或轻慢圣名。

Hare Kṛṣṇa Hare Kṛṣṇa  
Kṛṣṇa Kṛṣṇa Hare Hare  
Hare Rāma Hare Rāma  
Rāma Rāma Hare Hare

tṛṇād api sunīcena taror iva sahiṣṇunā  
amāninā mānadena kīrtanīyaḥ sadā hariḥ

na dhanaṁ na janaṁ na sundarīṁ  
kavitāṁ vā jagad-īśa kāmaye  
mama janmani janmanīśvare  
bhavatād bhaktir ahaitukī tvayi

idaṁ kīrtanaṁ śrī-kṛṣṇārpaṇam astu  
sarveṣāṁ maṅgalaṁ bhavatu  
vaiṣṇava-aparādha-nāma-aparādha-kṣamā-prārthanāṁ karomi  
Hare Kṛṣṇa
