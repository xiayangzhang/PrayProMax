## 威卡 Dianic

By earth and moon, by breath and flame,  
this place is made sacred.

I cast this circle as a boundary of truth,  
a circle of protection,  
a circle of blessing,  
a circle of the Goddess.

O Diana,  
Bright Lady of the Moon,  
Mother of the wild places,  
Keeper of women’s mysteries,  
be present in this circle.

{{petition}}

Let this prayer touch only what may be touched in freedom.  
Let it bind no unwilling heart.  
Let it cross no oath-bound door.  
Let it harm none.

O Diana,  
look upon the body with gentleness,  
upon the breath with calm,  
upon the blood, bones, nerves, and heart with blessing.

Where there is pain, let there be easing.  
Where there is weakness, let there be renewal.  
Where there is fever, let there be cooling.  
Where there is fear, let there be clear boundary.  
Where there is depletion, let there be restoration.  
Where there is illness, let healing paths open in wisdom.

All acts of love and pleasure are my rituals.

I place within this circle  
gratitude, clean breath, quiet strength,  
and the will to live in balance.

By the waxing, full, and waning Moon,  
by the dark and returning Moon,  
may what is true be strengthened,  
may what is harmful be dissolved,  
may vitality return in right measure,  
may pain depart in peace.

For health, long life, and peaceful well-being,  
I ask blessing, clarity, and right unfolding.  
May this healing seek no life-force stolen from another,  
no years taken from another,  
no blessing gained by another’s loss.

An it harm none, do what ye will.

O Diana,  
I thank you for your presence.  
Remain if you will,  
depart if you must,  
with blessing between us.

Let all that has been raised here be grounded in the earth,  
cleansed by moonlight,  
held within free will,  
sealed against coercion, oath-breaking, and harm.  
May only blessing remain.

The circle is open,  
but unbroken.  
Merry meet,  
merry part,  
and merry meet again.
