## Aurum Solis

In stillness and in purity I stand within the Light of the Ogdoadic Star, gathered to the solar heart and to the order of the Divine Mind.

Agios ho Theos,  
Agios Ischyros,  
Agios Athanatos,  
eleēson hēmas.

Deo Duce, Comite Ferro.

Let the work be established in harmony, concerning bodily health, release from illness and pain, and the blessing of peaceful longevity.

IAO.  
AGATHOS DAIMŌN.  
IAO.

AGATHOS DAIMŌN.  
IAO.

The word is spoken, the measure is sealed, and the force is returned to its proper sphere.

If any motion of will has gone beyond right measure, I withdraw it now into silence.  
If any force has been stirred without purity, let it be cleansed in the deathless Light.  
All powers invoked are released to their own place, in peace and in order.  
Let the wards stand clear, gentle, and complete.
