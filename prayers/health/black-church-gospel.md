## 黑人教会传统

Father God, in the name of Jesus, we come before You.

Leader:  
Lord, we thank You for waking us up, clothing us in our right mind, and keeping us by Your mercy.  
You have been our help in ages past, our shelter in the storm, and our strength when the body grows weak.

People:  
Amen. Thank You, Lord.

Leader:  
We come not in our own strength, but leaning on Your everlasting arms.  
Search us, Lord; forgive us, cleanse us, and make our hearts right before You.  
Where we have been fearful, give us faith.  
Where we have been weary, give us rest.  
Where we have forgotten Your goodness, bring us back to praise.

People:  
Lord, have mercy.

Leader:  
{{petition}}

Scripture:  
Is any sick among you? let him call for the elders of the church;  
and let them pray over him, anointing him with oil in the name of the Lord:  
And the prayer of faith shall save the sick, and the Lord shall raise him up;  
and if he have committed sins, they shall be forgiven him.  
Confess your faults one to another, and pray one for another, that ye may be healed.  
The effectual fervent prayer of a righteous man availeth much.

People:  
Amen.

Spiritual:  
There is a balm in Gilead,  
To make the wounded whole;  
There is a balm in Gilead,  
To heal the sin-sick soul.

Leader:  
Lord, where there is sickness, speak mercy.  
Where there is pain, bring comfort.  
Where there is weakness, restore strength.  
Where there is anxiety, breathe peace.  
Where there is inflammation, infection, injury, weariness, or hidden trouble, let wisdom, care, and healing be near.  
Guide the hands of caregivers, steady the minds of doctors and nurses, and bless every medicine, treatment, rest, and daily habit that serves life.

Let the body be guarded, the heart be strengthened, the mind be renewed, and the spirit be lifted.  
Let health rise with humility, joy return with gratitude, and peace settle deep in the soul.

Scripture:  
The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want.  
He maketh me to lie down in green pastures:  
he leadeth me beside the still waters.  
He restoreth my soul:  
he leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name’s sake.  
Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death,  
I will fear no evil: for thou art with me;  
thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.

People:  
Yes, Lord.

Leader:  
We remember the sick, the elderly, the tired, the lonely, the caregivers, the children, and all who carry pain in silence.  
Keep the living, strengthen the weary, comfort the fearful, and uphold every household that needs Your mercy.  
Let Your Spirit intercede with groanings which cannot be uttered, and let Your love hold us fast.

Scripture:  
He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High  
shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.  
I will say of the LORD, He is my refuge and my fortress:  
my God; in him will I trust.

People:  
Amen.

Leader:  
Now, Lord, teach us to pray:

All:  
Our Father which art in heaven,  
Hallowed be thy name.  
Thy kingdom come.  
Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.  
Give us this day our daily bread.  
And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.  
And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil:  
For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever.  
Amen.

Leader:  
We seal this prayer in faith, not as a bargain, not by might, nor by power, but by Your Spirit.  
Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, keep us humble, keep us grateful, keep us in love, and keep us rooted in Your Word.  
Let every praise be holy, every cry be sincere, every healing be received with thanksgiving, and every life be held under Your mercy and holy will.  
In Jesus’ name.

People:  
Amen. Amen. Amen.
