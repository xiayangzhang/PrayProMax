## 摩门教（LDS）

Our Father in Heaven,

We thank Thee for the gift of life, for the body Thou hast given, for family, care, and every measure of strength and comfort that comes from Thee.

We acknowledge Thy Son, Jesus Christ, our Savior and Redeemer, and we seek the guidance, peace, and comfort of the Holy Ghost.

{{petition}}

We do not seek to take health, blessing, life, or peace from any other soul, but ask only for Thy merciful help, in harmony with Thy justice, love, and holy will.

May we remember the Savior, keep His commandments, and have His Spirit to be with us. May all sacred things be treated with reverence, and may every blessing come through Thy mercy and the grace of Jesus Christ.

We say these things in the name of Jesus Christ, amen.
