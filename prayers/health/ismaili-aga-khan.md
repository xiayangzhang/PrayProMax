## 伊斯玛仪派 / 阿迦汗系

**Niyyah:**  
Allāhumma innī ajʿalu hādhā al-duʿāʾ li-wajhika al-karīm,  
wa aṭlubu raḥmataka wa hidāyataka fī ṭalabi ṣ-ṣiḥḥati wa l-ʿāfiyah,  
wa dafʿi l-maraḍi wa l-alami, wa ṭūli l-ʿumri fī ṭāʿatika,  
lā nabtaghī bihi ḍararan li-aḥadin, wa lā naqṣan min rizqi aḥadin aw ʿumrih.  

**Basmalah:**  
Bismillāhi r-Raḥmāni r-Raḥīm.  

**Al-Fātiḥah:**  
Al-ḥamdu lillāhi rabbi l-ʿālamīn.  
Ar-raḥmāni r-raḥīm.  
Māliki yawmi d-dīn.  
Iyyāka naʿbudu wa iyyāka nastaʿīn.  
Ihdinā ṣ-ṣirāṭa l-mustaqīm.  
Ṣirāṭa lladhīna anʿamta ʿalayhim, ghayri l-maghḍūbi ʿalayhim wa lā ḍ-ḍāllīn.  
Āmīn.  

**Shahādah wa walāyah:**  
Ashhadu an lā ilāha illā Allāh.  
Ashhadu anna Muḥammadan Rasūlu llāh.  
Ashhadu anna Mawlānā ʿAlīyyan Amīru l-Muʾminīn ʿAlīyyu llāh.  

**Ṣalawāt:**  
Allāhumma ṣalli ʿalā Muḥammadin wa āli Muḥammad.  
Allāhumma bārik ʿalā Muḥammadin wa āli Muḥammad.

**Āyat an-Nūr:**  
Allāhu nūru s-samāwāti wa l-arḍ.  
Mathalu nūrihī ka-mishkātin fīhā miṣbāḥ.  
Al-miṣbāḥu fī zujājah.  
Az-zujājatu ka-annahā kawkabun durrīyyun  
yūqadu min shajaratin mubārakatin zaytūnatin  
lā sharqīyyatin wa lā gharbīyyah,  
yakādu zaytuhā yuḍīʾu wa law lam tamsashu nār.  
Nūrun ʿalā nūr.  
Yahdī llāhu li-nūrihī man yashāʾ.  
Wa yaḍribu llāhu l-amthāla li-n-nās.  
Wa llāhu bi-kulli shayʾin ʿalīm.  

**Tasbīḥ:**  
Yā Allāh.  
Yā Muḥammad.  
Yā ʿAlī.  
Yā Ḥāḍir Imām, madad bi-idhnillāh fī ṭarīqi l-hidāyah.  

**Ginan remembrance:**  
Eji satgur miliyā munē āj, ānand ānand thayō.  
Eji sāhebjī tū̃ morē man bhāvē.  

**Duʿāʾ for the living and the departed:**  
Allāhumma ghfir li-l-muʾminīna wa l-muʾmināt,  
wa rḥam arwāḥa l-marḥūmīn wa l-marḥūmāt,  
wa-jʿal nūra l-hidāyah fī qulūbi jamāʿatinā kullihā.  

Allāhumma ijʿal hādhā d-duʿāʾa dhikran wa shukran,  
lā siḥran wa lā ikrāhan wa lā ḍararan,  
wa ṣaffi niyyatanā li-wajhika l-karīm,  
wa-jʿalnā min ahl al-raḥmah wa l-ʿāfiyah wa l-ʿamal aṣ-ṣāliḥ.  

Allāhumma taqabbal duʿāʾanā.  
Āmīn.  
Ya ʿAlī madad.
