## 威卡 Alexandrian

I stand within the Circle, in perfect love and perfect trust.  
Let all within this rite be bounded by the Rede:  
An it harm none, do what ye will.

By Air, by Fire, by Water, by Earth,  
this Circle is cast and sealed.  
Between the worlds, and in the presence of the Mighty Ones,  
let this rite be held for health, healing, long life, and peace of body.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

O Great Mother,  
Lady of the Moon,  
source of mercy, renewal, and living grace,  
receive this prayer for soundness of body, steadiness of breath,  
freedom from illness, and a life blessed with peace and length of days.

O Horned God,  
Lord of the Greenwood,  
guardian of life, death, and return,  
receive this prayer for strength restored, pain eased,  
vitality renewed, and courage kept through every season.

Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak,  
Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak.

If any word has strayed beyond right will,  
let it be stilled, cleansed, and released.  
If any force is not in perfect love and perfect trust,  
let it depart in peace and do no harm.

The Circle is open, but unbroken.  
Merry meet, merry part, and merry meet again.  
An it harm none, do what ye will.  
Blessed be.
