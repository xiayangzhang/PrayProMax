## 路易斯安那伏都 Voodoo

Bon Dieu, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit,  
{{petition}}  
Let this hand be named **Hand of Clean Health**, and let its prayer be bound only to healing, protection, and lawful good.  
I ask for long life without taking life from another; I seek blessing without dispossessing another; I ask relief without sending pain to any soul.

Pater noster, qui es in caelis,  
sanctificetur nomen tuum.  
Adveniat regnum tuum.  
Fiat voluntas tua,  
sicut in caelo et in terra.  
Panem nostrum quotidianum da nobis hodie,  
et dimitte nobis debita nostra,  
sicut et nos dimittimus debitoribus nostris.  
Et ne nos inducas in tentationem,  
sed libera nos a malo.  
Amen.

מִזְמוֹר לְדָוִד  
יְהוָה רֹעִי לֹא אֶחְסָר׃  
בִּנְאוֹת דֶּשֶׁא יַרְבִּיצֵנִי  
עַל־מֵי מְנֻחוֹת יְנַהֲלֵנִי׃  
נַפְשִׁי יְשׁוֹבֵב  
יַנְחֵנִי בְמַעְגְּלֵי־צֶדֶק  
לְמַעַן שְׁמוֹ׃  
גַּם כִּי־אֵלֵךְ בְּגֵיא צַלְמָוֶת  
לֹא־אִירָא רָע  
כִּי־אַתָּה עִמָּדִי  
שִׁבְטְךָ וּמִשְׁעַנְתֶּךָ  
הֵמָּה יְנַחֲמֻנִי׃  
אַךְ טוֹב וָחֶסֶד יִרְדְּפוּנִי  
כָּל־יְמֵי חַיָּי  
וְשַׁבְתִּי בְּבֵית־יְהוָה  
לְאֹרֶךְ יָמִים׃

Let this hand be closed in clean intention:  
for breath steady, blood quiet, bones firm, sleep restored, and fear set down.  
Let no unlawful binding be tied here, no secret name be taken, no grave be troubled, no spirit be confused.  
What belongs to sickness, let it pass away by mercy; what belongs to strength, let it return by grace.

Holy ancestors of my blood and my house, witness only what is clean in this prayer.  
Guard the road of health; cool the body, steady the heart, and keep the feet on righteous ground.  
Guarding spirits on the good road, stand only where Bon Dieu permits, and carry no harm from this work.

Gloria Patri, et Filio, et Spiritui Sancto.  
Sicut erat in principio, et nunc, et semper,  
et in saecula saeculorum.  
Amen.

לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים  
וְרוּחַ נָכוֹן חַדֵּשׁ בְּקִרְבִּי׃

If any word has gone beyond rightful measure, let it fall away and be forgiven.  
If any vow was spoken in ignorance, let it be released without debt, without fear, and without harm.  
Ancestors, be cooled; spirits, be thanked; the road be swept clean.  
This prayer is sealed for health, peace, and mercy under Bon Dieu.  
Amen. Ashe.
