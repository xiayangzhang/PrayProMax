## 现代术士派（Cochranian、Sabbatic Craft）

In the name of the Old One,  
Master of the Crooked Path,  
Keeper of the hidden fire,  
we hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
let this ring be whole;  
let no false word enter;  
let no oath be broken;  
let no secret be profaned.

{{petition}}

No stolen oath is spoken here.  
No hidden word is claimed here.  
Only the open charm is set,  
only the rightful prayer is made.

Bone to bone, blood to blood,  
breath to breath, and flesh to flesh;  
that which is torn be mended,  
that which is fevered be cooled,  
that which is weakened be strengthened,  
that which is burdened be eased.

Marrow be bright,  
heart be steady,  
breath be clear,  
sleep be gentle,  
step be firm upon the road.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
the word is spoken,  
the work is sealed,  
the compass is whole.

Old One, receive thanks.  
What has been rightly asked, let it stand;  
what has gone beyond right measure, let it fall away.  
Let every unruly thread be withdrawn,  
let every boundary return to its proper place.  
Let the road be shut where it must be shut.  
Let the road be open where it must be open.  
Peace to the ring,  
peace to the dead,  
peace to the living.

So mote it be.
