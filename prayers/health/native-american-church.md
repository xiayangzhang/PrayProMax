## 美洲原住民教会（NAC）

With humble hearts, we honor the Roadmen, Firemen, Drummers, families, and Native Nations who keep this sacred road.

{{petition}}

Let healing come in a clean way, without taking life, blessing, or breath from any other person.

Help the body rest, the mind become clear, the family stand together, and the road ahead be steady.

May we walk with repentance, forgiveness, sobriety, and respect, not reaching beyond what has been given.

For the people now living, for the ancestors, and for the children yet to come, we give thanks. Amen.
