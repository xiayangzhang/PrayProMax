## الكنيسة المارونية

B-shmo d-Abo w-d-Bro w-d-Ruho d-Qudsho, Had Aloho shariro. Amin.

Qadishat Aloho,  
Qadishat Hayelthono,  
Qadishat lo Moyutho,  
ethraham ‘alayn.

Shubho l-Abo w-la-Bro w-la-Ruho d-Qudsho,  
men ‘olam w-‘adamo l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Abun d-bashmayo,  
netqadash shmokh.  
Tite malkuthokh.  
Nehwe sebyonokh,  
aykano d-bashmayo oph bar‘o.  
Haw lan lahmo d-sunqonan yawmono.  
Washboq lan hawbayn wahtohayn,  
aykano doph hnan shbaqn l-hayyobayn.  
Wlo ta‘lan l-nesyuno,  
elo fasso lan men bisho.  
Metul d-dilokh hi malkutho w-haylo w-teshbuhto  
l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Shlom lekh Maryam,  
mlayat taybutho,  
Moran ‘amekh.  
Mbarakhto at b-neshe,  
w-mbarakh hu piro d-karsekh,  
Maran Yeshua.  
O qadishto Maryam,  
Yoldath Aloho,  
saloy hlofayn hnaye,  
hosho w-bsho‘at mawthan. Amin.

Moran Yeshua Mshiha,  
Asoyo d-nafshe w-d-fagre,  
Bro d-Aloho hayo,  
qabel sluthan b-rahmayk.  
{{petition}}

B-shfa‘otho d-Yoldath Aloho Maryam,  
w-d-Mor Maron,  
w-d-Mor Sharbel,  
w-d-Mor Efram,  
w-d-Mor Ya‘qub,  
w-d-kulhon qadisho,  
Moryo etraham ‘alayn.

Moryo, dkar l-‘abdayk w-l-amhotok,  
l-kulhon haye d-snon la-‘utho,  
l-mri‘e, l-khire, l-mishtaqle b-ke’be,  
w-l-aylen d-ba‘en menokh hulyono w-shayno.  
Afgah ‘layhon nuhrokh,  
qareb lhon rahmayk,  
w-hawi lhon mahse w-bet gawso.

Lo b-husrono d-nosh,  
w-lo b-qutoyo d-hayye d-akhrene,  
ella b-sebyonokh qadisho,  
b-rahme, b-shayno, w-b-taybutho,  
nehwe lan hulmono, kurhono nitraheq,  
w-hayye nethadthon b-shubhokh.

Dkar, Moryo, l-haye,  
w-ahlan b-shayno d-Mshiha.  
Dkar, Moryo, l-‘anide mhaymne,  
w-nahhar lhon b-nuhrokh d-lo ‘areb.

Moryo, nhoor lan b-nuhrokh,  
nattar lan b-slibokh,  
shadar ‘alayn rahmayk,  
w-ahlan b-shaynokh.

Lakh shubho,  
l-Abokh iqoro,  
w-l-Ruho d-Qudsho segedto,  
hosho w-bkul zban  
w-l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.
