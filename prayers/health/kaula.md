## कौल

[गुरु-वन्दना / guru-vandana]

oṃ śrī-gurubhyo namaḥ ।  
oṃ gurave namaḥ ।  
gurur brahmā gurur viṣṇuḥ gurur devo maheśvaraḥ ।  
guruḥ sākṣāt parabrahma tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ ॥

[गणपति-वन्दना / gaṇapati-vandana]

oṃ gaṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ ।  
vakratuṇḍa mahākāya sūryakoṭisamaprabha ।  
nirvighnaṃ kuru me deva sarvakāryeṣu sarvadā ॥

[सङ्कल्प / saṅkalpa]

mama upātta-samasta-durita-kṣaya-dvāra  
śrī-parādevī-prītyarthaṃ  
ārogya-dīrghāyuḥ-śānti-puṣṭi-siddhyarthaṃ  
roga-duḥkha-vyādhi-nivṛttyarthaṃ  
sukha-kṣema-bala-prāṇa-prasāda-siddhyarthaṃ  
idaṃ prārthanā-japaṃ kariṣye ॥

na parasya puṇyaṃ harāmi ।  
na parasya āyuḥ kṣapayāmi ।  
sarveṣāṃ hitāya śāntyai ca idaṃ samarpayāmi ॥

[कुल-देवी-आवाहन / kula-devī-āvāhana]

oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ parādevyai namaḥ ।  
oṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ kulāyai namaḥ ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ śrī-mātṛkāyai namaḥ ॥

[मन्त्र-जप / mantra-japa]

oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ parādevyai namaḥ ।  
oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ parādevyai namaḥ ।  
oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ parādevyai namaḥ ॥

tryambakaṃ yajāmahe sugandhiṃ puṣṭivardhanam ।  
urvārukam iva bandhanān mṛtyor mukṣīya mā'mṛtāt ॥

[शान्ति / śānti]

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ sarve santu nirāmayāḥ ।  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ॥

oṃ dyauḥ śāntir antarīkṣaṃ śāntiḥ ।  
pṛthivī śāntir āpaḥ śāntir oṣadhayaḥ śāntiḥ ।  
vanaspatayaḥ śāntir viśve devāḥ śāntir brahma śāntiḥ ।  
sarvaṃ śāntiḥ śāntir eva śāntiḥ ।  
sā mā śāntir edhi ॥

[समर्पण / samarpaṇa]

kāyena vācā manasendriyair vā  
buddhyātmanā vā prakṛteḥ svabhāvāt ।  
karomi yad yat sakalaṃ parasmai  
śrī-parādevyai samarpaṇam astu ॥

[क्षमा / kṣamā]

yat kṛtaṃ tu mayā devi mantra-hīnaṃ kriyā-hīnam ।  
tat sarvaṃ pūrṇatāṃ yātu tvat-prasādāt parāmbike ॥

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante 'har-niśaṃ mayā ।  
dāso 'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvari ॥

[फल-समर्पण / phala-samarpaṇa]

anena prārthanā-japena  
śāntiḥ astu ।  
rakṣā astu ।  
pāśa-chedaḥ śubhaḥ astu ।  
prāyaścitta-śuddhiḥ astu ।  
sarvabhūta-hitaṃ astu ।  
sarve santu nirāmayāḥ ॥

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ॥
