## 满族萨满 / 关姓堂、博、查玛

Guwan hala-i tangse de,  
bolgo gisun be alambi,  
hulhun gisun akū,  
aisilame alara gisun akū.

Abka enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Abka-i jalan de,  
abka-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i bolgo gisun be donji.

Na enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Na-i jalan de,  
na-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i bolgo gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mafa,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i dolo tehe mafa,  
hala-i dolo tehe mafa,  
ere bolgo gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mama,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i dolo tehe mama,  
hala-i dolo tehe mama,  
ere bolgo gisun be donji.

Tangse-i enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i weceku,  
donji, donji.

Neyen be jafambi,  
gisun be tucibumbi,  
abka enduri de alambi,  
na enduri de alambi,  
mafa mama de alambi,  
tangse de alambi.

Abka enduri,  
beye-i sain be donji.  
Na enduri,  
beye-i elhe be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mafa,  
nimeku goroki oho seme donji.  
Guwan hala-i mama,  
jobolon waliyaha seme donji.  
Tangse-i enduri,  
hūturi se elhe be donji.

Akū oci akū seme,  
bihe oci bihe seme,  
majige be majige seme,  
ambalinggū be ambalinggū seme,  
ehe faksi be waliyambi,  
beye-i dolo elhe be karmambi,  
boo-i dolo bolgo be karmambi.

Niyalma-i hūturi be gaiki akū,  
niyalma-i se be efujeki akū,  
niyalma-i jalan be jobobuki akū;  
sain be dosimbu,  
ehe be tucibu,  
beye be elhe obure be baimbi.

Mafa mama,  
gisun be donji,  
sain jugūn be neimbi,  
ehe jugūn be tuwame waliyambi,  
nimeku be goroki obumbi,  
jobolon be waliyambi.

Tangse-i enduri,  
weile be acabumbi,  
gisun be acabumbi,  
beye-i sain,  
nimeku-i goroki,  
hūturi-i dosire,  
se-i elhe be acabumbi.

Abka enduri de wesihun,  
na enduri de wesihun,

tangse-i enduri de wesihun.

Donjiha be donjiha seme,

Enduri gene,

Gisun wajire,  
neyen wajire.  
Aisin gisun akū,  
hulhun gisun akū,  
yargiyan gisun be alaha.  
Bolgo be bolgo seme,  
elhe be elhe seme,
