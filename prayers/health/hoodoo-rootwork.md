## Hoodoo / Rootwork

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.

I stand before the Most High God with clean speech and a steady heart.  
Let this prayer be for bodily health, the keeping away of sickness and pain, and the blessing of long life, peace, and soundness.  
Let no falsehood, coercion, wrongful hand, or harm to the innocent be in it.  
I seek no healing by taking another’s strength, no long life by shortening another’s days, and no blessing by dispossessing another soul.

יְהוָה אֱלֹהָי שִׁוַּעְתִּי אֵלֶיךָ וַתִּרְפָּאֵנִי׃  
יְהוָה הֶעֱלִיתָ מִן־שְׁאוֹל נַפְשִׁי חִיִּיתַנִי מִיָּרְדִי־בוֹר׃

בָּרֲכִי נַפְשִׁי אֶת־יְהוָה וְכָל־קְרָבַי אֶת־שֵׁם קָדְשׁוֹ׃  
בָּרֲכִי נַפְשִׁי אֶת־יְהוָה וְאַל־תִּשְׁכְּחִי כָּל־גְּמוּלָיו׃  
הַסֹּלֵחַ לְכָל־עֲוֹנֵכִי הָרֹפֵא לְכָל־תַּחֲלֻאָיְכִי׃  
הַגּוֹאֵל מִשַּׁחַת חַיָּיְכִי הַמְעַטְּרֵכִי חֶסֶד וְרַחֲמִים׃  
הַמַּשְׂבִּיעַ בַּטּוֹב עֶדְיֵךְ תִּתְחַדֵּשׁ כַּנֶּשֶׁר נְעוּרָיְכִי׃

I speak no harm into the innocent.  
I bind no unwilling heart.  
I take back every crooked word, every proud demand, and every petition not fit to stand before God.

As my word is spoken before the Lord,  
let every unjust obstacle to health be cut away.  
Let the road of healing be opened in righteousness.  
Let the house be guarded.  
Let the hands be clean.  
Let the feet be set on a straight path.

יֹשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃  
כִּי הוּא יַצִּילְךָ מִפַּח יָקוּשׁ מִדֶּבֶר הַוּוֹת׃  
בְּאֶבְרָתוֹ יָסֶךְ לָךְ וְתַחַת־כְּנָפָיו תֶּחְסֶה צִנָּה וְסֹחֵרָה אֲמִתּוֹ׃

If this prayer touches the living, let it touch them in justice and mercy.  
If this prayer concerns sickness, let mercy, strength, wisdom, and right care surround the body.  
If this prayer concerns danger, let the snare be seen, the harm be turned aside, and the soul be kept.

Set this petition for health, freedom from illness, and peaceful longevity under the judgment of the Most High.  
What is righteous, establish it.  
What is crooked, remove it.  
What is not mine to command, return it to God.

חָנֵּנִי אֱלֹהִים כְּחַסְדֶּךָ כְּרֹב רַחֲמֶיךָ מְחֵה פְשָׁעָי׃  
הֶרֶב כַּבְּסֵנִי מֵעֲוֹנִי וּמֵחַטָּאתִי טַהֲרֵנִי׃  
לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים וְרוּחַ נָכוֹן חַדֵּשׁ בְּקִרְבִּי׃

Lord, cleanse this prayer of pride, fear, and wrongful demand.  
I withdraw every petition that would force, defraud, injure, or profane Your holy name.  
Let this work stand only as protection, healing, cleansing, mercy, and surrender to Your righteous judgment.

Amen. Amen. Amen.
