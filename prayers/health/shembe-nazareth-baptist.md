## Shembe

**Ukuvula / Ukubiza igama**

Nkulunkulu kaShembe, Baba wethu osezulwini,  
siyakhuleka phambi kwakho ngokuthobeka.  
Siza kuwe singaziphakamisi,  
kodwa sithemba umusa wakho, ukuthula kwakho, nentando yakho engcwele.

**Ihubo / Ukudumisa**

Siyakudumisa, Jehova;  
siyakubonga, Nkosi;  
igama lakho malibongwe.  
Wena ungumalusi osiholayo,  
usondelisa abagulayo, ududuze abesabayo,  
uqinise abaphelelwe amandla.

**Ukuzithoba nokuphenduka**

Nkosi, uma kukhona isono, ukungcola, ukungathembeki,  
ukunganaki umzimba owawusinika wona,  
noma ukona okwaziwayo nokungaziwayo,  
siyacela intethelelo, ukuhlanzwa, nokubuyiselwa endleleni yakho.  
Sihlanze enhliziyweni, emzimbeni, nasemoyeni,  
ukuze sime phambi kwakho ngokuthula.

**Baba wethu osezulwini**

Baba wethu osezulwini,  
malingcweliswe igama lakho;  
mawufike umbuso wakho;  
mayenziwe intando yakho emhlabeni njengasezulwini.  
Usiphe namuhla isinkwa sethu semihla ngemihla;  
usithethelele amacala ethu,  
njengokuba nathi sibathethelela abanamacala kithi;  
ungasingenisi ekulingweni,  
kodwa usikhulule kokubi.  
Ngokuba umbuso ungowakho, namandla, nenkazimulo, kuze kube phakade.  
Amen.

**Ukubeka izandla / Isibusiso somfundisi**

Ngokuthobeka phambi kukaNkulunkulu,  
sibeka izandla zomkhuleko phezu kwabagulayo nabadinga ukuqina.  
Nkosi, baqinise, baphilise, ubahlanze, ubavikele.  
Banike ukuthula emzimbeni, enhliziyweni, nasemoyeni.  
Uma ukuphila kubuhlungu, basondelise kuwe;  
uma amandla encipha, baphakamise ngomusa wakho.  
Makube njalo ngokwentando yakho, hhayi ngentando yethu kuphela.

**Ukubonga nokuphetha**

Siyakubonga, Nkosi.  
Siyakudumisa, Jehova.  
Ukuthula makube kubantu bakho,  
emizimbeni yabo, emakhaya abo, nasebandleni lakho.  

Nkosi, hlanza amazwi ethu, uqondise izinhliziyo zethu,  
usigcine ekuziphakamiseni nasekusebenziseni umkhuleko kabi.  
Sibuyisela konke kuwe ngokubonga, ngokuthobeka, nangokuthula.  
Igama lakho malibongwe manje naphakade.

Amen.
