## अघोर

[गुरु-स्मरण / guru-smaraṇa]

ॐ

śrī-gurubhyo namaḥ ।  
oṃ gurave namaḥ ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।

[शिव-शरण / śiva-śaraṇa]

oṃ śivāya śāntāya aghorāya rudrāya namaḥ ।  
śivaṃ śaraṇaṃ prapadye ।  
rudraṃ śaraṇaṃ prapadye ।  
guruṃ śaraṇaṃ prapadye ।

[सङ्कल्प / saṅkalpa]

{{petition}}  
ahiṃsā-satya-śiva-sākṣitvena idaṃ prārthanā-japaṃ karomi ।

na parasya puṇyaṃ hriyate ।  
na parasya āyuḥ kṣīyate ।  
sarvabhūta-hite sthitvā śivānugrahaṃ yāce ।

[अघोर-मन्त्र / aghora-mantra]

oṃ aghorebhyo'tha ghorebhyo ghoraghoratarebhyaḥ ।  
sarvebhyaḥ sarvaśarvebhyo namaste astu rudrarūpebhyaḥ ॥

[मुख्य-जप / mūla-japa]

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।

tryambakaṃ yajāmahe sugandhiṃ puṣṭivardhanam ।  
urvārukam iva bandhanān mṛtyor mukṣīya mā'mṛtāt ॥

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ ।  
sarve santu nirāmayāḥ ।  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu ।  
mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ॥

[क्षमा-प्रार्थना / kṣamā-prārthanā]

aparādhasahasrāṇi kriyante'harniśaṃ mayā ।  
dāso'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvara ॥

yad ajñānāt kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ yad vā moha-vaśān mayā ।  
tat sarvaṃ kṣamyatāṃ deva śiva śiva namo'stu te ॥

[शान्ति / śānti]

oṃ aghorebhyo'tha ghorebhyo ghoraghoratarebhyaḥ ।  
sarvebhyaḥ sarvaśarvebhyo namaste astu rudrarūpebhyaḥ ॥

oṃ dyauḥ śāntir antarikṣaṃ śāntiḥ ।  
pṛthivī śāntir āpaḥ śāntir oṣadhayaḥ śāntiḥ ।  
vanaspatayaḥ śāntir viśve devāḥ śāntir brahma śāntiḥ ।  
sarvaṃ śāntiḥ śāntir eva śāntiḥ ।  
sā mā śāntir edhi ॥

[समर्पण / samarpaṇa]

kāyena vācā manasendriyair vā  
buddhyātmanā vā prakṛteḥ svabhāvāt ।  
karomi yad yat sakalaṃ parasmai  
śivāya tubhyaṃ samarpayāmi ॥

śrī-gurubhyo namaḥ ।
