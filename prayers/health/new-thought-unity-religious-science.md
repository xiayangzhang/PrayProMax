## 新思维 · Unity / Religious Science

God, Spirit, Divine Mind, Infinite Life, Infinite Love, Infinite Intelligence,  
there is one Presence, one Power, one Life, one Love, one Wisdom, one Substance.

I am one with this Presence.  
All life is held in this one Life.  
There is no separation between God and the living truth of wholeness, strength, peace, and vitality.  
The Christ within, the divine idea, the indwelling Spirit, is now recognized as present, active, and clear.

I deny power to fear, confusion, pain-centered belief, discouragement, and every false belief concerning health, body, longevity, and peace.  
They have no authority in Divine Mind, no law in Spirit, no substance in Truth.  
I do not deny the worth of the body, nor the wisdom of right care; I deny only the claim that disorder has ultimate power over Life.

I affirm:  
God is my help in every need;  
God does my every hunger feed;  
God walks beside me, guides my way  
Through every moment of the day.
