## 现代术士派（Cochranian、Sabbatic Craft）

In the name of the Old One,  
Master of the Crooked Path,  
Keeper of the hidden fire,  
I hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
let this ring be whole,  
let no false word enter,  
let no oath be broken,  
let no secret be profaned.

{{petition}}

No stolen oath is spoken here.  
No hidden word is claimed here.  
Only the open charm is set,  
only the rightful prayer is made.

Hand to hand, hearth to hearth,  
seed to field, and coin to purse;  
let honest increase gather,  
let waste be stopped,  
let need be answered.

Let no blessing sought here dispossess another.  
Let no life be shortened,  
no rightful bread be taken,  
no honest house be emptied,  
no free will be bound.  
Let gain come by clean road,  
by skill, diligence, fair dealing,  
timely counsel, and open opportunity.

Where the hand is idle, stir it to worthy craft.  
Where the purse is leaking, close the wasteful gate.  
Where the field is barren, send seed and season.  
Where the door is shut, show the lawful key.  
Where fear clouds judgment, kindle the hidden fire.  
Where the house is thin, let the hearth grow warm.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
the word is spoken,  
the work is sealed,  
the compass is whole.

Old One, receive thanks.  
If any word has overreached, let it fall to ash.  
If any road is not rightful, let it be shut.  
If any wish has crossed its bound, let it be withdrawn.  
Let the charm return to clean measure,  
let the hand return to its own labor,  
let the hearth return to peace.

Let the road be shut where it must be shut.  
Let the road be open where it must be open.  
Peace to the ring,  
peace to the dead,  
peace to the living.

So mote it be.
