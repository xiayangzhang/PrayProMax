## Aurum Solis

In stillness and in purity I stand within the Light of the Ogdoadic Star.

Agios ho Theos,  
Agios Ischyros,  
Agios Athanatos,  
eleēson hēmas.

Deo Duce, Comite Ferro.

Let the work be established in harmony, concerning wealth rightly ordered, in the matter of prosperity, vocation, and household abundance.

IAO.  
AGATHOS DAIMŌN.  
IAO.

Zeus Pater, source of measure, order, and beneficent increase;  
Zeus Ktesios, guardian of the good store;  
Zeus Sōtēr, upholder of lawful fortune:  
may the Jovian light be tempered by wisdom, justice, and right proportion.

Holy and deathless Light, source and crown of the work, receive this vow:  
{{petition}}

Agios ho Theos,  
Agios Ischyros,  
Agios Athanatos,  
eleēson hēmas.

IAO.  
Zeus Pater, establish increase in right measure.  
IAO.  
AGATHOS DAIMŌN, guide fortune toward virtue and wise use.  
IAO.

The word is spoken, the measure is sealed, and the force is returned to its proper sphere.

Agios ho Theos,  
Agios Ischyros,  
Agios Athanatos,  
eleēson hēmas.

All unfinished compulsion is stilled; all personal grasping is withdrawn into silence; all invoked power returns peacefully to its own place and proper order.  
By purity, by light, by the ward rightly set: let the circle be closed, the heart be clear, and the work rest in harmony.

Deo Duce, Comite Ferro.  
Fiat. Amen.
