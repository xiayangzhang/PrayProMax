In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Holy and triune God, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit,  
we come before you seeking daily bread, honest provision, fruitful labor, and a household kept in peace and sufficiency.  
You alone are God; you alone are to be worshiped; you alone hear prayer through Jesus Christ, our only Mediator.
