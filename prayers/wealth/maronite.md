## الكنيسة المارونية

B-shmo d-Abo w-d-Bro w-d-Ruho d-Qudsho, Had Aloho shariro. Amin.

Qadishat Aloho,  
Qadishat Hayelthono,  
Qadishat lo Moyutho,  
ethraham ‘alayn.

Shubho l-Abo w-la-Bro w-la-Ruho d-Qudsho,  
men ‘olam w-‘adamo l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Abun d-bashmayo,  
netqadash shmokh.  
Tite malkuthokh.  
Nehwe sebyonokh,  
aykano d-bashmayo oph bar‘o.  
Haw lan lahmo d-sunqonan yawmono.  
Washboq lan hawbayn wahtohayn,  
aykano doph hnan shbaqn l-hayyobayn.  
Wlo ta‘lan l-nesyuno,  
elo fasso lan men bisho.  
Metul d-dilokh hi malkutho w-haylo w-teshbuhto  
l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Shlom lekh Maryam,  
mlayat taybutho,  
Moran ‘amekh.  
Mbarakhto at b-neshe,  
w-mbarakh hu piro d-karsekh,  
Maran Yeshua.  
O qadishto Maryam,  
Yoldath Aloho,  
saloy hlofayn hnaye,  
hosho w-bsho‘at mawthan. Amin.

B-shfa‘otho d-Yoldath Aloho Maryam,  
w-d-Mor Maron,  
w-d-Mor Efram,  
w-d-Mor Ya‘qub,  
w-d-kulhon qadisho,  
Moryo etraham ‘alayn.

Moryo, dkar l-kulhon haye d-saymin sabrhon ‘alayk:  
barrek parnosthon, naqeh re‘yonhon,  
hawi madbro l-‘amlehon,  
w-‘bad d-ne‘bad kol meddem b-shmekh, b-shayno, w-b-kino.

Dkar, Moryo, l-‘anide mhaymne,  
w-ašre ‘alayon nuhro d-apeik,  
w-lan haw shukrono w-lebo dakyo,  
d-lo nethpak l-hubba d-kespo,  
elo neshamesh lakh b-‘amlo w-b-rahme.

Moryo, nhoor lan b-nuhrokh,  
nattar lan b-slibokh,  
dak lan men hasomo w-men ta‘mutho,  
qaddesh sebyanan b-sebyonokh,  
w-qabel sluthan lo ayk taggurotho,  
elo ayk bu‘otho d-bnaye d-rahmayk.

Lakh shubho,  
l-Abokh iqoro,  
w-l-Ruho d-Qudsho segedto,  
hosho w-bkul zban  
w-l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.
