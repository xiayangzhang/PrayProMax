## 楚科奇 / 科里亚克萨满

Ənən, ənən, ənən. Aj, aj, aj. Həj, həj, həj.

Enen of hearth, enen of ancestors, enen of guardians: hear only a household voice, not a hidden shaman-song.

{{petition}}

For house, work, herd-luck, sea-luck, stored food, warm fire: enen, enen, həj, həj, həj.

What is asked is placed before the enen; what is not rightful is released; what belongs to others remains with others.

Ənən, ənən. Tumgə, tumgə. Aj, aj, aj. Həj, həj, həj.

If the word has gone too far, the song is stopped, the asking is withdrawn, and apology is given before hearth, ancestors, and guardian enen; the mouth is closed, the path is closed, the song is ended. Ənən.
