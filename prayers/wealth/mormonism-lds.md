## 摩门教（LDS）

Our Father in Heaven,

We thank Thee for life, work, family, daily bread, and the opportunity to labor honestly before Thee.

We acknowledge Thy Son, Jesus Christ, our Savior and Redeemer, and we seek the guidance of the Holy Ghost in all temporal and spiritual matters.

{{petition}}

We do not seek gain by taking from others, harming others, or diminishing the blessings of another household. May prosperity come in ways that are honest, peaceful, lawful, and pleasing unto Thee.

May Thy will be done, and may our home receive abundance sufficient for service, generosity, family stability, and peace according to faith in Jesus Christ.

May we remember the Lord Jesus Christ, keep His commandments, follow the promptings of the Holy Ghost, and honor Thy sacred order with reverence and humility.

We say these things in the name of Jesus Christ, amen.
