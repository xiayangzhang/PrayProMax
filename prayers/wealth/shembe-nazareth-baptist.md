## Shembe

**Ukuvula / Ukubiza igama**

Nkulunkulu kaShembe, Baba wethu,  
siyakhuleka phambi kwakho ngokuthobeka.  
Siletha indaba yomsebenzi, yokondleka kwekhaya, nokuvuleka kwezindlela zokuphila.

**Ihubo / Ukudumisa**

Siyakudumisa, Jehova;  
siyakubonga, Nkosi;  
igama lakho malibongwe.  
Wena ongumelusi wabantu bakho,  
sihole endleleni eqondile, emsebenzini ohlanzekile, nasempilweni enokuthula.

**Ukuzithoba nokuphenduka**

Nkosi, uma kukhona ukungathembeki, ukungcola kwenhliziyo, ukuziqhenya, noma ukona okwaziwayo nokungaziwayo,  
siyacela intethelelo yakho.  
Sihlanze emicabangweni emibi, usibuyisele endleleni yokulunga,  
ukuze isibusiso somsebenzi singabi ngokuzigqaja, kodwa sibe ngokwethemba kuwe.

**Baba wethu osezulwini**

Baba wethu osezulwini,  
malingcweliswe igama lakho;  
mawufike umbuso wakho;  
mayenziwe intando yakho emhlabeni njengasezulwini.  
Usiphe namuhla isinkwa sethu semihla ngemihla;  
usithethelele amacala ethu,  
njengokuba nathi sibathethelela abanamacala kithi;  
ungasingenisi ekulingweni,  
kodwa usikhulule kokubi.  
Ngokuba umbuso ungowakho, namandla, nenkazimulo, kuze kube phakade.  
Amen.

**Ukubonga / Ukuvala ngokuthula**

Siyakubonga, Nkosi, ngoba isinkwa sethu sivela kuwe.  
Siyakudumisa, Jehova, ngoba amandla omsebenzi avela kuwe.  
Ukuthula makube sekhaya,  
ukuthula makube emsebenzini,  
ukuthula makube endleleni yokufuna impilo.

Konke lokhu makwenziwe ngokomusa wakho nangentando yakho engcwele,  
kungabi ngokuzigqaja kwethu, kodwa ngokwethemba kuwe.  
Igama lakho malibongwe manje naphakade.

Amen.
