{{petition}}

Let every word concerning prosperity be ordered by Wisdom, measured by Understanding, balanced by Beauty, and grounded in righteous manifestation.  
Let this prayer bind no will, coerce no person, diminish no life, and dispossess none of their rightful good.  
Let no wealth be sought through another’s loss, no success through another’s ruin, and no increase through injustice.  
Let the hidden pattern behind livelihood, work, and household provision be aligned with the Holy Names and with the true good of all concerned.

אדני מלך אדני הארץ

May supply be established in right measure.  
May enterprise be guided by clarity, patience, skill, and honorable exchange.  
May the fruits of labor be received with gratitude, governed with wisdom, and used in service of life.
