Matyox, matyox, matyox.  
Chuwach ri q'aq', chuwach ri pom, chuwach ri kotz'ij,  
k'o ri qatzij, k'o ri qanawal, k'o ri qanim.

At nawal rech wa q'ij,  
tat ri q'ij, nan ri q'ij,  
taqab'ana' utzil, taqab'ana' choq'ab',  
taqab'ana' saqil, taqab'ana' jikomal.

At Tz'ikin, nawal rech utzil, rech rutzil k'aslemal.  
At Q'anil, nawal rech ixim, rech tiko'n, rech wachaj.  
At Toj, nawal rech matyoxinik, rech k'axk'olil, rech suk'ulaj.  
Chuwach iwe k'o ri qatzij:  
are' ri qak'utunik rech qasipanik pa saqil,  
rech qasamaj pa jikomal,  
rech qachoch pa no'j, pa utzil, pa q'ijil.

Qati't, qamama',  
qachuch, qajaw,  
e k'o pa ri saqil, e k'o pa ri q'equmal,  
matyox chi iwe.

Chuwach ri juyub', chuwach ri taq'aj,  
chuwach ri ja', chuwach ri b'e,  
matyox chi iwe.
