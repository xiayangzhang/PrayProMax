## 伊斯玛仪派 / 阿迦汗系

**Niyyah / niyyah:**  
Allāhumma innī ajʿalu hādhā al-duʿāʾ li-wajhika al-karīm, wa aṭlubu raḥmataka wa hidāyataka fī saʿati r-rizq, wa yusri l-aʿmāl, wa barakati l-bayt, ʿalā ṣirāṭika l-mustaqīm, bi-shukrin wa amānah.

**Basmalah:**  
Bismillāhi r-Raḥmāni r-Raḥīm.  
بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

**Al-Fātiḥah:**  
Al-ḥamdu lillāhi rabbi l-ʿālamīn.  
Ar-raḥmāni r-raḥīm.  
Māliki yawmi d-dīn.  
Iyyāka naʿbudu wa iyyāka nastaʿīn.  
Ihdinā ṣ-ṣirāṭa l-mustaqīm.  
Ṣirāṭa lladhīna anʿamta ʿalayhim, ghayri l-maghḍūbi ʿalayhim wa lā ḍ-ḍāllīn.  
Āmīn.

**Shahādah wa walāyah:**  
Ashhadu an lā ilāha illā Allāh.  
Ashhadu anna Muḥammadan Rasūlu llāh.  
Ashhadu anna Mawlānā ʿAlīyyan Amīru l-Muʾminīn ʿAlīyyu llāh.

**Ṣalawāt:**  
Allāhumma ṣalli ʿalā Muḥammadin wa āli Muḥammad.  
Allāhumma bārik ʿalā Muḥammadin wa āli Muḥammad.

**Tasbīḥ:**  
Yā Allāh.  
Yā Muḥammad.  
Yā ʿAlī.  
Yā Ḥāḍir Imām, madad.

**Ginan remembrance:**  
Eji satgur miliyā munē āj, ānand ānand thayō.  
Eji sāhebjī tū̃ morē man bhāvē.

**Duʿāʾ li-l-arwāḥ wa l-jamāʿah:**  
Allāhumma ghfir li-l-muʾminīna wa l-muʾmināt,  
wa rḥam arwāḥa l-marḥūmīn wa l-marḥūmāt,  
wa anzil nūra l-hidāyah fī qulūbinā,  
wa fī qulūbi jamāʿatinā kullihā.

Allāhumma ajʿal hādhā dhikran wa shukran wa duʿāʾan laka waḥdaka,  
wa lā tajʿal fīhi ẓulman wa lā ikrāhan wa lā ḍararan,  
wa ayyidnā bi-raḥmatika ʿalā l-khayr, wa ṣ-ṣidq, wa l-amānah,  
wa bārik fī jamāʿatinā, wa fī Mawlānā Ḥāḍir Imām,  
bi-faḍlika wa raḥmatika, yā Arḥama r-rāḥimīn.

Allāhumma taqabbal duʿāʾanā.  
Āmīn.  
Yā ʿAlī madad.
