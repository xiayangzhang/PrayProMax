## Mami Wata 信仰

I stand by clean water, with cool heart and clean mouth.  
No pride, no force, no false spirit-work.  
Only peace I carry come near the water.

Mami Wata, Mammy Water, Nne mmiri, Yeye Omi,  
I call only your name for this prayer.  
No other spirit name dey this same mouth.

Mami Wata o, Mami Wata o,  
bia, nuru olu m.  
Nne mmiri, nne oma,  
mmiri di ọcha, mmiri ndu.

Mammy Water, I hail you.  
Fine lady for water, mother for river,  
she wey sabi deep water,  
she wey hold mirror for truth,  
she wey cool hot heart,  
she wey make market road shine,  
she wey bring better thing with clean hand.

{{petition}}

Mammy Water, bring better thing,  
but no let this prayer take another person portion.  
No make am dispossess any body,  
no make am cut short any life,  
no make am spoil another house.  
Let gain come by clean road,  
let trade come with truth,  
let prosperity come with cool heart.

Yeye Omi, iya omi,  
cover this wish with white water.  
Omi tutu, omi alaafia,  
ko si ija, ko si iro,  
alaafia ni mo pe.

Mami Wata o, Mammy Water o,  
no vex, no trouble.  
If wrong word don enter this mouth,  
make wrong word fall for water.  
If greedy thought don rise,  
make water cool am.  
If any promise no be true,  
make only truth stand.

Mmiri oma, kuru ihe ojoo laa.  
Mmiri ndu, mee ka obi di jụụ.  
Nne mmiri, receive this voice,  
receive this clean water,  
receive this word of peace.

No calling for possession,  
no false message,  
no oath, no sacrifice, no fear.  
Only praise, clean wish, and quiet respect.

If any bad road, wrong request, or careless spirit-call don happen,  
I withdraw am inside clean water.  
Let water wash, let water cool,  
let peace return to the river,  
let peace return to the road,  
let peace return to the house.

Mammy Water, I thank you.  
Nne mmiri, daalu.  
Yeye Omi, mo dupe.  
Alaafia. Udo. Peace.
