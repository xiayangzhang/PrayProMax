## 壮族师公 / 麽教

[净坛报坛]

今在家门清净处，  
为财源广进、事业顺遂、家宅丰盈之愿，  
香灯已列，清水已陈，坛面已净，门户已开。  
所求为正财正业、勤劳所得、家用充足；  
不夺他人福报，不损他人寿数，不以邪路取财，不以欺心成事。

[请师祖]

Cingj boux sai mo daengz.  
Cingj boux swhgung daengz.  
Cingj gyoengq swhbo daengz.  
Daihnaj miz dangh, daihlaeng miz roen.  
Gyoengq swhbo yawj, gyoengq swhbo couh.

[请布洛陀]

Cingj Bouxloxdoh daengz.  
Bouxloxdoh gwnz mbwn, Bouxloxdoh laj deih.  
Bouxloxdoh dingh leix, Bouxloxdoh dingh lwg.  
Cingj Bouxloxdoh yawj ranz,  
cingj Bouxloxdoh rox gij sienq ndei.

[请姆洛甲]

Cingj Mehloeggyap daengz.  
Mehloeggyap hoz vunz, Mehloeggyap hoz lwg.  
Mehloeggyap yawj ranz, Mehloeggyap yawj sim.  
Cingj Mehloeggyap yawj ranz,  
cingj Mehloeggyap hoz sim ndei.

[告祖]

Cingj goeng meh daengz.  
Ranz miz mbaw, vunz miz sim.  
Cingj goeng meh yawj ranz,  
cingj goeng meh yawj roen,  
hawj ranz miz fuk, hawj vunz miz roen ndei.

[告社土]

Cingj deih boux, cingj lungz ranz.  
Gwnz mbwn miz dingh, laj deih miz roen.  
Cingj deih boux dingh deih,  
cingj lungz ranz dingh dou.  
Dingh ranz, dingh dou, dingh roen haeux roen ngenz.

[报明事项]

{{petition}}  
Mbouj yawj dox, mbouj gangj laux,  
gangj ndaej caemh leix, gangj ndaej caemh fap.

[发愿 / 回向]

Gou daeuj baenz cieng,  
gou daeuj baenz raiz.  
Mbouj gangj dox, mbouj gangj laux.  
所陈为至诚祈请，非以财物交易；  
所得若有增长，愿知感恩，守诚信，济家门，行善路。

[正行唱诵：通用麽唱]

Fwen mo hwnj, fwen mo loengh.  
Hwnj bae gwnz mbwn, loengh daeuj laj deih.  
Roen ndei hwnj, roen ndei loengh,  
fuk ndei dauq ranz, ngenz ndei dauq dou.

[正行：请师祖照坛]

Cingj swhgung yawj roen,  
cingj swhbo yawj sim.  
Cingj gyoengq swhbo dawz fwen,  
cingj gyoengq swhbo dawz roen.  
Roen mbouj dam, roen mbouj doek,  
sim mbouj loek, fap mbouj loek.

[正行：请布洛陀定礼]

Cingj Bouxloxdoh dingh leix.  
Ranz miz gij ciengz, ranz miz gij sienq.  
Gij sienq mbouj loek, gij ciengz mbouj rox.  
Cingj Bouxloxdoh dingh roen saeh,  
hawj saeh ndei baenz, hawj roen ndei hoi.

[正行：请姆洛甲护生]

Cingj Mehloeggyap hoz lwg.  
Ranz miz gij ciengz, ranz miz gij sienq.  
Gij sienq mbouj loek, gij ciengz mbouj rox.  
Hoz vunz, hoz ranz, hoz sim, hoz dou,  
hawj vunz gwn haeux miz mbaw, hawj ranz gwn raemx miz ndei.

[正行：告祖定家]

Cingj goeng meh dingh ranz.  
Cingj gyoengq goeng meh dawz roen.  
Roen haeux ndei, roen ngenz ndei,  
roen saeh ndei, roen gya ndei.  
Hawj ranz miz cienz, hawj dou miz fuk,  
hawj gwnz laj caemh dingh.

[正行：告社土定寨]

Cingj deih lungz dingh mbanj.  
Cingj deih lungz dawz roen.  
Dingh deih, dingh dou, dingh roen, dingh ranz.  
Mbouj hawj sa doek, mbouj hawj ok laux.  
Hawj saeh youq roen cingq,  
hawj ngenz daeuj roen ndei.

[纳福归家]

Cingj Bouxloxdoh dawz fuk dauq.  
Cingj Mehloeggyap dawz lwg ndei dauq.  
Cingj goeng meh dawz roen ndei dauq.  
Cingj deih lungz dawz fuk ranz dauq.  
Fuk dauq ranz, roen ndei dauq dou,  
saeh ndei dauq sim, cienz ndei dauq gya.  
Dauq mbouj dam, dauq mbouj doek,  
dauq caemh leix, dauq caemh ndei.

[辞神送师]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq Bouxloxdoh dauq gwnz.  
Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq Mehloeggyap dauq dieg.  
Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq goeng meh dauq ranz.  
Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq deih lungz dauq deih.  
Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq swhgung dauq dangh,  
soengq swhbo dauq roen.

[封坛回向]

Dou laeg, roen laeg.  
Fwen gaeq, fap gaeq.  
Gij ndei dauq ranz,  
gij ndei dauq gya,  
gij ndei dauq vunz.  
愿所得之福，归于清净正路；  
愿家宅丰盈而心不贪，事业顺遂而行不欺，财源广进而不损众生。  
Mbouj loek fwen, mbouj loek leix.  
Dingh.
