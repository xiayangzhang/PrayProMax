## 满族萨满 / 关姓堂、博、查玛

Guwan hala-i tangse neyen.

Abka enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Abka-i jalan de,  
abka-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Na enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Na-i jalan de,  
na-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mafa,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i dolo tehe mafa,  
hala-i dolo tehe mafa,  
gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mama,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i dolo tehe mama,  
hala-i dolo tehe mama,  
gisun be donji.

Tangse-i enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i weceku,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i tangse de,  
boo be alambi,  
hala be alambi,  
sain be alambi.

Neyen be jafambi,  
gisun be tucibumbi,  
enduri de alambi,  
mafa mama de alambi,  
tangse de alambi.

Abka enduri,  
sain be donji.  
Na enduri,  
sain be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mafa,  
sain be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mama,  
sain be donji.  
Tangse-i enduri,  
sain be donji.

Akū oci akū seme,  
bihe oci bihe seme,  
majige be majige seme,  
ambalinggū be ambalinggū seme,  
ehe be tucibu,  
jobolon be waliyara,  
boo be karmambi,  
hala be karmambi.

Mafa mama,  
gisun be donji,  
sain be dosimbu,  
ehe be tucibu,  
weile be acabumbi,  
gisun be acabumbi,  
boo-i hūturi be elgiyembumbi.

Abka enduri de wesihun,  
na enduri de wesihun,  
Guwan hala-i mafa de wesihun,  
Guwan hala-i mama de wesihun,  
tangse-i enduri de wesihun.

Donjiha be donjiha seme,  
aliha be aliha seme,  
jifi seme jihe,  
gene seme genehe.

Enduri gene,  
mafa mama gene,  
tangse-i hūsun tangse de tehe.  
Gisun wajire,  
neyen wajire.  
Aisin gisun akū,  
hulhun gisun akū,  
yargiyan gisun be alaha.

谨以清净心谢神、谢祖、谢堂中护佑；不越秘传，不冒正仪，不擅动鼓帽神具，不作欺愿妄言。愿所祈归于家门安稳、勤业正财、和合丰盈；神归神位，祖归祖位，堂中清宁，门户安宁。
