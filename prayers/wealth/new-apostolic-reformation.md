## 新使徒改革

Father God, I come before You in the name of Jesus Christ concerning provision, righteous work, fruitful enterprise, and a household established in peace and abundance.

Πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς·  
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου·  
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου·  
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου,  
ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς·  
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον δὸς ἡμῖν σήμερον·  
καὶ ἄφες ἡμῖν τὰ ὀφειλήματα ἡμῶν,  
ὡς καὶ ἡμεῖς ἀφήκαμεν τοῖς ὀφειλέταις ἡμῶν·  
καὶ μὴ εἰσενέγκῃς ἡμᾶς εἰς πειρασμόν,  
ἀλλὰ ῥῦσαι ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τοῦ πονηροῦ.

Father, I confess and renounce every sin, fear, greed, anxiety, dishonest gain, idolatry of money, false security, ungodly vow, inherited pattern of lack, wastefulness, debt-bondage, and false word connected with work, finances, business, and household provision. I place my labor, resources, decisions, and home under the blood and lordship of Jesus Christ.

I forgive those connected with financial pressure, workplace injustice, broken promises, or past losses, and I release them from my judgment. I do not curse them, control them, or claim vengeance over them. I bless what You bless, and I reject only the works of darkness, lies, bondage, sin, oppression, and unrighteous mammon.

Οὐδεὶς δύναται δυσὶ κυρίοις δουλεύειν·  
ἢ γὰρ τὸν ἕνα μισήσει  
καὶ τὸν ἕτερον ἀγαπήσει,  
ἢ ἑνὸς ἀνθέξεται  
καὶ τοῦ ἑτέρου καταφρονήσει·  
οὐ δύνασθε θεῷ δουλεύειν καὶ μαμωνᾷ.

ὁ δὲ θεός μου πληρώσει πᾶσαν χρείαν ὑμῶν  
κατὰ τὸ πλοῦτος αὐτοῦ  
ἐν δόξῃ ἐν Χριστῷ Ἰησοῦ.

Ἰδοὺ δέδωκα ὑμῖν τὴν ἐξουσίαν  
τοῦ πατεῖν ἐπάνω ὄφεων καὶ σκορπίων,  
καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν δύναμιν τοῦ ἐχθροῦ,  
καὶ οὐδὲν ὑμᾶς οὐ μὴ ἀδικήσει.

ὃ ἐὰν δήσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται δεδεμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς,  
καὶ ὃ ἐὰν λύσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται λελυμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς.

Ὑποτάγητε οὖν τῷ θεῷ·  
ἀντίστητε δὲ τῷ διαβόλῳ,  
καὶ φεύξεται ἀφ᾽ ὑμῶν.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I bind every demonic assignment, accusation, covenant of darkness, curse, word of poverty, spirit of fear, spirit of greed, spirit of confusion, spirit of delay, spirit of debt-bondage, spirit of waste, and every power of the enemy operating through unrighteous mammon, deception, or oppression. I command every unclean influence attached to work, finances, business decisions, contracts, property, or household provision to leave and not return, according to the authority of Jesus Christ.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I loose truth, repentance, faith, holiness, wisdom, diligence, favor, honest opportunity, righteous increase, creative strategy, faithful stewardship, peace, protection, discernment, generosity, and obedience to the will of God over work, enterprise, resources, and household life.

I cut off every ungodly soul tie, false burden, occult claim, generational bondage, spoken curse, inner vow, fear agreement, poverty mindset, mammon agreement, and demonic access point connected with finances, career, business, and household provision. I declare that the handwriting against me is canceled in Christ and that every hostile power is disarmed under the cross of Jesus Christ.

{{petition}}

Καὶ αὐτοὶ ἐνίκησαν αὐτὸν  
διὰ τὸ αἷμα τοῦ ἀρνίου  
καὶ διὰ τὸν λόγον τῆς μαρτυρίας αὐτῶν.

Father, fill every cleansed place with the Holy Spirit, the peace of Christ, the fear of the Lord, wisdom, purity, steadfast love, disciplined stewardship, and generous hands. Establish Your kingdom order in my work, finances, decisions, business dealings, and household. Let provision increase, let the work of my hands prosper in righteousness, let the home be fruitful and peaceful, and let every resource serve Your purposes.

Father, keep this prayer under the lordship of Jesus Christ and within Your holy will. I direct no curse toward any human person or people; I reject only sin, lies, bondage, darkness, and the works of the enemy. If fear, compulsion, strife, or crisis arises, lead me away from aggressive speech and into repentance, forgiveness, pastoral wisdom, wise counsel, and peace. I thank You, Father, and seal this prayer in the name of Jesus Christ.

Ἀμήν.
