## 路易斯安那伏都 Voodoo

Bon Dieu, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit,  
I set my mouth clean and my intention straight.  
I speak this prayer for wealth, steady work, open roads, and a full house.  
Let this hand be named **Open Road Prosperity Hand**, and let its prayer be bound only to honest increase, rightful opportunity, peaceful business, and a home kept in sufficiency.  
I seek no gain that dispossesses another, no blessing that shortens another life, no road opened by harm, theft, coercion, or broken faith.

This hand is named **Open Road Prosperity Hand**.  
Its prayer is sealed for honest increase, steady career, fruitful labor, and household abundance.  
It is not sealed for domination, fraud, revenge, or taking what belongs to another.  
What is not mine by right, let it pass by me.  
What is mine by mercy, labor, and lawful blessing, let it find the road home.

Holy ancestors of my blood and my house, witness only what is clean in this prayer.  
Guard the road, strengthen the hands, steady the mind, and keep the house from hunger and disorder.  
Spirits who walk in righteousness under Bon Dieu, stand only where welcome, only where clean, only where peace is kept.  
Let no name be miscalled, no promise be made beyond measure, and no burden be placed on the living or the dead.

Gloria Patri, et Filio, et Spiritui Sancto.  
Sicut erat in principio, et nunc, et semper,  
et in saecula saeculorum.  
Amen.

If any word has gone beyond rightful measure, let it fall away and be forgiven.  
If any vow was implied, let it be released; I bind no unpaid debt to spirit, saint, ancestor, or the dead.  
Let the road be washed clean, the hand be kept honest, and the house be left in peace.  
Holy ancestors, be comforted; Bon Dieu, have mercy.  
Amen. Ashe.
