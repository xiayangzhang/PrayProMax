## Spiritualists' National Union

In stillness and harmony, we open this prayer with sincerity, goodwill, and peace.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, source of love, truth, and life,

We acknowledge the Seven Principles:

The Fatherhood of God.  
The Brotherhood of Man.  
The Communion of Spirits and the Ministry of Angels.  
The continuous existence of the human soul.  
Personal Responsibility.  
Compensation and Retribution hereafter for all the good and evil deeds done on earth.  
Eternal progress open to every human soul.

{{petition}}

May prosperity come through right effort, fair dealing, useful service, and peaceful cooperation.  
May no blessing sought here dispossess another, diminish another’s rightful good, or shorten the life or welfare of any living soul.  
May obstacles be eased without harm, fear, coercion, or domination.

May love surround this home.  
May truth guide each decision.  
May healing, comfort, and strength be present according to the highest good.  
May all thought, word, and action be held in kindness, honesty, and personal responsibility.

Where uncertainty is present, may wisdom unfold.  
Where effort is needed, may courage arise.  
Where resources are entrusted, may they be used with gratitude, fairness, and care.  
Where success is sought, may it be joined with compassion, humility, and eternal progress.

We send blessing to all living beings, and loving remembrance to those in the Spirit World, honouring the continuous existence of the human soul in peace and affection.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, let this prayer be only for love, truth, healing, consolation, spiritual growth, goodwill, and the highest good, without claim of certainty or demand of outcome.

Amen.
