## स्मार्त

oṃ apavitraḥ pavitro vā sarvāvasthāṃ gato 'pi vā |  
yaḥ smaret puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ sa bāhyābhyantaraḥ śuciḥ ||

oṃ keśavāya svāhā |  
oṃ nārāyaṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ mādhavāya svāhā ||

oṃ bhūḥ | oṃ bhuvaḥ | oṃ suvaḥ | oṃ mahaḥ | oṃ janaḥ | oṃ tapaḥ | oṃ satyam |  
oṃ tat savitur vareṇyaṃ bhargo devasya dhīmahi dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt |  
oṃ āpo jyotī raso 'mṛtaṃ brahma bhūr bhuvaḥ suvar oṃ ||

hiraṇyavarṇāṃ hariṇīṃ suvarṇarajatasrajām |  
candrāṃ hiraṇmayīṃ lakṣmīṃ jātavedo ma āvaha ||

tāṃ ma āvaha jātavedo lakṣmīm anapagāminīm |  
yasyāṃ hiraṇyaṃ vindeyaṃ gāmaśvaṃ puruṣān aham ||

aśvapūrvāṃ rathamadhyāṃ hastinādaprabodhinīm |  
śriyaṃ devīm upahvaye śrīr mā devīr juṣatām ||

kāṃ so'smitāṃ hiraṇyaprākārām ārdrāṃ jvalantīṃ tṛptāṃ tarpayantīm |  
padme sthitāṃ padmavarṇāṃ tām ihopahvaye śriyam ||

candrāṃ prabhāsāṃ yaśasā jvalantīṃ śriyaṃ loke devajuṣṭām udārām |  
tāṃ padminīm īṃ śaraṇam ahaṃ prapadye 'lakṣmīr me naśyatāṃ tvāṃ vṛṇe ||

oṃ śrī-mahālakṣmi namaḥ |  
namaste astu bhagavati śrī-mahālakṣmi |  
anena pūjā-japa-śrī-sūkta-pāṭhena dhana-dhānya-samṛddhi-vyāpāra-kārya-siddhi-gṛha-sampat-pravṛddhiḥ astu ||

mantra-hīnaṃ kriyā-hīnaṃ bhakti-hīnaṃ śrī-mahālakṣmi |  
yat pūjitaṃ mayā devi paripūrṇaṃ tad astu me ||

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante 'harniśaṃ mayā |  
dāso 'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvari ||

yāntu devagaṇāḥ sarve pūjām ādāya māmakīm |  
iṣṭakāma-prasiddhyarthaṃ punarāgamanāya ca ||

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ sarve santu nirāmayāḥ |  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ||

oṃ apavitraḥ pavitro vā sarvāvasthāṃ gato 'pi vā |  
yaḥ smaret puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ sa bāhyābhyantaraḥ śuciḥ ||

oṃ keśavāya svāhā |  
oṃ nārāyaṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ mādhavāya svāhā ||

kāyena vācā manasendriyair vā buddhyātmanā vā prakṛteḥ svabhāvāt |  
karomi yad yat sakalaṃ parasmai nārāyaṇāyeti samarpayāmi ||

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ||

sarvaṃ śrī-parameśvarārpaṇam astu ||
