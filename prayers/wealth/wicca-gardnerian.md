## 威卡 Gardnerian

In this place, in this hour, I stand between the worlds,  
with clean hands, clear speech, and a will held in Perfect Love and Perfect Trust.

I conjure thee, O Circle of Power,  
that thou beest a boundary between the world of men and the realms of the Mighty Ones,  
a guardian and a meeting-place for the working of wealth, right livelihood, and household abundance.

Lady of the Moon,  
Great Mother, Bright One, Queen of the secret place,  
hear the words spoken within this circle.

Whenever ye have need of anything,  
once in the month, and better it be when the moon is full,  
then shall ye assemble in some desert place,  
or in a forest all together join  
to adore the potent spirit of your queen.

Horned One,  
Lord of the wildwood, keeper of life and death,  
stand apart in thy own power and witness this working.

{{petition}}

Before the Lord I speak the boundary:  
Let this be gained by lawful labor, fair exchange, sound judgment, and willing cooperation;  
let it take nothing unjustly from another,  
dispossess no household, shorten no life, diminish no rightful blessing,  
and bind no person against true will.

Let this prayer be for prosperity in right measure,  
harming none, binding none against the true will,  
and turning aside all falsehood, fear, greed, and ill-wrought force.

Eko, Eko, Azarak,  
Eko, Eko, Zomelak.

By earth, let prosperity take lawful root.  
By air, let good counsel, clear plans, and fitting chances be known.  
By fire, let enterprise be quickened without harm.  
By water, let wealth flow in peace, generosity, and household blessing.

Lady of the Moon, receive this prayer.  
Horned One, witness this prayer.  
Mighty Ones of the circle, keep it within right measure.

As the cup and the bread are blessed in fellowship,  
so may the work be whole:  
for wealth, right livelihood, and a flourishing home,  
according to freedom, fairness, and Perfect Love and Perfect Trust.

May all excess pride be cleansed from this working;  
may all coercion fall away;  
may only what is rightful, peaceful, and life-giving remain.  
Lady and Lord, be thanked in reverence.  
Mighty Ones, depart in peace, as peace remains between us.  
The circle is open, yet unbroken.  
Merry meet, and merry part, and merry meet again.
