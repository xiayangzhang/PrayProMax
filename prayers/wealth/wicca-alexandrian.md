## 威卡 Alexandrian

I stand within the Circle, in perfect love and perfect trust.  
Let all within this rite be bounded by the Rede:  
An it harm none, do what ye will.

By Air, by Fire, by Water, by Earth,  
this Circle is cast and sealed.  
Between the worlds, and in the presence of the Mighty Ones,  
let this rite be held for prosperity, right livelihood, a smooth path of work, and abundance within the home.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

O Great Mother,  
Lady of the Moon,  
Listen to the words of the Great Mother.  
{{petition}}

O Horned One,  
Lord of the Greenwood,  
guardian of life, death, and return,  
Receive this prayer for strength in labor, courage in endeavor,  
right timing, fruitful effort, and increase that harms none.

Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak,  
Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak.

May prosperity come by rightful paths.  
May obstacles be removed without injury to any being.  
May skill, opportunity, honest reward, and household plenty grow together.  
May this blessing seek no theft of another’s fortune, no dispossession, no shortening of another’s life, and no binding of another’s will.

Within right will, right boundary, and harm to none,  
let the prayer be shaped in truth.

So mote it be.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

The Circle is open, but unbroken.  
Merry meet, merry part, and merry meet again.

By the Rede I close and ground this working:  
An it harm none, do what ye will.  
Let all excess be released into the Earth,  
let all blessing remain in right measure,  
and let peace be upon this house.

Blessed be.
