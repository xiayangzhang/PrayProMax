## Hoodoo / Rootwork

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.

I stand before the Most High God with clean speech and a steady heart.  
Let this prayer be for honest increase, steady work, open roads, prosperous business, and a household filled with lawful plenty.  
Let no falsehood, coercion, wrongful hand, or crooked gain be in it.  
I seek not to dispossess another, not to shorten another’s days, not to take bread from another table, but to receive provision by clean hands and righteous increase.

בִּנְאוֹת דֶּשֶׁא יַרְבִּיצֵנִי עַל־מֵי מְנֻחוֹת יְנַהֲלֵנִי׃  
נַפְשִׁי יְשׁוֹבֵב יַנְחֵנִי בְמַעְגְּלֵי־צֶדֶק לְמַעַן שְׁמוֹ׃  
גַּם כִּי־אֵלֵךְ בְּגֵיא צַלְמָוֶת לֹא־אִירָא רָע כִּי־אַתָּה עִמָּדִי שִׁבְטְךָ וּמִשְׁעַנְתֶּךָ הֵמָּה יְנַחֲמֻנִי׃  
תַּעֲרֹךְ לְפָנַי שֻׁלְחָן נֶגֶד צֹרְרָי דִּשַּׁנְתָּ בַשֶּׁמֶן רֹאשִׁי כּוֹסִי רְוָיָה׃  
אַךְ טוֹב וָחֶסֶד יִרְדְּפוּנִי כָּל־יְמֵי חַיָּי וְשַׁבְתִּי בְּבֵית־יְהוָה לְאֹרֶךְ יָמִים׃

The Lord is my Shepherd; I shall not want.  
Let the road of supply be opened in righteousness.  
Let good work come steady, let honest customers and helpers come in peace, let wise judgment guide every deal, every task, every hand that earns.  
Let the table be set, the cup be filled, the house be guarded, and the store of the household increase without theft, envy, fraud, or harm.

I speak no harm into the innocent.  
I bind no unwilling heart.  
I take back every crooked word, every proud demand, every petition not fit to stand before God.  
Let obstacles be cut away without injury to the innocent.  
Let confusion be cleared, waste be stopped, debt be handled with wisdom, and the feet be set on a straight path.

שִׁיר לַמַּעֲלוֹת אֶשָּׂא עֵינַי אֶל־הֶהָרִים מֵאַיִן יָבֹא עֶזְרִי׃  
עֶזְרִי מֵעִם יְהוָה עֹשֵׂה שָׁמַיִם וָאָרֶץ׃  
אַל־יִתֵּן לַמּוֹט רַגְלֶךָ אַל־יָנוּם שֹׁמְרֶךָ׃  
הִנֵּה לֹא־יָנוּם וְלֹא יִישָׁן שׁוֹמֵר יִשְׂרָאֵל׃

חָנֵּנִי אֱלֹהִים כְּחַסְדֶּךָ כְּרֹב רַחֲמֶיךָ מְחֵה פְשָׁעָי׃  
הֶרֶב כַּבְּסֵנִי מֵעֲוֹנִי וּמֵחַטָּאתִי טַהֲרֵנִי׃  
כִּי־פְשָׁעַי אֲנִי אֵדָע וְחַטָּאתִי נֶגְדִּי תָמִיד׃  
לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים וְרוּחַ נָכוֹן חַדֵּשׁ בְּקִרְבִּי׃

Create in me a clean heart, O God, and renew a right spirit within me.  
I withdraw every unjust demand, every greedy reach, every word spoken from fear, envy, or pride.  
Cleanse this petition; let it stand only where mercy, truth, and righteousness allow.  
By the keeping of the Most High, let the soul be guarded, the road be made straight, and the blessing be received without harm.

Amen. Amen. Amen.
