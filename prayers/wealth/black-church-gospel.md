## 黑人教会传统

Father God, in the name of Jesus, we come before You.

Leader:  
Lord, we thank You for waking us up, clothing us in our right mind, and keeping us by Your mercy.  
We thank You for bread on the table, strength for the labor, wisdom for the work, and grace for the household.

People:  
Amen. Thank You, Lord.

Leader:  
We come not in our own strength, but leaning on Your everlasting arms.  
Search us, Lord; forgive us, cleanse us, and make our hearts right before You.  
Teach us to seek first Your kingdom and Your righteousness, and to receive every good gift with humble and generous hearts.

People:  
Lord, have mercy.

Leader:  
{{petition}}

Scripture:  
The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want.  
He maketh me to lie down in green pastures:  
he leadeth me beside the still waters.  
He restoreth my soul:  
he leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name's sake.  
Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death,  
I will fear no evil: for thou art with me;  
thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.  
Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies:  
thou anointest my head with oil; my cup runneth over.  
Surely goodness and mercy shall follow me all the days of my life:  
and I will dwell in the house of the LORD for ever.

People:  
Amen.

Spiritual:  
There is a balm in Gilead,  
To make the wounded whole;  
There is a balm in Gilead,  
To heal the sin-sick soul.

Leader:  
Lord, where there is lack, give daily bread.  
Where there is debt, give discipline, mercy, and a way forward.  
Where there is closed opportunity, open the right door in Your time.  
Where there is weary labor, renew strength.  
Where there is confusion, give wisdom.  
Where there is fear about tomorrow, speak peace today.

Bless the work of honest hands.  
Guide decisions in business, employment, study, skill, and service.  
Let the household be ordered with gratitude, patience, and care.  
Let income be used with wisdom, savings be guarded with prudence, generosity be practiced with joy, and every blessing return praise to You.

People:  
Yes, Lord.

Leader:  
We remember the whole community before You:  
those looking for work, those underpaid and overlooked, those carrying family burdens, those raising children, those caring for elders, those whose cupboards are thin, and those who do not know how the next bill will be paid.  
Lord, make a way with justice and mercy.  
Let nobody be ashamed at the table of need, and let Your people stand with one another in love.

Scripture:  
Give us this day our daily bread.

People:  
Amen. Daily bread, Lord.

Leader:  
Now, Lord, teach us to pray:

All:  
Our Father which art in heaven,  
Hallowed be thy name.  
Thy kingdom come.  
Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.  
Give us this day our daily bread.  
And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.  
And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil:  
For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever.  
Amen.

Leader:  
Father, we receive Your provision by mercy, not by bargain; by grace, not by greed; by Your holy will, not by control.  
Keep this prayer rooted in Jesus Christ, covered by the love of the Father, and guided by the Holy Spirit.  
Let every increase be clean, every success be humble, every home be thankful, and every blessing serve love, justice, and praise.

In Jesus’ name.

People:  
Amen. Amen. Amen.
