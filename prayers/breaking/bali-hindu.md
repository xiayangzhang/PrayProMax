## 印尼 / 巴厘印度教

Om awighnam astu namo siddham.

Saṅkalpa:  
{{petition}}  
Ida Sang Hyang Widhi Wasa sākṣī.

Om bhūr bhuvaḥ svaḥ  
tat savitur vareṇyaṃ  
bhargo devasya dhīmahi  
dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt

Om Nārāyaṇa evedaṃ sarvaṃ  
yad bhūtaṃ yac ca bhavyam  
niṣkalaṅko nirañjano nirvikalpo nirākhyātaḥ  
śuddho devo eko Nārāyaṇaḥ  
na dvitīyo 'sti kaścana

Om tvaṃ śivaḥ tvaṃ mahādevaḥ  
īśvaraḥ parameśvaraḥ  
brahmā viṣṇuś ca rudraś ca  
puruṣaḥ parikīrtitaḥ

Om pāpo 'haṃ pāpakarmāhaṃ  
pāpātmā pāpasambhavaḥ  
trāhi māṃ puṇḍarīkākṣa  
sarvapāpahara hare

Om kṣamasva māṃ mahādeva  
sarvaprāṇi hitaṅkara  
māṃ mocaya sarvapāpebhyaḥ  
pālayasva sadā śiva

Ida Sang Hyang Widhi Wasa,  
愿厄难消解，业障清明，困境开散；  
愿所行不越礼序，不扰供养，不犯清净，  
愿净水、净心、净语、净业，皆归于安和。

Om kṣantavyaḥ kāyiko doṣaḥ  
kṣantavyo vāciko mama  
kṣantavyo mānaso doṣaḥ  
tat pramādāt kṣamasva mām

Om śuddhiḥ śuddhiḥ śuddhiḥ  
Om kṣemaṃ bhavatu  
Om maṅgalaṃ bhavatu  
Om śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ Om.
