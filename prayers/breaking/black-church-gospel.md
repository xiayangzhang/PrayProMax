## 黑人教会传统

Father God, in the name of Jesus, we come before You.

Leader:  
Lord, we thank You for waking us up, clothing us in our right mind, and keeping us by Your mercy.

People:  
Amen. Thank You, Lord.

Leader:  
We come not in our own strength, but leaning on Your everlasting arms.  
Search us, Lord; forgive us, cleanse us, and make our hearts right before You.

People:  
Lord, have mercy.

Leader:  
For every burdened heart, for deliverance from trouble, bondage, fear, and every heavy obstacle, and for all who are standing in need of prayer, we lift our hearts to You.

Scripture:  
The LORD is my light and my salvation; whom shall I fear?  
the LORD is the strength of my life; of whom shall I be afraid?

People:  
Amen.

Leader:  
{{petition}}

People:  
Yes, Lord.

Leader:  
We put on the whole armour of God.  
We stand having our loins girt about with truth,  
and having on the breastplate of righteousness;  
our feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace;  
above all, taking the shield of faith,  
wherewith we shall be able to quench all the fiery darts of the wicked.  
We take the helmet of salvation,  
and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God.

People:  
Amen. Cover us, Lord.

Spiritual:  
Wade in the water,  
Wade in the water, children,  
Wade in the water,  
God's gonna trouble the water.

Leader:  
Lord Jesus, lead us through the waters and out of distress.  
Break the grip of despair.  
Loose the knot of confusion.  
Lift the weight of shame.  
Deliver us from evil, and guide our feet into peace.  
Let no fear rule the heart, no darkness hide the path, and no trouble speak louder than Your promise.

People:  
Lord, make a way.

Leader:  
For the living, keep them.  
For the weary, strengthen them.  
For the lonely, surround them with love.  
For the oppressed, bring justice with mercy.  
For those in hardship, open doors that honor You.  
For all who cry in the night, remind them: weeping may endure for a night, but joy cometh in the morning.

Scripture:  
Nay, in all these things we are more than conquerors through him that loved us.  
For I am persuaded, that neither death, nor life, nor angels, nor principalities, nor powers,  
nor things present, nor things to come,  
Nor height, nor depth, nor any other creature,  
shall be able to separate us from the love of God, which is in Christ Jesus our Lord.

People:  
Amen.

Leader:  
Now, Lord, teach us to pray:

All:  
Our Father which art in heaven,  
Hallowed be thy name.  
Thy kingdom come.  
Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.  
Give us this day our daily bread.  
And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.  
And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil:  
For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever.  
Amen.

Leader:  
Father, keep our prayer humble, rooted in Your Word, filled with love, and free from fear, pride, coercion, or harm.  
Let our praise honor You, let our hope rest in Christ, and let our help come by the Holy Spirit.

We seal this prayer in faith, not by might, nor by power, but by Your Spirit.  
In Jesus' name.

People:  
Amen. Amen. Amen.
