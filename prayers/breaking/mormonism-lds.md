## 摩门教（LDS）

Dear Heavenly Father,

We thank Thee for Thy mercy, for the Atonement of Thy Son Jesus Christ, and for the comfort and guidance of the Holy Ghost.  
We acknowledge Jesus Christ as our Savior and Redeemer, and we seek the light of the Holy Ghost to help us discern truth, repent sincerely, and choose righteousness.

{{petition}}

Please grant peace to our hearts, clarity to our minds, strength to our bodies, and courage to keep Thy commandments.  
May the power of Jesus Christ’s Atonement cleanse, lift, and renew us.  
May we be guided to wise counsel, humble repentance, fasting and prayer as appropriate, and loving support from family and faithful servants.

If there is danger or spiritual oppression, may those who hold proper Melchizedek Priesthood authority act only as authorized, in the name of Jesus Christ, with humility and faith.  
May Thy will be done, and may we receive protection, release, and renewed hope according to faith in Jesus Christ.

Father, keep us within Thy revealed word, under the guidance of the Holy Ghost, and upon the covenant path.  
Let this prayer be offered with reverence, without pride, fear, secrecy, or compulsion, relying wholly upon Jesus Christ and Thy holy will.

We say these things in the name of Jesus Christ, amen.
