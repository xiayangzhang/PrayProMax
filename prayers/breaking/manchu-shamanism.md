## 满族萨满 / 关姓堂、博、查玛

Guwan hala-i tangse neyen.

Abka enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Abka-i jalan de,  
abka-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Na enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Na-i jalan de,  
na-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mafa,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i dolo tehe mafa,  
hala-i dolo tehe mafa,  
ere gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mama,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i dolo tehe mama,  
hala-i dolo tehe mama,  
ere gisun be donji.

Tangse-i enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i weceku,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i tangse de,  
ere niyalma-i jobolon be alambi,  
weile-i erehe be alambi,  
ehe jugūn ci tucire be alambi.

Neyen be jafambi,  
gisun be tucibumbi,  
enduri de alambi,  
mafa mama de alambi,  
tangse de alambi.

Abka enduri,  
jobolon be donji.  
Na enduri,  
weile be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mafa,  
ehe be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mama,  
faksi be donji.  
Tangse-i enduri,  
sain jugūn be donji.

Akū oci akū seme,  
bihe oci bihe seme,  
majige be majige seme,  
ambalinggū be ambalinggū seme,  
faksi be waliyambi,  
jobolon be waliyambi,  
weile-i ehe be waliyambi,  
sain jugūn be karmambi.

Ere niyalma-i beye be karmambi,  
ere niyalma-i boo be karmambi,  
ere niyalma-i hala be karmambi,  
ere niyalma-i jugūn be karmambi.

Mafa mama,  
gisun be donji,  
sain be dosimbu,  
ehe be tucibu,  
faksi be waliyara,  
jobolon be waliyara,  
weile-i urgun be selgiyere,  
mujilen be bolgo obure.

Tangse-i enduri,  
weile be acabumbi,  
gisun be acabumbi,  
jobolon ci tucire be acabumbi,  
sain jugūn de yabure be acabumbi.

Abka enduri de wesihun,  
na enduri de wesihun,  
Guwan hala-i mafa de wesihun,  
Guwan hala-i mama de wesihun,  
tangse-i enduri de wesihun.

Donjiha be donjiha seme,  
aliha be aliha seme,  
jifi seme jihe,  
gene seme genehe.

Enduri gene,  
mafa mama gene,  
tangse-i hūsun tangse de tehe.  
Gisun wajire,  
neyen wajire.  
Aisin gisun akū,  
hulhun gisun akū,  
yargiyan gisun be alaha.

谨净其心，谨谢诸神，谨安祖位。  
不冒师承，不越秘传；  
不擅行治病、送魂、查玛降神之正仪。  
愿堂中清净，门户安宁，  
身心有护，灾障远离。
