## कौल

[गुरु-वन्दना]

oṃ śrī-gurubhyo namaḥ ।  
oṃ gurave namaḥ ।  
gurur brahmā gurur viṣṇuḥ gurur devo maheśvaraḥ ।  
guruḥ sākṣāt parabrahma tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ ॥

[गणपति-वन्दना]

oṃ gaṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ ।  
vakratuṇḍa mahākāya sūryakoṭisamaprabha ।  
nirvighnaṃ kuru me deva sarvakāryeṣu sarvadā ॥

[सङ्कल्प]

mama upātta-samasta-durita-kṣaya-dvāra  
śrī-parādevī-prītyarthaṃ  
duḥkha-vighna-vipatti-kṣayārthaṃ  
karma-bandha-śamanārthaṃ  
pāśa-cheda-śānti-rakṣā-prāyaścitta-siddhyarthaṃ  
idaṃ prārthanā-japaṃ kariṣye ॥

[कुल-देवी-आवाहन]

oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ parādevyai namaḥ ।  
oṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ kulāyai namaḥ ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ śrī-mātṛkāyai namaḥ ॥

he parāmbike, he kula-devī, he śiva-śakti-maye ।  
anādy-avidyā-pāśān śamayā ।  
duritāni kṣapaya ।  
vighnān nivāraya ।  
bhayaṃ śāntimayaṃ kuru ।  
andhakāraṃ prakāśamayaṃ kuru ॥

[मन्त्र-जप]

oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ parādevyai namaḥ ।  
oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ parādevyai namaḥ ।  
oṃ aiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ parādevyai namaḥ ॥

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ॥

[प्रार्थना]

{{petition}}

na kasyacid duḥkhaṃ kāmaye ।  
na kasyacid hāniṃ kāmaye ।  
na kasyacid āyur-bhāgya-kṣayaṃ kāmaye ।  
kevalaṃ śāntiṃ rakṣāṃ pāśa-chedaṃ ca prārthaye ॥

tryambakaṃ yajāmahe sugandhiṃ puṣṭivardhanam ।  
urvārukam iva bandhanān mṛtyor mukṣīya mā'mṛtāt ॥

[शान्ति]

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ sarve santu nirāmayāḥ ।  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ॥

oṃ dyauḥ śāntir antarīkṣaṃ śāntiḥ pṛthivī śāntir āpaḥ śāntir oṣadhayaḥ śāntiḥ ।  
vanaspatayaḥ śāntir viśve devāḥ śāntir brahma śāntiḥ sarvaṃ śāntiḥ śāntir eva śāntiḥ sā mā śāntir edhi ॥

[समर्पण]

kāyena vācā manasendriyair vā  
buddhyātmanā vā prakṛteḥ svabhāvāt ।  
karomi yad yat sakalaṃ parasmai  
śrī-parādevyai samarpaṇam astu ॥

[क्षमा]

yat kṛtaṃ tu mayā devi mantra-hīnaṃ kriyā-hīnam ।  
tat sarvaṃ pūrṇatāṃ yātu tvat-prasādāt parāmbike ॥

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante'harniśaṃ mayā ।  
dāso'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvari ॥

[फल-समर्पण / शान्ति-रक्षा-प्रायश्चित्त-निवेदन]

asya prārthanā-japasya yat phalaṃ bhavet  
tat sarva-bhūta-hitāya, śāntyai, rakṣāyai, prāyaścittāya ca samarpyate ।  
māraṇa-vidveṣaṇa-hiṃsā-saṅkalpāḥ sarvathā śāmyantu ।  
śuddha-bhāvena śiva-śakti-prasādena pāśāḥ śāmyantu, cittam ujjvalaṃ bhavatu, mārgaḥ prasīdatu ॥

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ॥
