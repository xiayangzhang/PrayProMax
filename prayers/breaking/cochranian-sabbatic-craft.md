## 现代术士派（Cochranian、Sabbatic Craft）

In the name of the Old One,  
Master of the Crooked Path,  
Keeper of the hidden fire,  
I hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
let this ring be whole,  
let no false word enter,  
let no oath be broken,  
let no secret be profaned.

{{petition}}

No stolen oath is spoken here.  
No hidden word is claimed here.  
Only the open charm is set,  
only the rightful prayer is made.

Knot unknotted, cord uncorded,  
mark unmarked, and hook unhooked;  
what is not rightful is released,  
what is not sworn is severed,  
what is not owned is returned.

Let no curse be cast,  
let no will be broken,  
let no living soul be harmed.  
Let the obstacle be removed,  
let the snare be opened,  
let the road be found beneath the dark.

By thorn that guards,  
by flame that clears,  
by breath that returns,  
by bone that remembers,  
let affliction lose its hold,  
let confusion lose its tongue,  
let fear lose its throne.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
the word is spoken,  
the work is sealed,  
the compass is whole.

Old One, receive thanks.  
If any word has reached beyond its right,  
let that word be withdrawn.  
If any road has opened where it should not open,  
let that road be shut.  
If any shadow has gathered at the edge,  
let it be sent from this ring in peace.

Let the road be shut where it must be shut.  
Let the road be open where it must be open.  
Let each power return to its own place,  
let each boundary stand clear,  
let the heart be steady,  
let the hand be clean.

Peace to the ring,  
peace to the dead,  
peace to the living.

So mote it be.
