## Shembe

Nkulunkulu kaShembe, Baba wethu,  
siyakhuleka phambi kwakho ngokuthobeka.

Siyakudumisa, Jehova;  
siyakubonga, Nkosi;  
igama lakho malibongwe phakathi kwebandla, emakhaya, nasendleleni yethu.

{{petition}}

Baba wethu osezulwini,  
malingcweliswe igama lakho;  
mawufike umbuso wakho;  
mayenziwe intando yakho emhlabeni njengasezulwini.  
Usiphe namuhla isinkwa sethu semihla ngemihla;  
usithethelele amacala ethu,  
njengokuba nathi sibathethelela abanamacala kithi;  
ungasingenisi ekulingweni,  
kodwa usikhulule kokubi.  
Ngokuba umbuso ungowakho, namandla, nenkazimulo, kuze kube phakade.  
Amen.

Nkosi, bheka izinceku zakho ezikhala kuwe.  
Susa usizi olusindayo, izithiyo ezivala indlela, nokwesaba okuvalele inhliziyo.  
Usikhulule kokubi, usivikele ezilingweni, usiholele endleleni yokuphila, yokuthula, nokulunga.  
Makungabi khona isicelo sokulimaza omunye umuntu;  
makususwe isithiyo, kungalimali muntu, kungaphucwa muntu isibusiso sakhe.  
Yenza intando yakho, hhayi ngokuzigqaja kwethu, kodwa ngokwethemba kuwe.

Ngibeka izandla ngomkhuleko webandla, egameni lomusa wakho, Nkosi.  
Qinisa ababuthakathaka, pholisa abaphukileyo, hlanza izinhliziyo, uvikele imizi.  
Nikeza ukuthula emzimbeni, enhliziyweni, nasemoyeni.  
Makube njalo ngokwentando yakho.

Siyakubonga, Nkosi.  
Siyakudumisa, Jehova.  
Ukuthula makube sebantwini bakho, emindenini yabo, nasemhlanganweni wonke.

Nkosi, sigcine singaphenduli umkhuleko ube yisiqalekiso,  
singafuni impindiselo, singazigqaji ngamandla ethu.  
Sibophe ekuphendukeni, ekuthetheleleni, nasekuhambeni ngokuhlanzeka phambi kwakho.  
Usikhulule kokubi; usimboze ngomusa wakho; usiphe ukuthula kwakho.

Amen.
