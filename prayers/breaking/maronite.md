## الكنيسة المارونية

B-shmo d-Abo w-d-Bro w-d-Ruho d-Qudsho, Had Aloho shariro. Amin.

Qadishat Aloho,  
Qadishat Hayelthono,  
Qadishat lo Moyutho,  
ethraham ‘alayn.

Shubho l-Abo w-la-Bro w-la-Ruho d-Qudsho,  
men ‘olam w-‘adamo l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Abun d-bashmayo,  
netqadash shmokh.  
Tite malkuthokh.  
Nehwe sebyonokh,  
aykano d-bashmayo oph bar‘o.  
Haw lan lahmo d-sunqonan yawmono.  
Washboq lan hawbayn wahtohayn,  
aykano doph hnan shbaqn l-hayyobayn.  
Wlo ta‘lan l-nesyuno,  
elo fasso lan men bisho.  
Metul d-dilokh hi malkutho w-haylo w-teshbuhto  
l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.

Shlom lekh Maryam,  
mlayat taybutho,  
Moran ‘amekh.  
Mbarakhto at b-neshe,  
w-mbarakh hu piro d-karsekh,  
Maran Yeshua.  
O qadishto Maryam,  
Yoldath Aloho,  
saloy hlofayn hnaye,  
hosho w-bsho‘at mawthan. Amin.

{{petition}}

Lo nish’al nuksono l-nosh,  
w-lo hoblo l-akhayn,  
elo nish’al shayno, tyobutho,  
w-urho d-haye b-sebyonokh qadisho.

B-shfa‘otho d-Yoldath Aloho Maryam,  
w-d-Mor Maron,  
w-d-Mor Efram,  
w-d-Mor Ya‘qub,  
w-d-Mor Sharbel,  
w-d-kulhon qadisho,  
Moryo etraham ‘alayn.

Dkar, Moryo, l-‘abdayk w-l-amhotokh,  
aylen d-qaymin b-‘aqo w-b-teshwiqtho,  
w-aylen d-ba‘en purqono men ‘yade bisho,  
men ru‘oth rish‘o, men heshokh d-lebo,  
w-men kul urho d-mathyo l-mawto.  
Ahlan b-nuhrokh,  
qum ‘amman b-slibokh,  
w-hdar ‘uqothan l-sabro.

Dkar, Moryo, l-‘anide mhaymne,  
w-nahhar lehon b-nuhrak d-lo ‘aber;  
w-l-hnaye, shadar rahme,  
shayno, husoyo, w-haylo d-tyobutho.

Moryo, nhoor lan b-nuhrokh,  
nattar lan b-slibokh,  
shadar ‘alayn rahmayk,  
w-ahlan b-shaynokh.  
B-sebyonokh qadisho,  
la b-haylo d-mellin,  
ella b-taybuthokh w-b-rahmayk,  
nehwe lan purqono w-huroro.

Lakh shubho,  
l-Abokh iqoro,  
w-l-Ruho d-Qudsho segedto,  
hosho w-bkul zban  
w-l-‘olam ‘olmin. Amin.
