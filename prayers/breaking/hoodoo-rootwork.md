## Hoodoo / Rootwork

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.

I stand before the Most High God with clean speech and a steady heart.  
Let this prayer be for the breaking of affliction, the lifting of burdens, the clearing of crossed conditions, and the opening of a righteous road out of trouble.  
Let no falsehood, coercion, revenge, or wrongful hand be in it.

וְהוֹצִיא כָאוֹר צִדְקֶךָ  
וּמִשְׁפָּטֶךָ כַּצָּהֳרָיִם׃

I speak no harm into the innocent.  
I bind no unwilling heart.  
I seek no ruin, sickness, loss, or death for any living soul.  
I do not ask to take another’s portion, shorten another’s days, or darken another’s road.  
I ask only that what is crooked be made straight, what is unjust be removed, and what is heavy be lifted by the mercy of God.

If this prayer touches the living, let it touch them in justice and mercy.  
If harm has been sent, let it be turned aside without cruelty.  
If judgment is needed, let judgment belong to the Most High.  
If release is granted, let it come with humility, wisdom, and protection.

Set this petition under the judgment of the Most High.  
What is righteous, establish it.  
What is crooked, remove it.  
What is not mine to command, return it to God.

Lord, I take back every crooked word, every proud demand, every harmful wish, and every petition not fit to stand before You.  
Cleanse my mouth, cleanse my hands, cleanse my heart, and cleanse my road.  
Cover me under Your mercy and keep me from repeating wrongful work.  
Let this prayer end in protection, repentance, release, and peace.
