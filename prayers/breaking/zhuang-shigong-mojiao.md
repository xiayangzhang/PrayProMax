## 壮族师公 / 麽教

[净坛报坛]

香灯已列，清水已陈，坛面已净，门户已开。  
今为破厄解难、断障出困，至诚报坛；不求伤人，不求夺福，只求邪障远离、道路转明。

[请师祖]

Cingj boux sai mo daengz.  
Cingj boux swhgung daengz.  
Cingj gyoengq swhbo daengz.  
Daihnaj miz dangh, daihlaeng miz roen.  
Gyoengq swhbo yawj, gyoengq swhbo couh.

[请布洛陀]

Cingj Bouxloxdoh daengz.  
Bouxloxdoh gwnz mbwn, Bouxloxdoh laj deih.  
Bouxloxdoh dingh leix, Bouxloxdoh dingh lwg.  
Cingj Bouxloxdoh yawj gij nanh,  
cingj Bouxloxdoh rox gij ciengz.

[请姆洛甲]

Cingj Mehloeggyap daengz.  
Mehloeggyap hoz vunz, Mehloeggyap hoz lwg.  
Mehloeggyap yawj ranz, Mehloeggyap yawj sim.  
Cingj Mehloeggyap yawj sim,  
cingj Mehloeggyap hoz vunz.

[告祖]

Cingj goeng meh daengz.  
Ranz miz mbaw, vunz miz mingz.  
Cingj goeng meh yawj ranz,  
cingj goeng meh yawj sim.  
Gij nanh mbouj hawj hwnj,  
gij sa mbouj hawj daengz.

[告社土]

Cingj deih boux, cingj lungz ranz.  
Gwnz mbwn miz dingh, laj deih miz roen.  
Cingj deih boux dingh deih,  
cingj lungz ranz dingh dou.  
Roen dam cingj hawj doeng,  
roen doek cingj hawj hwnj.

[报明事项]

Sienq haeux: phá厄解难，断障离困。  
Mbouj yawj dox, mbouj gangj laux,  
gangj ndaej caemh leix, gangj ndaej caemh fap.  
Mbouj dox vunz, mbouj hawj vunz caengh;  
gij mbouj cingj, hawj ok; gij mbouj swng, hawj bae.

[正行唱诵：通用麽唱]

Fwen mo hwnj, fwen mo loengh.  
Hwnj bae gwnz mbwn, loengh daeuj laj deih.

[正行：请师祖照坛]

Cingj swhgung yawj roen,  
cingj swhbo yawj sim.  
Cingj gyoengq swhbo dawz fwen,  
cingj gyoengq swhbo dawz roen.

[正行：请布洛陀定礼]

Cingj Bouxloxdoh dingh leix.  
Gij ciengz mbouj loek,  
gij sienq mbouj rox.  
Cingj Bouxloxdoh dingh leix,  
hawj leix cingq, hawj roen cingq.

[正行：请姆洛甲护生]

Cingj Mehloeggyap hoz lwg.  
Gij sim mbouj dingh, hawj sim dingh.  
Gij ndang mbouj an, hawj ndang an.  
Cingj Mehloeggyap yawj ranz,  
hawj ranz ndei, hawj vunz ndei.

[正行：告祖定家]

Cingj goeng meh dingh ranz.  
Gij ciengz mbouj loek,  
gij nanh mbouj gaen.  
Cingj gyoengq goeng meh dawz roen,  
hawj roen dam baenz roen ngoenz,  
hawj sim doek baenz sim dingh.

[正行：告社土定寨]

Cingj deih lungz dingh mbanj.  
Cingj deih lungz dawz roen.  
Mbouj hawj sa doek,  
mbouj hawj ok laux.  
Lungz dingh, deih dingh,  
ranz dingh, vunz dingh.

[赶鬼押煞科]

Cingj swhgung dawz fap,  
cingj swhbo dawz bing.  
Gij mbouj cingj, ndaej ok.  
Gij mbouj swng, ndaej bae.  
Gij sa, gij nanh, gij ciengz dam,  
bae roen gvaq bya, bae roen gvaq dah.  
Mbouj dauq ranz, mbouj dauq dou,  
mbouj dauq sim, mbouj dauq ndang.  
Fap cingq, bing cingq,  
dou cingq, roen cingq.  
Gij ndei dauq, gij laeg doek;  
gij fuk dauq, gij sa bae.

[纳福定身]

Cingj Bouxloxdoh dawz fuk dauq.  
Cingj Mehloeggyap dawz an dauq.  
Cingj goeng meh dawz roen ndei dauq.  
Cingj deih lungz dawz dingh dauq.  
Dauq sim, dauq ndang,  
dauq ranz, dauq dou.  
Roen hoi, dou hoi,  
sim dingh, ndang an.

[辞布洛陀]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq Bouxloxdoh dauq gwnz.

[辞姆洛甲]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq Mehloeggyap dauq dieg.

[辞祖先]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq goeng meh dauq ranz.

[辞社土龙神]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq deih lungz dauq deih.

[送师祖]

Fwen mo gaeq, fwen mo soengq.  
Soengq swhgung dauq dangh,  
soengq swhbo dauq roen.

[封坛回向]

Dou laeg, roen laeg.  
Fwen gaeq, fap gaeq.  
Gij ndei dauq ranz,  
gij ndei dauq vunz,  
gij ndei dauq sim.  
Mbouj loek fwen,  
mbouj loek leix.  
Dingh.
