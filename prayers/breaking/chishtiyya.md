Allāhumma lā najʿalu dhālik bi-ẓulmin li-aḥad,  
wa-lā bi-qaṭʿi rizqi aḥad,  
wa-lā bi-ḍarari barīʾ,

Allāhumma in kāna fī qalbinā siḥrun aw ḥasadun aw khawfun aw ʿādatun muhlikatun aw waswasatun muḍilla,  
fa-abṭilhu bi-kalāmika, wa-shfihi bi-dhikrika, wa-ṭahhirhu bi-nūri tawḥīdika,  
innaka anta l-Qawiyyu l-Matīn, wa-anta ḥasbunā wa-niʿma l-wakīl.

Niʿma l-mawlā wa-niʿma n-naṣīr.

Allāhumma in kāna fī hādhā dh-dhikri wa-hādhihi t-tilāwati wa-hādhā s-samāʿi ṣ-ṣāmiti thawābun,  
fa-awṣil thawābahu ilā rūḥi sayyidinā Muḥammadin ṣallā llāhu ʿalayhi wa-sallam,  
wa-ilā arwāḥi l-anbiyāʾi wa-ṣ-ṣiddīqīna wa-sh-shuhadāʾi wa-ṣ-ṣāliḥīn,  
wa-ilā mashāyikhinā fī ṭarīqati l-Chishtiyya,  
wa-jʿalhu maqbulan ʿindaka yā Arḥama r-rāḥimīn.
