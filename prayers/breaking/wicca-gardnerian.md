## 威卡 Gardnerian

In this place, in this hour, we stand between the worlds,  
with clean hands, clear speech, and a will held in Perfect Love and Perfect Trust.

I conjure thee, O Circle of Power,  
that thou beest a boundary between the world of men and the realms of the Mighty Ones,  
a guardian and a meeting-place for the working of breaking ill fortune, loosening harmful bonds, and finding the way out of hardship.

Lady of the Moon, Great Mother, Bright One, Queen of the secret place,  
hear the words spoken within this circle.

Whenever ye have need of anything,  
once in the month, and better it be when the moon is full,  
then shall ye assemble in some desert place,  
or in a forest all together join  
to adore the potent spirit of your queen.

Horned One, Lord of the wildwood, keeper of life and death,  
stand apart in thy own power and witness this working.

{{petition}}

Before the Lord we speak the boundary:  
let no harm be sent to any soul,  
let no will be bound against its truth,  
let no curse, coercion, or domination take root here;  
only what is false, harmful, and ill-wrought may fall away.

Let this prayer be for release, cleansing, and right passage,  
harming none, binding none against the true will,  
and turning aside all falsehood, fear, and ill-wrought force.

Eko, Eko, Azarak,  
Eko, Eko, Zomelak.

By earth, let the feet find firm ground again.  
By air, let the mind be cleared of shadow and confusion.  
By fire, let courage rise without wrath or cruelty.  
By water, let grief and obstruction be washed into peace.

Lady of the Moon, receive this prayer.  
Horned One, witness this prayer.  
Mighty Ones of the circle, keep it within right measure.

As the cup and the bread are blessed in fellowship,  
so may the work be whole:  
for release from ill fortune, for the undoing of harmful entanglement,  
for passage out of hardship,  
and for healing that harms none.

May all that is not rightful be unmade.  
May all that is not loving be dissolved.  
May all that is not free be released.  
May what remains be clear, whole, and blessed.

The blessing is given without bargain, debt, or demand.  
The prayer is sealed in Perfect Love and Perfect Trust.  
The Lady’s light abides; the Horned One’s strength abides.  
The circle is open, yet unbroken.  
Merry meet, and merry part, and merry meet again.
