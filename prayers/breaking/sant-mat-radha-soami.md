**Nirmal man aur rukh**

Satgurū Dayāl, Rādhāsoāmī Dayāl,  
man ko namratā, satya, dayā aur kṣamā meṅ sthir karo;  
bhay, uljhan, vipatti, andhakār aur bhārī karm-bandhan ke bīch  
surat ko tere hukam aur terī dayā kī or pher do.

**Samarpan aur maryādā**

jo kuchh hai, terā hai;  
jo phal ho, hukam meṅ ho;  
jo bandhan ṭūṭe, vah ahankār se nahīṅ, terī dayā se ṭūṭe;  
jo rāstā khule, vah satya, dayā, sevā aur bhakti ke sāth khule.
