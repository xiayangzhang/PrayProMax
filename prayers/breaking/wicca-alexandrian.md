## 威卡 Alexandrian

I stand within the Circle, in perfect love and perfect trust.  
Let all within this rite be bounded by the Rede:  
An it harm none, do what ye will.

By Air, by Fire, by Water, by Earth,  
this Circle is cast and sealed.  
Between the worlds, and in the presence of the Mighty Ones,  
let this rite be held for the breaking of misfortune, the release of burdens, and the opening of a way out of hardship.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

O Great Mother, Lady of the Moon,  
Listen to the words of the Great Mother.  
Receive this prayer for the dissolving of ill fortune,  
for the loosening of harmful bonds,  
for the cleansing of old burden, shadow, and obstruction.

O Lord of the Greenwood, Horned One,  
guardian of life, death, and return,  
Receive this prayer for courage, boundary, and renewal,  
that what is worn out may pass away,  
and what is true and living may return.

Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak,  
Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak.

{{petition}}

Within right will, right boundary, and harm to none,  
let all that binds unjustly be released.  
Let no suffering be sent to any being.  
Let no curse be made, no will be forced, no debt be laid.  
Only cleansing, severing, protection, and peace are sought.

So mote it be.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

The Circle is open, but unbroken.  
Merry meet, merry part, and merry meet again.

All excess power returns to Earth.  
All wrongful crossing is undone.  
All fear is grounded, all malice is dismissed,  
and this rite is sealed again by the Rede:  
An it harm none, do what ye will.

Blessed be.
