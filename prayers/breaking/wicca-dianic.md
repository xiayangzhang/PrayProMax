## 威卡 Dianic

By earth and moon, by breath and flame,  
this place is made sacred.

I cast this circle as a boundary of truth,  
a circle of protection,  
a circle of blessing,  
a circle of the Goddess.

O Diana,  
Bright Lady of the Moon,  
Mother of the wild places,  
Keeper of women’s mysteries,  
be present in this circle.

{{petition}}

Let this prayer touch only what may be touched in freedom.  
Let it bind no unwilling heart.  
Let it cross no oath-bound door.  
Let it harm none.

O Diana,  
look upon this hardship and upon the one who seeks release.

Where there is fear, let there be clear boundary.  
Where there is grief, let there be remembrance.  
Where there is confusion, let there be inner knowing.  
Where there is depletion, let there be renewal.  
Where there is bondage, let there be release.  
Where there is obstruction, let the path open without injury to any being.  
Where there is love, let it remain free.

All acts of love and pleasure are my rituals.

I place within this circle:  
clean breath, honest will, a small light freely given,  
and the wish to be cleansed without coercion,  
released without revenge,  
and guided without harm.

By the waxing, full, and waning Moon,  
by the dark and returning Moon,  
may what is true be strengthened,  
may what is harmful be dissolved,  
may what belongs to me return to me,  
may what is not mine depart in peace.

May misfortune be broken as a cord that no longer binds.  
May burdens be loosened as knots under moonlight.  
May old sorrow be washed from the threshold.  
May the way forward be seen, step by step,  
with courage, clarity, and right unfolding.

An it harm none, do what ye will.

O Diana,  
I thank you for your presence.  
Remain if you will,  
depart if you must,  
with blessing between us.

The circle is open,  
but unbroken.  
Merry meet,  
merry part,  
and merry meet again.

Let this working remain within freedom, blessing, and clear consent.  
Let it bind no unwilling heart, cross no oath-bound door, and harm none.  
So may the released burden depart in peace,  
and so may the cleansed path be guarded by the light of the Moon.
