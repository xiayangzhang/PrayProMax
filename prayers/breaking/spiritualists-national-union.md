## Spiritualists' National Union

In stillness, harmony, and sincerity, we open this prayer.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, source of love, truth, and life,

We acknowledge with reverence the Seven Principles:

The Fatherhood of God.  
The Brotherhood of Man.  
The Communion of Spirits and the Ministry of Angels.  
The continuous existence of the human soul.  
Personal Responsibility.  
Compensation and Retribution hereafter for all the good and evil deeds done on earth.  
Eternal progress open to every human soul.

{{petition}}

May love surround this situation.  
May truth guide every thought, word, and action.  
May healing, comfort, and strength be present according to the highest good.  
May there be clarity without fear, comfort without coercion, guidance without domination, and peace without any claim beyond truth.

Where distress is present, may calm be near.  
Where obstacles stand, may a right path gently open.  
Where old burdens remain, may wisdom, courage, and personal responsibility help release them.  
Where sorrow is present, may consolation be felt.  
Where conflict is present, may brotherhood, compassion, and understanding arise.

We do not ask for harm to any person, nor for suffering to be returned to any source.  
We ask only that obstacles be removed in love, that harmful patterns be dissolved in truth, and that all may move toward healing and eternal progress.

May the living be strengthened in hope, honesty, and goodwill.  
May those in the Spirit World be remembered with love and peace.  
May the communion of spirits and the ministry of angels uplift, comfort, and inspire only according to the highest good.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, let this prayer serve only love, truth, healing, comfort, spiritual growth, goodwill, and personal responsibility.  
May no fear, dependency, coercion, or false certainty remain within it.  
May it be held in humility, kindness, and peace.

Amen.
