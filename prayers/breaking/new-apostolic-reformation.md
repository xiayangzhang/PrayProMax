## 新使徒改革

{{petition}}

Πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς·  
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου·  
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου·  
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου,  
ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς·  
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον δὸς ἡμῖν σήμερον·  
καὶ ἄφες ἡμῖν τὰ ὀφειλήματα ἡμῶν,  
ὡς καὶ ἡμεῖς ἀφήκαμεν τοῖς ὀφειλέταις ἡμῶν·  
καὶ μὴ εἰσενέγκῃς ἡμᾶς εἰς πειρασμόν,  
ἀλλὰ ῥῦσαι ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τοῦ πονηροῦ.

Father, I confess and renounce every sin, fear, bitterness, accusation, idolatry, occult involvement, ungodly vow, inherited pattern, false word, and agreement with despair. I place this whole matter under the blood and lordship of Jesus Christ.

I choose to forgive, and I release all people from judgment and vengeance. I do not curse, control, shame, or harm any person. I reject only the works of darkness, lies, bondage, sin, oppression, and every unclean influence that stands against the will of God.

הַיֹּשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן  
בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי  
אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃

Ἰδοὺ δέδωκα ὑμῖν τὴν ἐξουσίαν  
τοῦ πατεῖν ἐπάνω ὄφεων καὶ σκορπίων,  
καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν δύναμιν τοῦ ἐχθροῦ,  
καὶ οὐδὲν ὑμᾶς οὐ μὴ ἀδικήσει.

ὃ ἐὰν δήσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται δεδεμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς,  
καὶ ὃ ἐὰν λύσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται λελυμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς.

Ὑποτάγητε οὖν τῷ θεῷ·  
ἀντίστητε δὲ τῷ διαβόλῳ,  
καὶ φεύξεται ἀφ᾽ ὑμῶν.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I bind every demonic assignment, accusation, covenant of darkness, curse, word of death, spirit of fear, spirit of confusion, spirit of oppression, and every power of the enemy operating through affliction and bondage. I command every unclean influence to leave and not return, according to the authority of Jesus Christ.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I loose truth, repentance, faith, holiness, peace, protection, discernment, healing, wisdom, endurance, and obedience to the will of God. Let every path of escape ordained by the Father be opened, and let every snare of darkness be broken under the lordship of Christ.

I cut off every ungodly soul tie, false burden, occult claim, generational bondage, spoken curse, inner vow, fear agreement, and demonic access point connected with this distress. I declare that the handwriting against me is canceled in Christ, and that every hostile power is disarmed under the cross of Jesus Christ.

Καὶ αὐτοὶ ἐνίκησαν αὐτὸν  
διὰ τὸ αἷμα τοῦ ἀρνίου  
καὶ διὰ τὸν λόγον τῆς μαρτυρίας αὐτῶν.

Father, fill every cleansed place with the Holy Spirit, the peace of Christ, the fear of the Lord, wisdom, purity, steadfast love, and soundness of mind. Establish Your kingdom order, close every door to torment, and surround this life with Your protection.

Father, keep this prayer within Your mercy, holiness, and biblical love. Let no word become fear, control, accusation, or harm toward any human being. I bless people and resist only Satan, evil spirits, sin, lies, bondage, and the works of darkness. If fear, compulsion, conflict, or crisis arises, lead me into repentance, forgiveness, wise pastoral care, safety, and proper help. I thank You, Father, and seal this prayer in the name of Jesus Christ.

Ἀμήν.
