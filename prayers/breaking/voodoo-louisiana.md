## 路易斯安那伏都 Voodoo

Bon Dieu, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit,  
I set my mouth clean and my intention straight.  
I speak this prayer for uncrossing, release from burden, and a clean road out of trouble.  
Let this hand be named **Clean Road Hand**, and let its prayer be bound only to what is rightful, merciful, and free from harm.

Pater noster, qui es in caelis,  
sanctificetur nomen tuum.  
Adveniat regnum tuum.  
Fiat voluntas tua,  
sicut in caelo et in terra.  
Panem nostrum quotidianum da nobis hodie,  
et dimitte nobis debita nostra,  
sicut et nos dimittimus debitoribus nostris.  
Et ne nos inducas in tentationem,  
sed libera nos a malo.  
Amen.

Holy ancestors of my blood and my house, witness only what is clean in this prayer.  
Guard the road, steady the feet, cool the head, and turn aside what has no rightful claim.  
Let the burden fall to the ground; let the spirit stand upright; let the way be made plain.

Gloria Patri, et Filio, et Spiritui Sancto.  
Sicut erat in principio, et nunc, et semper,  
et in saecula saeculorum.  
Amen.

By the mercy of Bon Dieu, any word spoken beyond rightful measure is stopped.  
Any forced prayer, tangled promise, unfinished vow, or unclean bond is loosened and forgiven.  
Let clean repentance wash the mouth; let the ancestors be cooled and at peace;  
let this work close within its proper boundary, under protection, humility, and grace.  
Amen. Ashe.
