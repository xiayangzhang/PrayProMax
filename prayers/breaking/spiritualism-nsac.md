## Spiritualism / NSAC

Infinite Intelligence, God of love, law, and life,  
I come in reverence, in sincerity, and in personal responsibility,  
holding this prayer for release from hardship, obstruction, and burdensome conditions before Thee.

Our Father which art in heaven,  
Hallowed be thy name.  
Thy kingdom come.  
Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.  
Give us this day our daily bread.  
And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.  
And lead us not into temptation,  
but deliver us from evil:  
For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever.  
Amen.

I affirm:  
We believe in Infinite Intelligence.  
We believe that the phenomena of Nature, both physical and spiritual,  
are the expression of Infinite Intelligence.  
We affirm that true religion is living in obedience to Nature's Laws.  
We affirm that the existence and personal identity of the individual  
continue after the change called death.  
We affirm that communication with the so-called dead is a fact,  
scientifically proven by the phenomena of Spiritualism.  
We affirm the highest morality contained in the Golden Rule:  
Therefore all things whatsoever ye would that men should do to you,  
do ye even so to them.  
We affirm moral responsibility, and that each soul makes its own happiness  
or unhappiness as it obeys or disobeys Nature's physical and spiritual laws.  
We affirm that the doorway to reformation is never closed  
against any soul here or hereafter.  
We affirm that the precepts of Prophecy and Healing are Divine attributes  
proven through Mediumship.

{{petition}}

Let the words of my mouth, and the meditation of my heart,  
be acceptable in thy sight, O LORD, my strength, and my redeemer.

May no word spoken here replace conscience, responsibility, or truth.  
May no spirit, person, or influence be compelled.  
May all be governed by Infinite Intelligence, Divine Love, and Natural Law.  
May loving spirit helpers, only as permitted by Divine Law,  
minister peace, steadiness, and uplift without coercion or fear.

I now lay aside all anxious seeking after signs, messages, or control.  
I return to the simplicity of prayer, to quietness, to personal responsibility,  
and to obedience to Nature's physical and spiritual laws.  
Where earthly help is needed, may the path open toward wise, honest, and proper care.  
In stillness, under Infinite Intelligence, may deliverance, reform, and peace prevail.

Amen.
