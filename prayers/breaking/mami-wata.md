## Mami Wata 信仰

We stand by clean water with cool heart.  
No strange name for this mouth, no quarrel for this bowl.  
Mami Wata, Mammy Water, Nne mmiri, Yeye Omi,  
we call only your name for this prayer.

Mami Wata o, Mami Wata o,  
bia, nuru olu anyi.  
Nne mmiri, nne oma,  
mmiri di ọcha, mmiri ndu.

Mammy Water, we hail you.  
Fine lady for water, mother for river,  
she wey know deep place,  
she wey hold mirror for truth,  
she wey cool hot heart and show clean road.

Yeye Omi, iya omi,  
bo wa pelu omi funfun.  
Omi tutu, omi alaafia,  
ko si ija, ko si iro,  
alaafia ni a pe.

Mami Wata o, Mammy Water o,  
if wrong word don pass this mouth,  
make wrong word fall for water.  
If foolish calling don rise before,  
make that noise cool, make peace remain.  
If any heavy promise no get truth,  
make the false rope soften;  
make only clean word, honest heart, and rightful peace stand.

Mmiri oma, kuru ihe ojoo laa.  
Mmiri ndu, mee ka obi di jụụ.  
Nne mmiri, nara olu anyi,  
nara mmiri a,  
nara okwu udo a.

Mammy Water, no vex, no trouble.  
We withdraw rough word, proud word, and unclean request.  
We no force spirit, we no call possession,  
we no send harm, we no take another person blessing.  
Clean water for your coolness,  
white peace for the river,  
quiet heart for the road.

Mami Wata o,  
peace for water,  
peace for road,  
peace for house,  
peace for body and spirit.  
Mammy Water, we thank you.  
Nne mmiri, daalu.  
Yeye Omi, mo dupe.  
Alaafia. Udo. Peace.
