## 基督教科学派

Our Father which art in heaven,  
Our Father-Mother God, all-harmonious,

Hallowed be Thy name.  
Adorable One.

Thy kingdom come.  
Thy kingdom is come; Thou art ever-present.

Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.  
Enable us to know,—as in heaven, so on earth,—God is omnipotent, supreme.

Give us this day our daily bread;  
Give us grace for to-day; feed the famished affections;

And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.  
And Love is reflected in love;

And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil;  
And God leadeth us not into temptation, but delivereth us from sin, disease, and death.

For Thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, forever.  
For God is infinite, all-power, all Life, Truth, Love, over all, and All.

Let motive and act be purified by divine Love. Let no fear, resentment, personal will, accusation, or desire to control another have place in thought. Let consciousness be awake to duty, obedient to Truth, and gentle under the government of divine Principle.

There is no life, truth, intelligence, nor substance in matter.  
All is infinite Mind and its infinite manifestation, for God is All-in-all.  
Spirit is immortal Truth; matter is mortal error.  
Spirit is the real and eternal; matter is the unreal and temporal.  
Spirit is God, and man is His image and likeness.  
Therefore man is not material; he is spiritual.

In the light of divine Mind, every claim of calamity, bondage, inherited burden, fated obstruction, sin, disease, death, or animal magnetism is denied as having no true law, no true intelligence, no true substance, and no true power. No curse, fear, suggestion, hatred, or false belief can stand before the omnipotence of God, good.

{{petition}}

Let man be known as the image and likeness of Spirit: upright, free, beloved, guided, protected, and sustained. Let divine Love reveal that no past, no fear, no material condition, and no hostile suggestion can separate God’s idea from God’s care.

Thy kingdom come; let the reign of divine Truth, Life, and Love be established in consciousness and conduct.  
God is Spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth.  
God is Love; and Love is reflected in love.

May this prayer rest wholly in God’s will, harming no one, commanding no one’s will, accusing no person, and granting no authority to fear. May every thought be cleansed of malice, superstition, and material worship, and be held in the pure acknowledgment that God is infinite Mind, divine Principle, Life, Truth, and Love.

Amen.
