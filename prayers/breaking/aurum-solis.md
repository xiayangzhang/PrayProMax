## Aurum Solis

In stillness and in purity I stand within the Light of the Ogdoadic Star.

Agios ho Theos,  
Agios Ischyros,  
Agios Athanatos,  
eleēson hēmas.

Deo Duce, Comite Ferro.

Let the work be established in the matter of breaking adversity, releasing burdens of error and consequence, and finding the ordered way out of distress.

IAO.  
AGATHOS DAIMŌN.  
IAO.

Let that which is confused be clarified;  
let that which is harmful be withdrawn from its hold;  
let that which is tangled be loosened in due measure;  
let every adverse influence return to its proper sphere, deprived of power to afflict.

IAO. IAO. IAO.

By the Light of the Divine Mind, let fear be quieted, error corrected, and the path of ascent made visible.  
By the harmony of the sacred order, let the soul stand free from oppression and turn again toward virtue, courage, and peace.

Deo Duce, Comite Ferro.  
Agios ho Theos, Agios Ischyros, Agios Athanatos, eleēson hēmas.

The word is spoken, the measure is sealed, and the force is returned to its proper sphere.

I release all compulsion, all unfinished force, and all personal insistence.  
I withdraw the intention from coercion and return every power invoked to its rightful station in peace.  
Let purity remain; let the ward stand; let no harm be carried onward.

Agios ho Theos,  
Agios Ischyros,  
Agios Athanatos,  
eleēson hēmas.

Deo Duce, Comite Ferro.  
IAO.
