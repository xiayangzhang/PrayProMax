## 美洲原住民教会（NAC）

With humble hearts, we honor the Native American Church Roadmen, Firemen, Drummers, families, tribes, and elders who keep the road.

{{petition}}

May this blessing take nothing from another person’s road, life, or portion; remove obstacles without harming anyone.

Where the church and family songs are held, keep them with their rightful keepers; guide us by listening, repentance, forgiveness, and a straight road.

For the people, the ancestors, and the generations coming, we give thanks; may we honor without overreach, and leave the medicine and meeting ways in authorized hands. Amen.
