## 现代术士派（Cochranian、Sabbatic Craft）

In the name of the Old One,  
Master of the Crooked Path,  
Keeper of the hidden fire,  
I hallow this compass.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
let this ring be whole,  
let no false word enter,  
let no oath be broken,  
let no secret be profaned.

{{petition}}

No stolen oath is spoken here.  
No hidden word is claimed here.  
Only the open charm is set,  
only the rightful prayer is made.

Fire in the head, light in the bone,  
the crooked road beneath the feet;  
let cunning be awakened,  
let folly be uncovered,  
let the true sign be known.

Let the eye be clear upon the page,  
let the ear be keen to the teacher’s word,  
let the hand be steady in its writing,  
let memory hold what is needful,  
let fear be cooled,  
let wandering thought be gathered,  
let diligence ripen into skill.

Let success come by rightful road,  
not by theft of another’s portion,  
not by dimming another’s lamp,  
not by breaking another’s chance,  
but by honest labour, clear wit, and fitting grace.

By stang, by stone, by thorn, by flame,  
by bone, by blood, by breath, by name,  
the word is spoken,  
the work is sealed,  
the compass is whole.

If any word has gone beyond its rightful bound,  
let it be withdrawn and made harmless.  
If any door has been opened amiss,  
let it be shut in peace.  
If any edge has been set too keen,  
let it be turned aside from harm.

Old One, receive thanks.  
Let the road be shut where it must be shut.  
Let the road be open where it must be open.  
Peace to the ring,  
peace to the dead,  
peace to the living.

So mote it be.
