## Spiritualists' National Union

In stillness, harmony, and sincerity, we open this prayer in the light of love, truth, and life.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit, source of wisdom, compassion, and eternal progress,

We acknowledge with reverence the Seven Principles:

The Fatherhood of God.  
The Brotherhood of Man.  
The Communion of Spirits and the Ministry of Angels.  
The continuous existence of the human soul.  
Personal Responsibility.  
Compensation and Retribution hereafter for all the good and evil deeds done on earth.  
Eternal progress open to every human soul.

May the mind be made clear for study.  
May understanding deepen, memory be steady, and attention be calm.  
May learning become fruitful through patience, honest effort, and right use of every opportunity.  
May examinations and academic undertakings be approached with courage, balance, and peace.

{{petition}}

May love surround every effort.  
May truth guide every choice.  
May healing, comfort, and strength be present according to the highest good.  
May all thought, word, and action be held in kindness, honesty, and personal responsibility.

Where confusion is present, may wisdom unfold.  
Where anxiety is present, may peace arise.  
Where discouragement is present, may inner strength be renewed.  
Where comparison is present, may brotherhood, fairness, and goodwill be remembered.

We do not ask to take another’s blessing, place, peace, or rightful progress.  
We ask only that each soul may advance through sincerity, service, and the light of truth.

Bless the living with patience, guidance, and hope.  
Honour all souls in the continuing life with love, remembrance, and peace.  
May all upliftment serve the greater good, never coercion, fear, or claim of certainty beyond truth.

Infinite Intelligence, Great Spirit,  
let this prayer serve only love, truth, healing, harmony, personal responsibility, and eternal progress.

Amen.
