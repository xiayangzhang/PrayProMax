## अघोर

ॐ

**गुरु-स्मरण / guru-smaraṇa**

śrī-gurubhyo namaḥ ।  
oṃ gurave namaḥ ।  
gurur brahmā gurur viṣṇuḥ gurur devo maheśvaraḥ ।  
guruḥ sākṣāt parabrahma tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ ॥

akhaṇḍamaṇḍalākāraṃ vyāptaṃ yena carācaram ।  
tatpadaṃ darśitaṃ yena tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ ॥

**शिव-शरण / śiva-śaraṇa**

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ aghorāya namaḥ ।  
oṃ bhairavāya śivāya namaḥ ।  
śivaṃ śaraṇaṃ prapadye ।  
guruṃ śaraṇaṃ prapadye ।  
aghoraṃ śaraṇaṃ prapadye ।

**मुख्य-जप / mūla-japa**

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।

oṃ namaḥ śivāya gurave ।  
saccidānanda-mūrtaye ।  
niṣprapañcāya śāntāya ।  
śrīśivāya namo namaḥ ॥

tryambakaṃ yajāmahe sugandhiṃ puṣṭivardhanam ।  
urvārukam iva bandhanān mṛtyor mukṣīya mā'mṛtāt ॥

oṃ aghorebhyo'tha ghorebhyo ghoraghoratarebhyaḥ ।  
sarvebhyaḥ sarvaśarvebhyo namaste astu rudrarūpebhyaḥ ॥

he śiva, me buddhiṃ prasādaya ।  
he aghora, me cittaṃ nirmalaṃ kuru ।  
he guru, me smṛtiṃ dhāraṇāṃ vivekaṃ ca vardhaya ।  
ajñāna-timiraṃ śamayatu,  
ālasyam avadhāna-bhaṅgaṃ ca śamayatu,  
adhyayane dhairyaṃ, vinayaṃ, ekāgratāṃ ca dehi ।

**क्षमा-प्रार्थना / kṣamā-prārthanā**

aparādhasahasrāṇi kriyante'harniśaṃ mayā ।  
dāso'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvara ॥

yad-akṣaraṃ pari-bhraṣṭaṃ mātrā-hīnaṃ ca yad bhavet ।  
tat sarvaṃ kṣamyatāṃ deva prasīda parameśvara ॥

**शान्ति / śānti**

oṃ saha nāv avatu ।  
saha nau bhunaktu ।  
saha vīryaṃ karavāvahai ।  
tejasvināv adhītam astu mā vidviṣāvahai ।  
oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ॥

asato mā sad gamaya ।  
tamaso mā jyotir gamaya ।  
mṛtyor mā amṛtaṃ gamaya ।  
oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ॥

**समर्पण / samarpaṇa**

kāyena vācā manasendriyair vā  
buddhyātmanā vā prakṛteḥ svabhāvāt ।  
karomi yad yat sakalaṃ parasmai  
śivāya tubhyaṃ samarpayāmi ॥

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ ।  
sarve santu nirāmayāḥ ।  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu ।  
mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ॥

śrī-gurubhyo namaḥ ।  
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ।  
oṃ aghorāya namaḥ ।  
oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ।
