## कबीर पंथ

Sāhib Bandagī.

Satguru Kabīr Sāhib kī jai.  
Sāhib, Satnām.  
Rām, Rām.

He Satguru Kabīr Sāhib,  
man, vachan aur kāyā ko satya, dayā aur sahaj mẽ rakhkar vinay hai.  
{{petition}}

Guru Govind dou khaṛe, kāke lāgū̃ pāy;  
balihārī guru āpane, Govind diyo batāy.

Pothī paṛh paṛh jag muā, paṇḍit bhayā na koy;  
ḍhāī ākhar prem kā, paṛhe so paṇḍit hoy.

He Satguru Sāhib,  
pustak kā abhimān nahī̃, satya-jñān kā prakāś mile.  
Jo paṛhā jāy, vah samajh mẽ āy;  
jo samjha jāy, vah dayā aur vivek mẽ phale.  
Bhram, ālas, bhay, ahaṃkār, chintā aur vikṣep satnām ke ujiyāre mẽ chhūṭe.  
Parīkṣā ke samay man sthir rahe, smaraṇ spaṣṭ rahe, vachan satya rahe, karma niṣkapaṭ rahe.

Moko kahā̃ ḍhū̃ḍhe re bande, maĩ to tere pās mẽ;  
nā tīrath mẽ, nā mūrat mẽ, nā ekānt nivās mẽ.  
Nā mandir mẽ, nā masjid mẽ, nā Kābe Kailās mẽ;  
maĩ to tere pās mẽ bande, maĩ to tere pās mẽ.

Prem galī ati sā̃karī, tā mẽ do na samāy;  
jab maĩ thā tab Hari nahī̃, ab Hari hai maĩ nāy.

Dukh mẽ sumiran sab kare, sukh mẽ kare na koy;  
jo sukh mẽ sumiran kare, to dukh kāhe ko hoy.

Kal kare so āj kar, āj kare so ab;  
pal mẽ pralaya hoyegī, bahuri karegā kab.

Sāhib, Satnām.  
Rām, Rām.

Satguru Sāhib,  
vidyā mẽ lobh nahī̃, sevā kā bhāv rahe;  
jñān mẽ garv nahī̃, namratā rahe;  
saphalatā mẽ anyāy nahī̃, sarvahit rahe.  
Satya, dayā, sahaj aur vairāgya mẽ antar-man sthir ho.  
Jāti, janm, rūp, liṅg, panth yā pad se pare, sab jīv ek Sāhib ke prakāś mẽ dekhe jāỹ.

Jo kuchh bhī śubh ho, vah sab jīv ke hit mẽ samarpit.  
Nirguṇ Sāhib ke nām mẽ, Satguru ke jñān mẽ, satya-dayā-sahaj ke mārg mẽ yah vinay pūrṇ ho.  
Satguru Kabīr Sāhib kī jai.  
Sāhib Bandagī.
