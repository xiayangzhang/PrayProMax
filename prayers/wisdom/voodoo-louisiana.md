## 路易斯安那伏都 Voodoo

Bon Dieu, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit,  
I clean my mouth of fear, confusion, boasting, and crooked speech.  
I speak this prayer for wisdom, clear mind, steady study, fair examination, and honest progress in learning.  
Let this hand be named **Clear Mind Hand**, and let its prayer be bound only to clean understanding, disciplined memory, rightful opportunity, and good use of knowledge.  
I seek no seat stolen from another, no blessing taken from another table, no success bought by harm, envy, or domination.

Pater noster, qui es in caelis,  
sanctificetur nomen tuum.  
Adveniat regnum tuum.  
Fiat voluntas tua,  
sicut in caelo et in terra.  
Panem nostrum quotidianum da nobis hodie,  
et dimitte nobis debita nostra,  
sicut et nos dimittimus debitoribus nostris.  
Et ne nos inducas in tentationem,  
sed libera nos a malo.  
Amen.

This mojo hand is sealed in clean intention only:  
for clarity without pride,  
memory without confusion,  
examination without fear,  
progress without harming another,  
and learning that walks on the right road.

Holy ancestors of blood, house, and good keeping, witness only what is clean in this prayer.  
Guard the road of study; turn away distraction, dullness, and crooked counsel.  
Let no spirit be pressed, no name be misused, no vow be made beyond rightful measure.  
What is not mine to carry, let it fall from my hand.  
What is mine to learn, let it come with humility.

Gloria Patri, et Filio, et Spiritui Sancto.  
Sicut erat in principio, et nunc, et semper,  
et in saecula saeculorum.  
Amen.

Bon Dieu, if any word has crossed its proper boundary, wash it clean.  
If any promise has been spoken without wisdom, let it be released without debt and without harm.
