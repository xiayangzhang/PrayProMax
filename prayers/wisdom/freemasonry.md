## 共济会

Most holy and glorious Lord God,  
the Great Architect of the Universe,  
the Giver of all good gifts and graces:

Thou hast promised that where two or three are gathered together in Thy Name,  
Thou wilt be in the midst of them and bless them.

{{petition}}

Grant that every thought, word, and act  
may be ordered by Wisdom,  
supported by Strength,  
and adorned with Beauty.  
Let the mind be as a well-laid stone,  
true to the line, square in intention,  
and fit for the building of a virtuous life.

הִנֵּה מַה־טּוֹב וּמַה־נָּעִים  
שֶׁבֶת אַחִים גַּם־יָחַד׃

Let Brotherly Love, Relief, and Truth  
govern our hearts toward all brethren and all mankind.  
Let no envy, vanity, unlawful gain, false witness,  
or betrayal of trust enter this labor.  
In seeking success, we do not seek to dispossess another,  
to darken another’s light,  
or to take from another’s rightful opportunity.

Direct the studies now undertaken;  
open the understanding to receive what is true,  
strengthen memory without confusion,  
steady the hand and heart in examination,  
and make diligence fruitful in due season.  
May learning become not pride, but service;  
not idle display, but moral improvement;  
not selfish advantage, but a lamp set in order.

Keep us faithful to every lawful obligation,  
silent where silence is due,  
truthful where speech is required,  
temperate in desire,  
upright in conduct,  
and charitable toward all mankind.

May the Supreme Grand Master  
preside over this intention,  
until all work is closed in peace.

So mote it be.
