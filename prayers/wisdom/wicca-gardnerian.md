## 威卡 Gardnerian

In this place, in this hour, I stand between the worlds,  
with clean hands, clear speech, and a will held in Perfect Love and Perfect Trust.

I conjure thee, O Circle of Power,  
that thou beest a boundary between the world of men and the realms of the Mighty Ones,  
a guardian and a meeting-place for wisdom, study, clear mind, good examination fortune, and steady learning.

Lady of the Moon, Great Mother, Bright One, Queen of the secret place,  
hear the words spoken within this circle.  
Shine upon the inward lamp; open the quiet door of understanding;  
let memory be ordered, thought be clean, and the heart be calm.

Whenever ye have need of anything,  
once in the month, and better it be when the moon is full,  
then shall ye assemble in some desert place,  
or in a forest all together join  
to adore the potent spirit of your queen.

Horned One, Lord of the wildwood, keeper of life and death,  
stand apart in thy own power and witness this working.  
Grant steadiness of will, discipline of hand, courage before trial,  
and the strength to learn without fear.

{{petition}}

Before the Lord I speak the boundary:  
Let this prayer harm none, bind none, and bend no will against its truth.  
Let no success be taken by theft of another’s blessing,  
no gain be won by another’s loss,  
and no fear, envy, falsehood, or ill-wrought force have power here.

Let this prayer be for wisdom rightly sought,  
harming none, binding none against the true will,  
and turning aside all confusion, panic, negligence, and self-doubt.

Eko, Eko, Azarak,  
Eko, Eko, Zomelak.

By earth, let learning take lawful root.  
By air, let thought be known in clarity.  
By fire, let diligence be quickened without harm.  
By water, let memory flow in peace.

Lady of the Moon, receive this prayer.  
Horned One, witness this prayer.  
Mighty Ones of the circle, keep it within right measure.

As the cup and the bread are blessed in fellowship,  
so may the work be whole:  
for wisdom, clear study, fair examination fortune, and steady progress,  
according to freedom, honesty, and goodwill toward all.

What is hidden, let it remain veiled;  
what is public and pure, let it bless without overreach.  
What is restless, be cleansed; what is harmful, be turned aside;  
what is rightful, be strengthened in peace.

Lady of the Moon, I thank thee.  
Horned One, I thank thee.  
Mighty Ones, I thank you.  
May this blessing go forth only as love, clarity, courage, and wisdom.

The circle is open, yet unbroken.  
Merry meet, and merry part, and merry meet again.
