## 新使徒改革

{{petition}}

Πάτερ ἡμῶν ὁ ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς·  
ἁγιασθήτω τὸ ὄνομά σου·  
ἐλθέτω ἡ βασιλεία σου·  
γενηθήτω τὸ θέλημά σου,  
ὡς ἐν οὐρανῷ καὶ ἐπὶ γῆς·  
τὸν ἄρτον ἡμῶν τὸν ἐπιούσιον δὸς ἡμῖν σήμερον·  
καὶ ἄφες ἡμῖν τὰ ὀφειλήματα ἡμῶν,  
ὡς καὶ ἡμεῖς ἀφήκαμεν τοῖς ὀφειλέταις ἡμῶν·  
καὶ μὴ εἰσενέγκῃς ἡμᾶς εἰς πειρασμόν,  
ἀλλὰ ῥῦσαι ἡμᾶς ἀπὸ τοῦ πονηροῦ.

Father, I confess and renounce every sin, pride, laziness, fear, comparison, anxiety, distraction, false identity, ungodly vow, and agreement with confusion connected with study and examinations. I place my mind, memory, diligence, schedule, and future under the blood and lordship of Jesus Christ.

I forgive all who have discouraged, pressured, misunderstood, or judged me, and I release them from my judgment. I do not curse, control, shame, or seek harm against any person. I bless what You bless, and I reject only the works of darkness, lies, bondage, sin, fear, and oppression.

Your Word says: “If any of you lacks wisdom, let him ask of God.” Father, I ask for the Spirit of wisdom and revelation, the fear of the Lord, discernment, teachability, focus, understanding, and obedience. Let truth be established in my inward parts, and let every thought be brought captive to the obedience of Christ.

הַיֹּשֵׁב בְּסֵתֶר עֶלְיוֹן  
בְּצֵל שַׁדַּי יִתְלוֹנָן׃  
אֹמַר לַיהוָה מַחְסִי וּמְצוּדָתִי  
אֱלֹהַי אֶבְטַח־בּוֹ׃

Ἰδοὺ δέδωκα ὑμῖν τὴν ἐξουσίαν  
τοῦ πατεῖν ἐπάνω ὄφεων καὶ σκορπίων,  
καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν δύναμιν τοῦ ἐχθροῦ,  
καὶ οὐδὲν ὑμᾶς οὐ μὴ ἀδικήσει.

ὃ ἐὰν δήσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται δεδεμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς,  
καὶ ὃ ἐὰν λύσῃς ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς  
ἔσται λελυμένον ἐν τοῖς οὐρανοῖς.

Ὑποτάγητε οὖν τῷ θεῷ·  
ἀντίστητε δὲ τῷ διαβόλῳ,  
καὶ φεύξεται ἀφ᾽ ὑμῶν.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I bind every spirit of confusion, fear, mental fog, procrastination, despair, accusation, comparison, distraction, and every demonic assignment that resists godly wisdom, peace, diligence, and learning. I command every unclean influence attached to anxiety, academic pressure, false words, or ungodly expectations to leave and not return, according to the authority of Jesus Christ.

In the name of Jesus Christ, I loose wisdom, clarity, understanding, memory, discernment, peace, humility, disciplined effort, righteous favor, emotional steadiness, and obedience to the will of God. I ask for success that honors You; I do not seek to dispossess others, diminish others, or gain favor through injustice, manipulation, or harm.

I cut off every ungodly soul tie, false burden, fear agreement, spoken curse, inner vow, generational pattern of failure, and demonic access point connected with ignorance, confusion, pressure, or fear of examinations. I declare that the handwriting against me is canceled in Christ, and that every hostile power is disarmed under the cross of Jesus Christ.

Καὶ αὐτοὶ ἐνίκησαν αὐτὸν  
διὰ τὸ αἷμα τοῦ ἀρνίου  
καὶ διὰ τὸν λόγον τῆς μαρτυρίας αὐτῶν.

Father, fill every cleansed place with the Holy Spirit, the peace of Christ, the fear of the Lord, wisdom, purity, courage, and steadfast love. Establish Your kingdom order in my thoughts, study habits, examination preparation, sleep, speech, and decisions.

I receive Your peace and lay down striving, panic, resentment, and all harsh judgment. Let my learning serve truth, love, justice, and the good of others. Keep my prayers free from vengeance, control, and fear; lead me in repentance, forgiveness, wise counsel, and faithful action.

I thank You, Father, and seal this prayer in the name of Jesus Christ.

Ἀμήν.
