## 印尼 / 巴厘印度教

[净身净意]  
Om awighnam astu namo siddham.  
Om śuddha-dehaḥ śuddha-cittaḥ śuddha-vākyaḥ śuddha-karmā bhavatu.  

[saṅkalpa / 发愿]  
{{petition}}  
Ida Sang Hyang Widhi Wasa sākṣī.  

[Tri Sandhya 皈敬与冥想]  
Om bhūr bhuvaḥ svaḥ  
tat savitur vareṇyaṃ  
bhargo devasya dhīmahi  
dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt  

Om Nārāyaṇa evedaṃ sarvaṃ  
yad bhūtaṃ yac ca bhavyam  
niṣkalaṅko nirañjano nirvikalpo nirākhyātaḥ  
śuddho devo eko Nārāyaṇaḥ  
na dvitīyo 'sti kaścana  

Om tvaṃ śivaḥ tvaṃ mahādevaḥ  
īśvaraḥ parameśvaraḥ  
brahmā viṣṇuś ca rudraś ca  
puruṣaḥ parikīrtitaḥ  

Om Sarasvatyai namaḥ.  
yā kundendu-tuṣāra-hāra-dhavalā  
yā śubhra-vastrāvṛtā  
yā vīṇā-vara-daṇḍa-maṇḍita-karā  
yā śveta-padmasanā  
yā brahmācyuta-śaṅkara-prabhṛtibhir  
devaiḥ sadā vanditā  
sā māṃ pātu Sarasvatī bhagavatī  
niḥśeṣa-jāḍyāpahā  

[忏悔与护念]  
Om pāpo 'haṃ pāpakarmāhaṃ  
pāpātmā pāpasambhavaḥ  
trāhi māṃ puṇḍarīkākṣa  
sarvapāpahara hare  

Om Sarasvatī prasīda,  
dhiyo naḥ śuddhāḥ bhavantu,  
manāṃsi prasannāni bhavantu,  
adhyayanam vardhatām,  
smṛtiḥ medhā ca vardhatām,  
parīkṣāsu śubha-mārgaḥ pravartatām.  

Na parabhāgya-haraṇena,  
na paravidyā-kṣayena,  
na parasukha-vighātena;  
kevalaṃ dharma-mārgeṇa  
vidyā, prajñā, śāntiś ca vardhatām.  

[kṣamāpaṇa / 请恕过失]  
Om kṣamasva māṃ mahādeva  
sarvaprāṇi hitaṅkara  
māṃ mocaya sarvapāpebhyaḥ  
pālayasva sadā śiva  

Om kṣantavyaḥ kāyiko doṣaḥ  
kṣantavyo vāciko mama  
kṣantavyo mānaso doṣaḥ  
tat pramādāt kṣamasva mām  

[śānti / 回向平息]  
Ida Sang Hyang Widhi Wasa,  
sarvavidyā-prakāśaṃ dehi,  
citta-śuddhiṃ dehi,  
dhairyaṃ dehi,  
satya-dharma-niṣṭhāṃ dehi.  

Sarvabhūta-hitaṃ bhavatu.  
Sarveṣāṃ śāntiḥ bhavatu.  
Śuddham astu, pavitram astu, kṣemaṃ astu.  
Na mama; Ida Sang Hyang Widhi Wasa arpaṇam astu.  

Om śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ Om.
