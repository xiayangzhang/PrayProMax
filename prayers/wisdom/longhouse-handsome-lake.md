## 长屋宗教（Longhouse / Handsome Lake）

Shé:kon.  
Skennen’kó:wa ken?

Ohén:ton Karihwatéhkwen.  
Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.

Nia:wen kówa ne Onkwehón:we.  
We give thanks for the People, for families, teachers, elders, and all who help the young and the learners walk with a good mind.  
Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.

Nia:wen kówa ne Yethi’nistenha Ohwéntsia.  
We give thanks to Mother Earth, who holds our feet steady, so that study may be patient, honest, and rooted.  
Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.

Nia:wen kówa ne kahnekaronnyòn:ke.  
We give thanks to the Waters, that our thoughts may be washed clear, our memory refreshed, and our speech made clean.  
Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.

Nia:wen kówa ne kontírio.  
Nia:wen kówa ne ononhkwa’shón:’a.  
Nia:wen kówa ne ohonte’shón:’a.  
We give thanks for the fish, the plants, the food plants, and the medicines, that the body may be nourished and the mind not grow dull.  
Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.

Nia:wen kówa ne otsí:nonwa.  
Nia:wen kówa ne kentsyon’shón:’a.  
Nia:wen kówa ne rotiwé:ras.  
We give thanks for the animals, the trees, and the birds, who teach attention, endurance, listening, and right timing.  
Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.

Nia:wen kówa ne karhákon:ha.  
Nia:wen kówa ne yotkaráhkwa.  
We give thanks to the Four Winds and the Thunderers, that confusion, fear, laziness, and harmful distraction may pass away without harm to any person.  
Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.

Nia:wen kówa ne iethihsótha áhsonthenhne.  
Nia:wen kówa ne otsistohkwa’shón:’a.  
We give thanks for the Sun, Grandmother Moon, and the Stars, that light may come into study, order into memory, and calm into examinations.  
Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.

Nia:wen kówa ne Shonkwaia’tíson.  
We give thanks to the Creator, and we remember the Good Message: to live truthfully, to keep a clear mind, to avoid lying, harmful speech, drunkenness, gambling, violence, and all ways that break peace.

Étho niiohtónhak ne onkwa’nikòn:ra.
