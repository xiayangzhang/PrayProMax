## 金光教

Kashikomi kashikomi mo mōsu.

Tenchi Kane no Kami-sama.

Ikigami Konko Daijin-sama.

Ikigami Konko Daijin  
Tenchi Kane no Kami  
Isshin ni negai  
Okage wa waga kokoro ni ari  
Kongetsu konnichi de tanomei.

Konnichi made no go-hiirei, go-shugo, moromoro no okage o arigataku onrei mōshiagematsuru.  
Shirazu shirazu ni okashitaru ayamachi, kokoro no itaranaki koto, mi no itaranaki koto o owabi mōshiagematsuru.

{{petition}}

Mata, kono negai wa hito no okage o ubawazu, hito no michi o samatagazu,  
jibun no tsutome o tadashiku hatashi, tenchi no megumi no naka ni manabi o fukameru tame no negai nite gozaru.

Tenchi Kane no Kami-sama.

Ikigami Konko Daijin-sama.

Waga kokoro o arawani, mi o arawani,  
hito o tasuke, hito ni tasukerare,  
tenchi no okage no naka ni,  
kokoro akaruku, shian sadamari, benkyō ni hagende, manabi no mi o musubi,  
sono toki sono ba ni fusawashiki hataraki o arawasashime tamae to,  
kashikomi kashikomi mo mōsu.

Konnichi no negai, onrei, owabi, subete o omakase mōshiagematsuru.  
Arigataku, kashikomi kashikomi mo mōsu.
