## Hoodoo / Rootwork

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.

I stand before the Most High God with clean speech and a steady heart.  
{{petition}}  
Let no falsehood, coercion, theft of blessing, or wrongful hand be in it.

גַּל־עֵינַי וְאַבִּיטָה נִפְלָאוֹת מִתּוֹרָתֶךָ׃  
הוֹרֵנִי יְהוָה דֶּרֶךְ חֻקֶּיךָ וְאֶצְּרֶנָּה עֵקֶב׃  
הֲבִינֵנִי וְאֶצְּרָה תוֹרָתֶךָ וְאֶשְׁמְרֶנָּה בְכָל־לֵב׃

Open thou mine eyes, O Lord.  
Set light in the mind, order in the memory, calm in the heart, and right judgment in the hand.  
Let lessons be understood, books be opened with profit, words be remembered in due season, and examinations be met with courage and clear thought.

I speak no harm into the innocent.  
I bind no unwilling heart.  
I seek no success by stealing another’s blessing, darkening another’s mind, or cutting short another’s road.  
Let every crooked advantage fall away, and let honest study bear honest fruit.

As my word is spoken before the Lord,  
let confusion be cut away.  
Let fear be quieted.  
Let laziness, distraction, and dullness be removed.  
Let the road of learning be opened in righteousness.  
Let the work of the mind be clean, and the feet be set on a straight path.

שִׁיר לַמַּעֲלוֹת אֶשָּׂא עֵינַי אֶל־הֶהָרִים מֵאַיִן יָבֹא עֶזְרִי׃  
עֶזְרִי מֵעִם יְהוָה עֹשֵׂה שָׁמַיִם וָאָרֶץ׃  
אַל־יִתֵּן לַמּוֹט רַגְלֶךָ אַל־יָנוּם שֹׁמְרֶךָ׃  
הִנֵּה לֹא־יָנוּם וְלֹא יִישָׁן שׁוֹמֵר יִשְׂרָאֵל׃  
יְהוָה שֹׁמְרֶךָ יְהוָה צִלְּךָ עַל־יַד יְמִינֶךָ׃  
יוֹמָם הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ לֹא־יַכֶּכָּה וְיָרֵחַ בַּלָּיְלָה׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָרְךָ מִכָּל־רָע יִשְׁמֹר אֶת־נַפְשֶׁךָ׃  
יְהוָה יִשְׁמָר־צֵאתְךָ וּבוֹאֶךָ מֵעַתָּה וְעַד־עוֹלָם׃

Set this petition under the judgment of the Most High.  
What is righteous, establish it.  
What is crooked, remove it.  
What is not mine to command, return it to God.

חָנֵּנִי אֱלֹהִים כְּחַסְדֶּךָ כְּרֹב רַחֲמֶיךָ מְחֵה פְשָׁעָי׃  
הֶרֶב כַּבְּסֵנִי מֵעֲוֹנִי וּמֵחַטָּאתִי טַהֲרֵנִי׃  
לֵב טָהוֹר בְּרָא־לִי אֱלֹהִים וְרוּחַ נָכוֹן חַדֵּשׁ בְּקִרְבִּי׃

I take back every proud demand, every crooked word, every petition not fit to stand before God.  
Wash away error, cleanse the road, and make this prayer only for wisdom, right effort, mercy, and truth.  
In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost.

Amen. Amen. Amen.
