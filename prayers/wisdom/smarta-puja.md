## स्मार्त

oṃ apavitraḥ pavitro vā sarvāvasthāṃ gato 'pi vā |  
yaḥ smaret puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ sa bāhyābhyantaraḥ śuciḥ ||

oṃ keśavāya svāhā |  
oṃ nārāyaṇāya svāhā |  
oṃ mādhavāya svāhā ||

oṃ bhūḥ | oṃ bhuvaḥ | oṃ suvaḥ | oṃ mahaḥ | oṃ janaḥ | oṃ tapaḥ | oṃ satyam |  
oṃ tat savitur vareṇyaṃ bhargo devasya dhīmahi dhiyo yo naḥ pracodayāt |  
oṃ āpo jyotī raso 'mṛtaṃ brahma bhūr bhuvaḥ suvar oṃ ||

oṃ gaṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ |  
vakratuṇḍa mahākāya sūryakoṭisamaprabha |  
nirvighnaṃ kuru me deva sarvakāryeṣu sarvadā ||

oṃ kalaśasya mukhe viṣṇuḥ kaṇṭhe rudraḥ samāśritaḥ |  
mūle tatra sthito brahmā madhye mātṛgaṇāḥ smṛtāḥ |  
kukṣau tu sāgarāḥ sarve sapta-dvīpā vasundharā |  
ṛgvedo 'tha yajurvedaḥ sāmavedo hy atharvaṇaḥ |  
aṅgaiś ca sahitāḥ sarve kalaśāmbu samāśritāḥ ||

yā kundendu-tuṣārahāra-dhavalā yā śubhra-vastrāvṛtā |  
yā vīṇā-vara-daṇḍa-maṇḍita-karā yā śveta-padmāsanā |  
yā brahmācyuta-śaṅkara-prabhṛtibhir devaiḥ sadā vanditā |  
sā māṃ pātu sarasvatī bhagavatī niḥśeṣa-jāḍyāpahā ||

oṃ śrī-sarasvatīm āvāhayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ āsanaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ pādyaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ arghyaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ ācamanīyaṃ samarpayāmi |

oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ akṣatān samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ puṣpāṇi samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ dhūpam āghrāpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ dīpaṃ darśayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ naivedyaṃ nivedayāmi |

oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ tāmbūlaṃ samarpayāmi |  
oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ nīrājanaṃ samarpayāmi ||

oṃ aiṃ sarasvatyai namaḥ ||

medhāṃ me indro dadātu medhāṃ devī sarasvatī |  
medhāṃ me aśvināv ubhāv ādhattāṃ puṣkarasrajā ||

sarasvatī namastubhyaṃ varade kāmarūpiṇi |  
vidyārambhaṃ kariṣyāmi siddhir bhavatu me sadā ||

śuklāṃ brahmavicāra-sāra-paramām ādyāṃ jagadvyāpinīm |  
vīṇā-pustaka-dhāriṇīm abhaya-dāṃ jāḍyāndhakārāpahām |  
haste sphāṭika-mālikāṃ vidadhatīṃ padmāsane saṃsthitām |  
vande tāṃ parameśvarīṃ bhagavatīṃ buddhi-pradāṃ śāradām ||

na parasya śriyaṃ harāmi na parasya vidyāṃ haniṣye |  
sarveṣāṃ maṅgalena saha mama vidyā-vṛddhir astu |  
śuddhena cittena dharmeṇa ca adhyayana-siddhir astu ||

oṃ śrī-sarasvatyai namaḥ |  
namaste astu bhagavati śrī-sarasvati |  
anena pūjā-japa-stotra-pāṭhena vidyā-buddhi-medhā-smṛti-prakāśa-citta-prasāda-parīkṣā-saukhyādhyayana-pragati-siddhir astu ||

mantra-hīnaṃ kriyā-hīnaṃ bhakti-hīnaṃ sureśvari |  
yat pūjitaṃ mayā devi paripūrṇaṃ tad astu me ||

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante 'harniśaṃ mayā |  
dāso 'yam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvari ||

yāntu devagaṇāḥ sarve pūjām ādāya māmakīm |  
iṣṭakāma-prasiddhyarthaṃ punarāgamanāya ca ||

sarve bhavantu sukhinaḥ sarve santu nirāmayāḥ |  
sarve bhadrāṇi paśyantu mā kaścid duḥkhabhāg bhavet ||

oṃ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ ||

sarvaṃ śrī-parameśvarārpaṇam astu ||
