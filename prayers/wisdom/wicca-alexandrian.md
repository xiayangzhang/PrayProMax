## 威卡 Alexandrian

I stand within the Circle, in perfect love and perfect trust.  
Let all within this rite be bounded by the Rede:  
An it harm none, do what ye will.

By Air, by Fire, by Water, by Earth,  
this Circle is cast and sealed.  
Between the worlds, and in the presence of the Mighty Ones,  
let this rite be held for the increase of wisdom, clear mind, fortunate examinations, and steady progress in study.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I do summon, stir, and call you up,  
to witness and guard this rite.

O Goddess,  
Great Mother, Lady of the Moon,  
Listen to the words of the Great Mother.  
Kindle inner wisdom, calm the wandering mind,  
and let understanding ripen in study, reading, memory, and judgment.

O God,  
Lord of the Greenwood, Horned One,  
guardian of life, death, and return,  
Strengthen discipline, courage, and right effort,  
that learning may be pursued with steadiness and honor.

Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak,  
Eko Eko Azarak,  
Eko Eko Zomelak.

{{petition}}

May success come without theft of another’s chance,  
without dimming another’s mind,  
without dispossessing any soul of rightful blessing.  
May obstacles be cleared, not by harm, but by light, order, patience, and skill.

So mote it be.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the North,  
powers of Earth,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the West,  
powers of Water,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the South,  
powers of Fire,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

Ye Lords of the Watchtowers of the East,  
powers of Air,  
I thank you for witnessing and guarding this rite.  
Hail and farewell.

The Circle is open, but unbroken.  
Merry meet, merry part, and merry meet again.

Blessed be.

All that is unsettled is now grounded.  
All excess is released into Earth, to be made clean and harmless.  
By the Rede I reaffirm: An it harm none, do what ye will.  
In clear boundary, in peaceful will, and in perfect trust, this rite is ended.
