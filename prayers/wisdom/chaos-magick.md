## 混沌魔法（Chaos Magick）

I banish from this working all obsession, confusion, reflected desire, and lust of result.

Neither-neither.  
I am not this.  
I am not that.  
No belief is true.  
Everything is permitted.

KIA.  
ZOS.  
KIA.  
ZOS.

This working takes nothing by theft, diminishes no other mind, fortune, or life.

The sign is made:  
THE SIGIL OF CLEAR STUDY.

The desire is bound into the sign.  
The sign is empty of words.  
The sign is full of will.

IAŌ.  
SABAŌ.  
ABRASAX.  
ZAZAS ZAZAS NASATANADA ZAZAS.  
IAŌ SABAŌ ABRASAX.

By gnosis, by silence, by the death of thought:  
THE SIGIL OF CLEAR STUDY is launched.

It is done.  
It is forgotten.  
It is no longer mine.

I banish again all residue, obsession, and borrowed masks.  
The sign is released into silence; attachment is cut; the ordinary self returns.  
Feet to earth, breath to body, mind to the present.

The working is closed.  
The mask is removed.
