Abun d-ba-shmayyā,  
nethqaddash shmakh;  
tite malkuthakh;  
nehwe sebyanakh,  
aykanna d-ba-shmayyā ap b-ar‘a.  
Haw lan laḥma d-sunqanan yawmana;  
washbuq lan ḥawbayn wa-khṭahayn,  
aykana d-af ḥnan shbaqn l-ḥayyabayn;  
wela ta‘lan l-nesyuna,  
ella paṣṣan min bisha.  
Meṭul dilakh hi malkutha w-ḥayla w-teshbuḥta  
l-‘ālam ‘ālmin. Āmēn.

Marya,  
‘al ḥekhmta w-yulpana d-bnay w-bnathā d-‘edtā,  
haw nuhrā d-malkuthakh, shayna d-lebbā,  
ḥnana b-buḥranē, w-tar‘ā pthīḥā l-yedi‘ta;  
w-neḥwē mazla ṭābā b-maṣ‘ayhon,  
lā b-ḥṭupya, lā b-ḥsama, lā b-nuqṣana d-aḥrane,  
ella b-kušara, b-ṭaybutha, w-b-ṣebyānā dīlakh.

Marya,  
lā nsha’al d-nesṭaleb ṭābta men aḥrane,  
w-lā d-netbaṣṣar ḥayla aw shayna d-kul bar-nasha;  
ella d-tetpataḥ urḥa d-kušara,  
d-net‘abrū men kesyānē d-yulpana,  
w-d-netnaṭrun men nesyuna, pḥama bisha,  
w-men kul šgusha d-ra‘yānā.

Lakhu Mara d-kol modēnan,  
w-lakh Yeshu Mshiḥa mshabḥīnan,  
d-ant hu mnaḥmana d-pagrayn,  
w-ant hu paroqa d-nafshātan.

Marya,  
dkar l-ṭabta l-kul d-qāymīn b-yulpana,  
l-malpanē, l-talmīdē, w-l-kul d-say‘īn l-ḥekhmta;  
‘bed raḥme ‘al aylen d-ra‘yānhon šgīsh,  
w-haw lhon shayna, sabra, w-nuhrā;  
w-qabbel ba‘utan b-ṣebyānā dīlakh,  
lā b-ṣebyānā dīlan.

Shubḥā l-Abā w-la-Brōnā w-la-Rūḥā d-Qudshā,  
min ‘ālam w-‘damā l-‘ālam. Āmēn.
