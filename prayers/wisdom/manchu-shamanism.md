## 满族萨满 / 关姓堂、博、查玛

Guwan hala-i tangse neyen.

Abka enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Abka-i jalan de,  
abka-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Na enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Na-i jalan de,  
na-i hūsun de,  
tangse-i gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mafa,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i mafa,  
boo-i dolo tehe mafa,  
hala-i dolo tehe mafa,  
gisun be donji.

Guwan hala-i mama,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i mama,  
boo-i dolo tehe mama,  
hala-i dolo tehe mama,  
gisun be donji.

Tangse-i enduri,  
donji, donji.  
Boo-i weceku,  
donji, donji.  
Guwan hala-i tangse de,  
gisun be alambi,  
hūwaliyambure mujilen be alambi.

Bi enduringge de alambi,

Neyen be jafambi,  
gisun be tucibumbi,  
enduri de alambi,  
mafa mama de alambi,  
tangse de alambi.

Abka enduri,  
sain gisun be donji.  
Na enduri,  
sain gisun be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mafa,  
sain gisun be donji.  
Guwan hala-i mama,  
sain gisun be donji.  
Tangse-i enduri,  
sain gisun be donji.

Akū oci akū seme,  
bihe oci bihe seme,  
majige be majige seme,  
ambalinggū be ambalinggū seme,  
ehe be waliyambi,  
sain be karmambi.

Beye be karmambi,  
mujilen be karmambi,  
gisun be karmambi,  
jugūn be karmambi.

Mafa mama,  
gisun be donji,  
sain be dosimbu,  
ehe be tucibu,  
faksi be waliyara,  
jobolon be waliyara.

Tangse-i enduri,  
weile be acabumbi,  
gisun be acabumbi,  
sain mujilen be acabumbi,  
sain jugūn be acabumbi.

Abka enduri de wesihun,  
na enduri de wesihun,  
Guwan hala-i mafa de wesihun,  
Guwan hala-i mama de wesihun,  
tangse-i enduri de wesihun.

Donjiha be donjiha seme,  
aliha be aliha seme,  
jifi seme jihe,  
gene seme genehe.

Enduri gene,  
mafa mama gene,  
tangse-i hūsun tangse de tehe.  
Gisun wajire,  
neyen wajire.  
Aisin gisun akū,  
hulhun gisun akū,  
yargiyan gisun be alaha.

不冒师承，不越秘传；不擅改堂内神歌、神名次第、鼓点供规。  
治病、亡者、驱祟、查玛降神，悉归传承主持。  
今惟净心告祖，敬谢天地、祖先、堂神，护正念，守清明，告退封辞。
