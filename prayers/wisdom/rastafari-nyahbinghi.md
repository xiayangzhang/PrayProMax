## 拉斯塔法里 Nyahbinghi

Jah Rastafari.  
His Imperial Majesty Emperor Haile Selassie I,  
King of Kings, Lord of Lords,  
Conquering Lion of the Tribe of Judah,  
Elect of God, Light of this world.

{{petition}}

Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly,  
nor standeth in the way of sinners,  
nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful.  
But his delight is in the law of the LORD;  
and in his law doth he meditate day and night.  
And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water,  
that bringeth forth his fruit in his season;  
his leaf also shall not wither;  
and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper.  
The ungodly are not so:  
but are like the chaff which the wind driveth away.  
Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment,  
nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.  
For the LORD knoweth the way of the righteous:  
but the way of the ungodly shall perish.

Jah Rastafari.  
Jah Rastafari.  
Jah Rastafari.

Teach I Thy ways, Jah.  
Teach I Thy truth, Jah.  
Open I and I eyes to wisdom.  
Open I and I ears to right instruction.  
Jah is I light and I salvation.  
Jah is I shepherd; I shall not want.  
Jah Rastafari, ever living, ever faithful, ever sure.

I and I ask Jah to guide every hour of study.  
Keep I and I from confusion, vanity, fear, laziness, and wicked counsel.  
Break the yoke of Babylon from ignorance, distraction, and false pride.  
Let no success sought by I and I dispossess another, shame another, or take away another’s portion.  
Let learning be used for righteousness, service, justice, mercy, and upliftment.  
Set I and I feet upon Zion way.

I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills,  
from whence cometh my help.  
My help cometh from the LORD,  
which made heaven and earth.  
He will not suffer thy foot to be moved:  
he that keepeth thee will not slumber.  
Behold, he that keepeth Israel  
shall neither slumber nor sleep.  
The LORD is thy keeper:  
the LORD is thy shade upon thy right hand.  
The sun shall not smite thee by day,  
nor the moon by night.  
The LORD shall preserve thee from all evil:  
he shall preserve thy soul.  
The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in  
from this time forth, and even for evermore.

Give thanks and praises unto the Most High Jah Rastafari.  
Let this prayer stand only in righteousness, clean livity, peace, and truth.  
Let no curse, no malice, no confusion, and no strange spirit enter I and I work.  
Let the elders’ order, unity, and reverence keep the ground clean before Jah.  
Qədamawi Ḫaylä Śəllase.  
Jah Rastafari.  
Amen.  
Selah.
