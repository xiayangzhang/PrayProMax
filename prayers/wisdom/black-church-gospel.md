## 黑人教会传统

Father God, in the name of Jesus, we come before You.

Leader:  
Lord, we thank You for waking us up, clothing us in our right mind, and keeping us by Your mercy.

People:  
Amen. Thank You, Lord.

Leader:  
We come not in our own strength, but leaning on Your everlasting arms.  
Search us, Lord; forgive us, cleanse us, and make our hearts right before You.  
Quiet every anxious thought, humble every proud place, and teach us to walk in Your truth.

People:  
Lord, have mercy.

Leader:  
{{petition}}

Scripture:  
If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God,  
that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not;  
and it shall be given him.

People:  
Amen.

Scripture:  
I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills,  
from whence cometh my help.  
My help cometh from the LORD,  
which made heaven and earth.  
He will not suffer thy foot to be moved:  
he that keepeth thee will not slumber.

People:  
Yes, Lord.

Spiritual:  
There is a balm in Gilead,  
To make the wounded whole;  
There is a balm in Gilead,  
To heal the sin-sick soul.

Leader:  
Lord, where there is confusion, give wisdom.  
Where there is fear, speak peace.  
Where there is forgetfulness, bring remembrance.  
Where there is weariness, give strength.  
Where there is distraction, give focus.  
Where there is discouragement, lift up the head.  
Where there is pride, give humility.  
Where there is pressure, give a calm and faithful spirit.

Bless the hours of reading and study.  
Bless the mind with understanding, the heart with courage, and the hands with discipline.  
Let knowledge be joined with wisdom, wisdom be joined with love, and success be joined with gratitude.  
Guide every examination, every lesson, every page, every teacher, every classroom, and every step forward.

People:  
Amen. Order our steps, Lord.

Leader:  
We put on the whole armour of God.  
We stand having our loins girt about with truth,  
and having on the breastplate of righteousness;  
our feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace;  
above all, taking the shield of faith,  
the helmet of salvation,  
and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God.

People:  
Amen.

Leader:  
For all students who are tired, strengthen them.  
For all teachers who are burdened, renew them.  
For families carrying hopes and worries, comfort them.  
For communities longing for doors to open, make a way in righteousness.  
Let learning become service, let opportunity become responsibility, and let every good gift give glory back to You.

Scripture:  
The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in  
from this time forth, and even for evermore.

People:  
Amen.

Leader:  
Now, Lord, teach us to pray:

All:  
Our Father which art in heaven,  
Hallowed be thy name.  
Thy kingdom come.  
Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.  
Give us this day our daily bread.  
And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.  
And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil:  
For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever.  
Amen.

Leader:  
Father, keep this prayer humble, faithful, and rooted in Your Word.  
Let every request rest under Your mercy and Your holy will.  
Let no fear, no pride, no bargain, and no harm have a place here.  
By the grace of the Father, by the love of the Son, and by the help of the Holy Spirit, lead us in wisdom and peace.  
We seal this prayer in faith, not by might, nor by power, but by Your Spirit.  
In Jesus’ name.

People:  
Amen. Amen. Amen.
