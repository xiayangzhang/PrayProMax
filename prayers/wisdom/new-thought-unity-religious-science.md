## 新思维 · Unity / Religious Science

God, Spirit, Divine Mind, Infinite Life, Infinite Love, Infinite Intelligence,  
there is one Presence, one Power, one Life, one Love, one Wisdom, one Substance.

I am one with this Presence.  
There is no separation between God and the living truth of my mind, my study, my learning, and my right path.  
The Christ within, the divine idea, the indwelling Spirit, is now recognized as light, clarity, order, and understanding.

{{petition}}

I deny power to fear, confusion, dullness, haste, self-doubt, distraction, and every false belief concerning learning and examination.  
They have no authority in Divine Mind, no law in Spirit, no substance in Truth.

I affirm:  
God is my help in every need;  
God does my every hunger feed;  
God walks beside me, guides my way  
Through every moment of the day.

God is my wisdom.  
Divine Intelligence thinks through me.  
Divine Order establishes my study.  
Divine Guidance leads me to the right materials, the right teachers, the right methods, and the right timing.  
Divine Peace steadies my heart.  
Divine Clarity illumines my mind.  
Divine Life renews my strength for disciplined, joyful, and effective study.

Right action unfolds in every lesson, every review, every paper, every test, and every examination.  
I receive success in ways that bless all and harm none; I do not seek to take another’s good, diminish another’s opportunity, or prevail by another’s loss.  
Whatever is not of Truth is released.  
Whatever is of Love, Wisdom, Integrity, and Right Achievement remains.

Divine light is around me.  
Divine love is within me.  
Divine power upholds me.  
Divine presence is with me, in every place.

I give thanks that this word is already known in Divine Mind.  
I give thanks for peace, clarity, right action, disciplined study, growing wisdom, favorable examination conditions, and the visible expression of academic good.

I release this word into the Law of Mind, into Spirit, into Love.  
I let it be.  
And so it is.  
Amen.
