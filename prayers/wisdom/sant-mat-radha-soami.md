Rādhāsoāmī Dayāl,  
jo kuchh hai, terā hai;  
jo phal ho, hukam meṅ ho;  
jo safaltā mile, namratā meṅ rahe;  
jo kaṭhināī āye, dhairya aur abhyās meṅ badle.

Antim ardas aur samarpan:  
Rādhāsoāmī Dayāl, hamārā simran śuddh bhakti meṅ rahe;  
gupt nām gupt raheṅ, antar kī maryādā akhaṇḍ rahe;  
man na śakti, na siddhi, na prabhutā māṅge—  
keval daya, vivek, satya, sevā aur bhakti māṅge.

Ahiṁsā, śākāhār, satya-jīvan, nasha-tyāg,  
pavitra sambandh aur Satgurū ke hukam meṅ calne kī śakti do.  
Sab jīvāṅ ko śānti mile, sab ke hit meṅ buddhi lage,  
aur surat dhīre-dhīre śabd kī or phire.
