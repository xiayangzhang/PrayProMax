## Mami Wata 信仰

Anyi guzo n’akụkụ mmiri dị ọcha.  
Mami Wata, Mammy Water, Nne mmiri, Yeye Omi,  
anyị na-akpọ naanị aha gị n’ọkpụkpọ a.

Mami Wata o, Mami Wata o,  
bia, nuru olu anyi.  
Nne mmiri, nne oma,  
mmiri dị ọcha, mmiri ndu.

Mammy Water, we hail you.  
Fine lady for water, mother for river,  
she wey sabi deep water,  
she wey hold mirror for truth,  
she wey cool hot heart.

Mami Wata o,  
maka ịmụ akwụkwọ na amamihe,  
mee ka anya ime hụ nke ọma,  
mee ka ntị nụ ezi okwu,  
mee ka uche dị jụụ,  
mee ka echiche ghara ịgba ọsọ n’ọchịchịrị.

Mmiri nye anyị anya.  
Mmiri nye anyị nti.  
Mmiri mee ka obi dị jụụ.  
Nne mmiri, kpuchie ụzọ ọmụmụ n’udo.  
Ka akwụkwọ meghee, ka nghọta bịa nwayọọ,  
ka ncheta dị ike, ka ụjọ daa n’ime mmiri.  
Ka okwu ajụjụ pụta ìhè dịka ihu n’enyo mmiri.  
Ka aka na-ede ihe ziri ezi,  
ka isi cheta ihe a mụrụ,  
ka ọmụmụ na omume ọma too.

Yeye Omi, iya omi,  
omi tutu, omi alaafia.  
Ko si ija, ko si iro.  
Alaafia ni a pe.  
No bad mouth, no hot heart, no crooked road.  
Only clear mind, honest work, and peaceful success.

Mami Wata o, Mammy Water o,  
no vex, no trouble.  
If wrong word don fall from our mouth,  
make wrong word fall into clean water and go.  
If foolish promise don rise in the heart,  
make only truth stand,  
make false bond cool,  
make fear loosen,  
make water carry confusion away.

Mmiri ọma, kuru ihe ọjọọ laa.  
Mmiri ndu, mee ka obi dị jụụ.  
Nne mmiri, nara olu udo a,  
nara mmiri dị ọcha a,  
nara okwu nsọpụrụ a.

We call no other spirit with this same mouth.  
Only Mami Wata name dey here.  
Peace for water,  
peace for road,  
peace for study,  
peace for house,  
peace for the head that learns.

Mammy Water, we thank you.  
Nne mmiri, daalu.  
Yeye Omi, mo dupe.  
Alaafia. Udo. Peace.
