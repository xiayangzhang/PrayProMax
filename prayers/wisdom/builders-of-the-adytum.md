## BOTA

In the One Life, centered in stillness, I stand in reverence before the Light.

Holy art Thou, Lord of the Universe.  
Holy art Thou, Whom Nature hath not formed.  
Holy art Thou, the Vast and the Mighty One.  
Lord of the Light and of the Darkness.

אהיה  
Ehyeh.

יה  
Yah.

יהוה אלהים  
YHVH Elohim.

{{petition}}

Let every word concerning this aspiration be ordered by Wisdom, measured by Understanding, balanced by Beauty, and grounded in righteous manifestation.  
Let this prayer bind no will, coerce no person, diminish no mind, and take no blessing, merit, opportunity, or success from another.  
Let obstacles be clarified and removed without harm, and let all attainment serve the true good of all concerned.

א  
Aleph: let the Breath of the One Life move silently through thought, making the mind lucid and awake.

ב  
Beth: let the house of study be inwardly ordered, that memory, attention, and comprehension may dwell together in peace.

ג  
Gimel: let the path between understanding and expression be made clear, so that what is learned may be rightly recalled and rightly spoken.

אהיה יה יהוה אלהים  
Ehyeh. Yah. YHVH Elohim.
